## Recovered handoff relation

A scoped finding can become a bounded result only through a local sufficiency, warrant, reliance, explanation, handoff, refusal, Unknown-preservation, lawful-stop, or completion act that states what the result is and what it is not.

The producer is the scoped finding or comparison artifact from pass 055, possibly together with preserved admission and examination artifacts from pass 054. The constitutional act is local result formation: it may warrant reliance, compose an explanation, transfer responsibility, refuse or defer action, preserve Unknown, stop inquiry within a stated territory, or declare bounded completion when the required sufficiency and authority are present. The artifact crossing the boundary is the bounded result itself: a reliance permission, explanation, handoff, refusal/non-performance record, deferred item, Unknown record, scoped stop, bounded answer, or scoped completion artifact.

The next consumer becomes eligible only to use that result within its evidence, purpose, scope, authority, confidence, Unknowns, conflicts, negative authority, tested preservation boundary, and stopping conditions.

## Authority change

The authority change is from scoped finding use to bounded result use. It is not automatic. A finding can support a result only where local sufficiency, warrant, reliance, or stop authority is established. A result may be partial: it can answer one bounded question, explain one evidence territory, refuse one requested act, hand off one responsibility, preserve one Unknown, or stop one movement without resolving neighboring inquiries.

Completion is the strongest recovered result and remains narrower than global pipeline completion. It requires the completion artifact to identify the bounded goal or question, sufficiency basis, evidence territory, authority scope, surviving Unknowns, negative authority, and what remains outside completion.

## Branches, bypasses, convergence, and stop

Movement may branch from one scoped finding into explanation, further examination, refusal, Unknown, handoff, or partial answer. It may bypass result formation where authority fails and the lawful output is no result beyond preserved finding standing. It may converge when multiple scoped findings support one explanation or one bounded stop while retaining their source-local limits. It may re-enter earlier movement when an explanation or insufficiency exposes a new possible inquiry, but re-entry requires a new identity/admission boundary and does not continue by sequence alone.

Lawful stop may occur as refusal, deferment, no warrant, no reliance, no sufficient explanation, Unknown, bounded exhaustion, outside-scope boundary, missing owner, missing authority, missing capability, conflict, or scoped completion.

## Cross-examination

### Constitutional Grammar

A bounded result is an artifact with standing, not a universal conclusion. The constitutional act must preserve the question or subject, support, scope, sufficiency basis, authority, confidence or uncertainty, Unknowns, conflicts, forbidden inferences, and stop limits.

### External Grammar

If the result explains or relies on external material, source grammar remains external unless separately translated and bounded. Explanation cannot assimilate source authority, and handoff cannot silently adopt upstream assertions.

### Fidelity

A bounded result is faithful only if it consumes prior findings within tested preservation and does not erase, invent, mutate, relocate, or expand authority. Structural compression in an explanation or handoff is lawful only while the invariants survive.

### Frontier standing

A bounded result may close, stop, or answer only the admitted inquiry movement it identifies. Pass 053 continues to govern neighboring possibilities: they remain visible, deferred, refused, Unknown, or separately admissible unless the result has broader authority and evidence.

### Responsibility

Result formation is local. The repository does not support a central inquiry pipeline owner, runtime orchestrator, universal completion auditor, or global handoff owner. Responsibility transfer requires explicit handoff evidence.

### Reliance

Reliance consumes warranted content without strengthening it. A reliance artifact permits downstream use for the bounded role and purpose only; it does not establish truth, implementation authority, mutation authority, owner assignment, or Book ratification.

### Stopping

Stopping is a lawful result when the artifact states its scope and basis. Completion is only a subset of lawful stopping. Refusal, deferment, Unknown, and bounded exhaustion are lawful pipeline results when they preserve why movement cannot lawfully continue.

## Book projection decision

No Book change is warranted. Current Book clauses already preserve bounded reliance, explicit handoff, refusal/non-performance, stopping versus completion, explanation/evidence boundaries, and Fidelity determination as bounded consumption. Pass 056 records their inquiry-pipeline topology and current frontier state rather than adding a new constitutional clause.

## Bounded pipeline result recovered

A bounded inquiry pipeline result is any local artifact that lawfully changes the standing of an admitted inquiry movement after consuming preserved prior standing and authority. The recovered result forms are: scoped answer or explanation, warranted reliance, explicit handoff, refusal/non-performance, deferment, Unknown preservation, bounded exhaustion or stop, and scoped completion. Each result must preserve identity, evidence territory, authority, scope, confidence or uncertainty, Unknowns, conflicts, negative authority, consumer limits, and neighboring-inquiry boundaries.

## Live frontier after pass 056

The inquiry pipeline frontier is not complete. It now contains three recovered movements:

1. admitted inquiry participation may become bounded examination eligibility;
2. examination output may become scoped comparison or finding standing; and
3. scoped findings may support bounded result artifacts only through local sufficiency, warrant, reliance, explanation, handoff, refusal, Unknown, stop, or completion authority.

Remaining work includes exact standing vocabulary, owner maps, runtime realization, result envelopes, re-entry topology, and complete neighboring-inquiry topology.
