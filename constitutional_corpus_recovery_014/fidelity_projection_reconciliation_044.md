## Combined grammar recovered by passes 041–043

Passes 041 through 043 collectively recover more than three adjacent safeguards. Together they recover a constitutional act of Fidelity determination.

The combined grammar is:

```text
Fidelity determination
consumes constitutional authority to be preserved
and implementation realization evidence for the changed-form boundary under review

examines whether required invariants survive
translation, handoff, distribution, narrowing, compatibility, or changed form

separates lawful implementation freedom and structural asymmetry
from erasure, invention, mutation, or relocation of authority

and produces a bounded qualitative finding:
faithful within scope / unfaithful boundary crossing / Unknown
```

The responsibility is derivative and boundary-preserving. Fidelity does not create constitutional authority, own the implementation mechanism, transfer responsibility, prove producer occurrence, recover a complete topology, or certify all implementation. It evaluates whether a specific realization boundary preserved the authority and limits already supplied by other constitutional evidence.

The evidence it consumes is two-sided:

1. constitutional authority evidence for the responsibility, warrant, scope, Unknowns, negative authority, recording boundary, mutation boundary, and consumer limits that must survive; and
2. implementation realization evidence for the translated artifact, handoff, consumer, distribution, narrowing, compatibility object, diagnostic row, rendered payload, or other changed-form mechanism under review.

The question it decides is not whether the implementation resembles the constitutional vocabulary. It decides whether the required invariants survived the realization boundary without erasure, invention, mutation, or relocation of authority.

The artifact it produces is not a runtime participant, schema, score, diagnostic, owner map, or completion declaration. It is a bounded qualitative finding tied to the reviewed evidence and boundary. Later work may consume the finding for reliance, responsibility transfer, lawful stop, implementation ownership recovery, diagnostic recording, or Book projection only within the finding's stated scope.

```text

```

That seam is appropriate because the recovered grammar concerns lawful realization and capability boundaries, not evidence in isolation, handoff in isolation, or diagnostic public surface design.

The clause preserves:

- the responsibility: a fidelity determination examines a specific realization boundary;

The Book change also adds related distinctions and failure modes so the new clause cannot be read as a runtime engine, score, public diagnostic, global certification, complete owner map, or topology claim.
