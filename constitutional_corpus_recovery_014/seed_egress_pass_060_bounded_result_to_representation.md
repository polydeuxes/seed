# Seed Egress Pass 060 — Bounded Result to Representation

Repository authority wins.

## Prior material consumed

This pass consumes inquiry pipeline pass 056, operator-ingress passes 057–059, Fidelity reconciliation 044, grammar seam pass 050, and the current Book chapters on External Grammar, evidence/provenance/explanation, communication/handoff, refusal, authority, and stopping.

## Bounded egress relation recovered

A bounded result may become externally or operator-facing only through a representation act that preserves the standing already carried by the result.

```text
bounded result
+ representation / communication / handoff preserving limits
= outward representation of that bounded result
!= stronger truth, broader authority, unrestricted reliance, action permission, receipt, understanding, or completion
```

The bounded result consumed is the pass-056 result: a scoped answer or explanation, warranted reliance, explicit handoff, refusal/non-performance, deferment, Unknown preservation, bounded exhaustion or stop, or scoped completion artifact. The constitutional act is egress representation: rendering, summarizing, quoting, reporting, citing, or communicating the result while retaining its identity, evidence territory, provenance, authority scope, confidence or uncertainty, Unknowns, conflicts, negative authority, refusal conditions, tested-preservation boundary, and neighboring-inquiry boundary.

The outward artifact receives communication or presentation standing for the bounded result. It does not acquire native truth, new evidence, new provenance, broader completion, operator authority, external authority, runtime authority, or mutation authority from the fact that it crossed outward.

## Evidence followed

Pass 056 establishes that result formation is local and that downstream consumers are eligible only within evidence, purpose, scope, authority, confidence, Unknowns, conflicts, negative authority, tested preservation, and stopping conditions. Pass 050 establishes that summary, indexing, citation, comparison, rendering, attachment, and projection are lawful only while limits travel with the material. Fidelity reconciliation 044 supplies the changed-form test: egress is faithful only when invariants survive without erasure, invention, mutation, or relocation of authority. The Book's communication chapter already distinguished communication from establishment and explanation rendering from evidence creation; this pass exposes an independently warranted egress responsibility at the same seam.

## Lawful recipient eligibility

A recipient becomes eligible to understand the representation as a bounded communication of the result and to question, compare, preserve, refuse, or locally consume it only within the carried limits. Recipient eligibility is not proof that the recipient received the artifact, understood it, relied lawfully on it, may act on it, or may reopen the completed inquiry. Those stronger standings require separate evidence and authority.

## Cross-examination

### Constitutional Grammar

The representation is an artifact with communication standing. It is not a new constitutional source of the result's truth. Formatting, compression, delivery convenience, and outward readability do not alter the result identity or create broader standing.

### External Grammar

If egress includes external-source material, source grammar remains source grammar. Translation limits, provenance Unknowns, confidence limits, and refusal conditions must remain visible or explicitly preserved.

### Fidelity

Egress is a changed-form boundary. A faithful representation preserves required invariants; an unfaithful representation erases limits, invents unsupported certainty, mutates the result into truth or action authority, or relocates authority to the renderer, formatter, report, CLI, API, operator, or recipient.

### Inquiry-result standing

The pass-056 result remains bounded. Egress may make it visible, quotable, or consumable, but cannot convert a partial stop into global completion or a scoped explanation into unrestricted answer standing.

### Communication, reliance, responsibility, and lawful stop

Communication can carry the result; reliance still requires warrant; responsibility transfer still requires explicit transfer evidence; lawful stop still remains scoped. A report can successfully represent a refusal, Unknown, or completion limit without proving that the recipient accepts it.
