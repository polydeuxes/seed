- Realization/Fidelity clauses preserve seam-specific survival review without requiring structural symmetry or creating global certification.
- Handoff and egress clauses preserve result-to-representation without strengthening result standing, and distinguish delivery from uptake.
- Refusal and stopping clauses preserve lawful non-performance, deferment, Unknown, local stop, and non-completion boundaries.

Passes 063 and 064 recover how these clauses compose, where adjacent relations join, and where preservation remains Unknown. They do not identify a missing independent constitutional act. The absence of a single circulation clause is therefore not a Book defect.
