## Reconciliation of major conflicts

- Older public answer family terrain remains valid only at public-answerability/inventory resolution. Recent bounded-question centralization is valid for explicit bounded-question artifacts and current constitutional-pipeline invocation. Neither subsumes the other.
- Typed Unknowns are implementation-local constitutional records with `unknown_type`, `area`, and `reason`; public `unknowns` compatibility fields are narrower projections and must not erase typed standing.
- question form, methodological warrant, and public answer family occupy different resolutions. A question form can inform a methodological warrant; that warrant can support survey artifacts; only exact public answer family inventory rows create public bounded ask identity.
- Inquiry Need is not an individual question. It is a need classification from repository/world uncertainty testimony and current movement context.
- Bounded inquiry frontier and examination frontier are separate. The first assembles boundary clauses for a selected need and refuses to formulate/open/authorize/execute. The second classifies candidate work for a bounded question and refuses execution.
- The historical raw constitutional-pipeline ingress road has no current standing after deletion; current pipeline contract consumes an already-established bounded question.
- Answer availability is not completion; completion is not retirement; delivery is not receipt or reliance; response is not correction or reopening.
- Circulation topology remains a graph of local relations and stops, not a single implemented circulation runtime. Current implementation realizes selected shores only.
