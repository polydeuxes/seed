# Constitutional Circulation Pass 064 — Cross-seam Fidelity

Repository authority wins.

## Recovery question

Do invariants survive across repository-evidenced circulation paths or partial paths without erasure, invention, mutation, or relocation of constitutional standing?

This pass consumes pass 063. It does not invent a universal carrier schema, score, or circulation envelope.

## Path A — Operator expression to possible inquiry

### Boundary A1: operator expression to addressable pressure

- **Originating invariants:** source attribution, provenance or provenance Unknown, expression scope, uncertainty, confidence limits, authority limits, negative authority, refusal conditions, and Unknowns.
- **Later preservation obligations:** translation and frontier consumers must retain source role and limits rather than treat the expression as Constitutional Grammar, evidence of truth, answer standing, dispatch, or mutation.
- **Lawful narrowing:** a narrower pressure scope is lawful when the narrowed scope names its boundary and does not erase provenance or authority limits.
- **Local authority added:** addressability adds examination/translation eligibility only.
- **Fidelity risk:** assimilation of prompt wording into Seed-native constitutional terms would be mutation or relocation.
- **Unknown:** exact operator-pressure record vocabulary and owner map.

### Boundary A2: addressable pressure to possible inquiry

- **Originating invariants:** translated need or uncertainty, source, provenance, scope, evidence demand, authority limit, lawful stop, exclusions, negative authority, refusal conditions, and Unknowns.
- **Later preservation obligations:** admission and frontier consumers must preserve identity as conjunctive, not wording-derived.
- **Lawful narrowing:** clarification may narrow the need or evidence territory without changing standing if exclusions and Unknowns remain visible.
- **Local authority added:** possible inquiry standing for consideration.
- **Fidelity risk:** treating restatement, presentation labels, or dispatchability as admission invents authority.
- **Unknown:** complete possible-inquiry artifact vocabulary and dispatch topology.

## Path D — Bounded result to egress to re-entry

### Boundary D1: bounded result to outward representation

- **Originating invariants:** result identity, source inquiry or subject, evidence territory, provenance, authority scope, confidence or uncertainty, Unknowns, conflicts, negative authority, refusal conditions, neighboring-inquiry boundaries, and lawful stop or completion limits.
- **Later preservation obligations:** delivery, recipient, and response consumers must not treat representation as stronger truth, broader authority, unrestricted reliance, action permission, receipt, understanding, completion, or mutation.
- **Lawful narrowing:** rendering, compression, citation, or summary may change form while preserving limits.
- **Local authority added:** presentation or communication standing.
- **Fidelity risk:** formatting away limits is erasure; treating convenience output as stronger result is mutation.
- **Unknown:** renderer/runtime participants, durable egress records, and canonical egress vocabulary.

### Boundary D2: representation to delivered availability

- **Originating invariants:** all representation and result limits.
- **Later preservation obligations:** any receipt, understanding, reliance, action, or responsibility-transfer claim requires separate evidence and authority.
- **Lawful narrowing:** availability can be recorded without proving uptake.
- **Local authority added:** delivered/available representation standing only.
- **Fidelity risk:** delivery as reliance proof invents recipient standing.
- **Unknown:** proof mechanics for receipt, understanding, reliance, action, and responsibility transfer.

### Boundary D3: operator response to new ingress pressure

- **Originating invariants:** response source attribution, provenance, relation or non-relation to prior representation, scope, uncertainty, authority limits, and Unknowns.
- **Later preservation obligations:** new ingress must preserve the prior result as prior result; response must not silently reopen, mutate, correct, supersede, complete, or relocate prior authority.
- **Lawful narrowing:** response may start clarification, neighboring inquiry, correction inquiry, refusal, or no action.
- **Local authority added:** new addressable pressure, possible inquiry, or admitted movement only if ingress translation/admission acts.
- **Fidelity risk:** response-as-reopening silently relocates reopening authority to the recipient interface.
- **Unknown:** correction, supersession, and reopening owner maps; response taxonomy.

## Cross-path findings

1. **Invariant origin is seam-local.** Source/provenance invariants originate at addressability; identity and evidence-demand invariants originate at translation and frontier formation; participation invariants originate at admission; relevance and method limits originate at examination eligibility; attribution and support limits originate at findings; result limits originate at local result formation; presentation limits originate at egress; uptake limits originate at delivery.
2. **Later seams preserve, but do not inherit authority to strengthen.** A later seam may add local authority for its own act, such as admission, examination relevance, finding standing, result standing, representation standing, or delivery standing. It may not convert the earlier standing into stronger standing absent separate evidence.
3. **Lawful narrowing is not standing mutation.** Translation, admission, examination selection, finding formation, result formation, and rendering may narrow form or scope when inherited limits remain visible and exclusions/Unknowns are preserved.
4. **Erasure and invention risks recur at every seam.** Dropping provenance, Unknowns, refusal conditions, negative authority, or neighboring boundaries is erasure. Adding unsupported owner, taxonomy, source reliability, receipt, reliance, action, correction, reopening, or completion is invention.
5. **Preservation remains Unknown where topology is unsupported.** Complete owner maps, canonical envelopes, runtime realization, proof mechanics, and universal route composition are not evidenced.

## Pass result

Fidelity across circulation is compositional and boundary-specific. There is no universal carrier schema or score. Each seam must preserve the invariants it received, may add only its local bounded authority, and must stop as refusal, lawful inactivity, deferment, partial finding, local completion, or Unknown when preservation cannot be evidenced.
