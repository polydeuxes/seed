# Book Projection Pass 029 — Correlation and Sequence Are Not Causation

## Book comparison

The Book already distinguishes evidence from fact, explanation from establishment, provenance representation from verification, bounded negative findings from stronger claims, and source grammar from constitutional grammar. It did not directly preserve the recovered cross-family causation boundary.

## Projection decision

A direct Book change was warranted because the distinction is independently supported, present-facing, and constitutional rather than implementation-mechanical. The projection does not introduce a causation model or close causation kinds.

## Book change

Added `05.Evidence.D — Correlation and sequence are not causation` to `book_of_seed/05-evidence-and-knowledge/evidence-provenance-and-explanation.md`.

The clause preserves observed association, sequence, adjacency, repeated linkage, source-attributed causal language, and model-inferred causal language as possible inquiry orientation or bounded observed-relation support, while forbidding automatic promotion into causation, explanation, conduct, responsibility, legal causation, root cause, or proof that one event produced another.

## Not projected

Causation schema, causal graph, root-cause engine, statistical-correlation engine, legal-causation engine, source scoring, authority scoring, credibility scoring, and implementation design remain report-only or unsupported.
