# Fidelity Recovery Pass 041 — Warrant Survival Through Translation

## Selected Fidelity pressure

This pass recovers the Fidelity pressure that lawful realization may translate constitutional material across carrier, vocabulary, report shape, helper object, or consumer surface only when the constitutional warrant survives the translation. A realization is faithful only if the translated form preserves enough provenance, role, authority limits, negative authority, confidence limits, and Unknowns for a later consumer to know what it may lawfully rely on.

This is narrower than restating that realization preserves authority. The pressure is the survival test for translated artifacts: realization may lawfully change form, but it may not silently change the warrant carried by the material.

## Recovered pressure

Faithful realization is distinguished from mere compatibility by whether the realized form remains consumable under the same bounded warrant. Compatibility may keep a CLI flag, JSON shape, alias, or row relation working; fidelity requires that the carrier not create stronger authority than the source material had.

The pressure can be stated as:

```text
lawful form change
!= warrant expansion
```

A faithful translation preserves:

- the source or support path sufficient for bounded reliance;
- the consumer role and scope that may use the material;
- the authority boundary that permits and limits use;
- negative authority and stop conditions;
- confidence limits and Unknowns;
- the distinction between representation, evidence, fact, projection, diagnostic visibility, and mutation.

## Cross-examination against authority and implementation evidence

`constitutional_fidelity_investigation.md` treats exact admission, inquiry-note orientation, selected-surface dispatch, post-dispatch compatibility, diagnostic aliasing, and shape audit as lawful only while they do not decide admission, recovery, completion, or knowledge by convenience. That shows compatibility is insufficient: the same mechanism can be lawful or unlawful depending on whether it preserves or expands the carried warrant.

`constitutional_action_mutation_frontier_survey.md` supplies the sharper translation rule: translation may move content across form or consumer surface only when warrant, provenance, authority limits, confidence, and Unknowns survive; translation does not create mutation rights or repository knowledge. `constitutional_access_transition_characterization.md` gives the access-transition side of the same pressure: preserved external representation may become available for bounded orientation or later inspection, but truth, fact, verification, source authority, admission, and mutation do not cross merely because the representation arrived or was preserved.

Implementation evidence supports lawful narrowing rather than expansion. Exact `QuestionFamily` lookup narrows admission to inventory-backed exact text; prepared eligibility excludes dispatch selection, presentation, rendering, surface arguments, and mutation; selected dispatch constructs compatibility handoffs to existing surfaces without owning general Recovery. Diagnostic shape audit translates declarations and observed implementation shape into rows and summaries, but those rows remain diagnostic status rather than cluster truth or universal completion authority.

## Constitutional invariants

The constitutional invariant is not a specific artifact shape. The invariant is warrant survival. When realization changes form, the following must not be lost or strengthened without separate authority:

1. provenance or support boundary;
2. bounded consumer role;
3. use scope;
4. negative authority;
5. preserved Unknowns and confidence limits;
6. local stop/refusal conditions;
7. non-mutation and non-truth boundaries for diagnostic, orientation, projection, and compatibility material.

## Lawful implementation freedom

Implementation may lawfully change carrier or shape: exact lookup result, eligibility input, dispatch request, CLI namespace update, JSON compatibility object, diagnostic row, rendered payload, or summary. It may also narrow the constitutional possibility to a smaller supported surface. These are lawful if the new form remains bounded by the original warrant and does not become a new source of authority.

## Erasure, invention, mutation, or relocation of authority

A realization erases authority when it drops a stop condition, Unknown, source limitation, or negative authority needed to bound downstream reliance. It invents authority when a compatibility object or presentation label is treated as admission, knowledge, fact, or mutation authority. It mutates authority when a translated read-only or diagnostic finding becomes cluster truth or operational permission. It relocates authority when selected surfaces that own local evidence are replaced by a central translated artifact without producer/artifact/consumer evidence.

## Bounded fidelity determination

The determination is bounded to the translated artifact or handoff under review. It does not decide all Fidelity, all translation, all implementation ownership, or a universal pipeline. It can produce a finding of:

```text
warrant survives the translation / warrant does not survive / Unknown
```

The finding is consumable by later reliance, handoff, diagnostic recording, or lawful-stop analysis to decide whether a downstream consumer may use the realized form.

## Preserved Unknowns

## Result

Pass 041 materially advances Fidelity by recovering warrant survival as the criterion that distinguishes faithful form change from merely compatible representation.
