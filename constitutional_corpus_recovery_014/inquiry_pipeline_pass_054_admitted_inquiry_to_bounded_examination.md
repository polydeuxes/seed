# Inquiry Pipeline Pass 054 — Admitted Inquiry to Bounded Examination

## Pass boundary

This pass consumes inquiry frontier passes 051 through 053 and begins the requested pipeline recovery without assuming a linear pipeline. The selected movement is the first evidenced authority change after an inquiry has admitted standing: admitted bounded participation may make examination work eligible, but only through a local examination responsibility, selection, or handoff artifact.

This pass does not design operator ingress, Seed egress, runtime implementation, a generic Book frontier sweep, a universal process owner, or a mandatory stage order.

## Recovered handoff relation

An admitted inquiry can move toward bounded examination only when a local examination boundary consumes the admitted inquiry's preserved identity bundle and authority limits and produces an examination-eligible artifact.

The producer is the local admission, selection, handoff, or examination-responsibility artifact that can show the inquiry identity, source/provenance, scope, evidence demand, present movement boundary, authority limits, Unknowns, conflicts, negative authority, and lawful stop conditions that survived admission.

The constitutional act is not examination itself. It is bounded examination eligibility: a local responsibility may treat the admitted inquiry as relevant enough to select or form a bounded probe request. The artifact crossing the boundary may be a selected examination method, probe request, handoff, or explicit lawful inactivity/refusal record, depending on the local owner. The next consumer becomes eligible to examine within that boundary, not to establish truth, select all probes, execute tools, mutate state, record findings as cluster truth, or answer the inquiry globally.

## Authority change

The authority change is from admitted inquiry participation to bounded examination eligibility. It is smaller than answer authority and smaller than execution authority. Admission does not select a method by itself; method applicability does not require method selection; probe request does not prove probe execution; probe output remains testimony or evidence until an establishment boundary acts.

If the admitted inquiry lacks explicit record material supporting the examination responsibility, subject binding, provenance or evidence requirements, and authority boundary required by that responsibility, the lawful result is inactivity, refusal, deferment, or Unknown rather than silent examination.

## Branches, bypasses, convergence, and stop

Movement may branch because one admitted inquiry can expose multiple applicable methods, each with different owners and refusal conditions. It may bypass examination where a local authority already has sufficient admitted evidence for a scoped explanation or stop. It may converge when independent examination outputs later become comparable testimony. It may stop locally when material is irrelevant, unsupported, insufficiently bound, outside authority, stale, unavailable, conflicted, unsafe, capability-missing, or already sufficient within scope.

The stop preserves the admitted inquiry's identity and neighboring possible inquiries unless the stopping artifact expressly has broader authority and evidence.

## Cross-examination

### Constitutional Grammar

Question standing and frontier identity survive into admitted inquiry movement, but they do not become examination results. The grammar permits movement only through a bounded act that states what the examination consumer may do and what it may not infer.

### External Grammar

External wording, operator urgency, provider labels, or presentation vocabulary cannot supply examination authority. If the admitted inquiry originated from translated external material, source role, provenance, grammar boundary, translated claim scope, refusal conditions, and Unknowns must survive into the examination-eligible artifact.

### Fidelity

The handoff is faithful only if the examination artifact preserves the invariants supplied by the admitted inquiry and adds no stronger claim than the local act supports. Dropping Unknowns, negative authority, excluded scope, or lawful stop conditions makes the movement unfaithful or Unknown.

### Frontier standing

Pass 051's conjunctive frontier identity remains the identity substrate. Pass 052's admission boundary supplies participation. Pass 053's neighbor-preserving stop prevents failed or refused examination eligibility from deleting nearby possible inquiry.

### Responsibility

Responsibility is local. A process description, pipeline surface, or adjacency to implementation does not identify a universal examination owner. The producer must be the artifact or owner that actually performs the local eligibility act.

### Reliance

Downstream consumers may rely on the examination-eligible artifact only for bounded examination. They may not rely on it as truth, answer standing, completion, producer occurrence, implementation readiness, or mutation authority.

### Stopping

Lawful stop at this boundary includes explicit refusal, non-performance, lawful inactivity, deferment, no applicable method, missing authority, missing capability, exhausted evidence territory, and Unknown. None is a pipeline completion declaration.

## Book projection decision

No Book change is warranted. `04.Examination.A` and `04.Examination.B` already preserve bounded relevance before movement and lawful inactivity; `08.Authority.B` preserves admission as bounded participation; `08.Handoff.A` preserves explicit transfer; and stopping/refusal clauses preserve non-performance. This pass records the topology of their handoff relation for the current inquiry pipeline.
