# Book Projection Pass 061 — Delivery Without Receipt or Reliance

Repository authority wins.

## Projection question

Does delivery or availability of an outward representation require another Book clause beyond `08.Handoff.C`?

## Decision

No Book change is warranted.

## Reason

`08.Handoff.C` expressly blocks treating delivery, acknowledgment, readability, or convenience as proof of receipt, understanding, lawful reliance, action authority, neighboring completion, or state mutation. Existing communication, evidence, observability, reliance, refusal, and stopping clauses already preserve the neighboring distinctions.

## Recorded topology

Delivery may create delivered or available representation standing for the bounded result. It may satisfy a communication condition only where the bounded goal requires delivery or availability. It does not by itself establish recipient uptake or downstream authority.

## Preserved Unknowns

Delivery channels, proof mechanics, acknowledgment semantics, recipient duties, and runtime notification implementations remain Unknown or report-only.
