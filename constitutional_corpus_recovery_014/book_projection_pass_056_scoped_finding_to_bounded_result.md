# Book Projection Pass 056 — Scoped Finding to Bounded Result

## Projection question

Does pass 056 recover an independently missing Book clause for the movement from scoped finding standing to bounded result artifacts?

## Decision

No Book change is warranted.

## Reason

The current Book already preserves the required result boundaries. `08.Authority.A` limits reliance to the preserved role, purpose, source/provenance, evidence, authority, scope, confidence or uncertainty, and Unknown boundaries. `08.Handoff.A` requires explicit responsibility transfer. `08` refusal and stopping chapters distinguish refusal, non-performance, stopping, and completion. Evidence and explanation clauses preserve support and provenance boundaries. `07.Realization.B` preserves bounded Fidelity determination and downstream consumption only within the finding's evidence, boundary, authority scope, consumer limits, and Unknowns.

Pass 056 contributes pipeline topology rather than a missing Book clause. It identifies bounded result formation as a local act that may produce scoped answer or explanation, warranted reliance, handoff, refusal, deferment, Unknown preservation, bounded exhaustion/stop, or scoped completion without declaring a universal pipeline, runtime orchestrator, or global completion.

