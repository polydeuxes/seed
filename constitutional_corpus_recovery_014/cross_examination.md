# Cross-Examination of Recovered Families

## Families examined

1. Authority, Reliance, Warrant, and Admission.
2. Evidence, Unknown, Absence, and Boundary-Claim.
3. Artifact Grammar, Kind, Standing, and Availability.
4. Questions, Inquiry, Advancement, and Bounded Work.
5. Acts, Movement, Access, Capability, and Constraint.
6. Observation, Provider Translation, and External Source.
7. Implementation Visibility, Readiness, Projection, and Diagnostic.
8. Responsibility, Governance, Ownership, and Handoff.

## Where families overlap

- **Authority/reliance overlaps evidence/Unknowns** because reliance cannot be lawful unless evidence strength, absence boundaries, Unknowns, and negative authority are preserved.
- **Artifact standing overlaps implementation visibility** because read models, diagnostics, and pipeline outputs are artifacts with bounded standing.
- **Questions/inquiry overlaps acts/movement** because a bounded question can open movement, but advancement need and selection remain distinct from authorization.
- **Observation/translation overlaps evidence** because observations become evidence only through provenance-preserving translation.
- **Responsibility/governance overlaps every family** because owner, handoff, and authority boundaries constrain all movement.

## Where families remain independent

- Evidence/Unknown boundary claims remain independent from artifacts: an artifact can be well-formed while its claim remains unsupported.
- Observation/translation remains independent from implementation: an implemented provider connector does not make provider language constitutional grammar.
- Acts/access/capability remains independent from authority/reliance: a capability surface may exist without use authority or reliance warrant.
- Questions/inquiry remains independent from Book readiness: a question can be lawful and still not mature enough for Book preservation.

## Where one family consumes another

No family wholly consumes another. The closest compression occurs in implementation diagnostics, where one diagnostic surface may expose artifact standing, question scope, evidence shape, Unknowns, capability boundaries, and responsibility. This is operational compression, not constitutional consumption.

## Where one family constrains another

- Evidence/Unknown constraints bind authority/reliance by preventing unsupported reliance.
- Authority/reliance constrains acts/movement by preventing movement from becoming mutation or implementation without warrant.
- Artifact standing constrains Book readiness by preventing report existence from becoming law.
- Observation/translation constrains evidence by requiring provenance and grammar boundaries.
- Responsibility/governance constrains implementation by requiring explicit owners and handoff evidence.
- Implementation visibility constrains operational changes by requiring inventory and shape-audit witnesses.

## Where reports disagree or refine each other

- Readiness reports sometimes sound close to implementation authorization; implementation-pressure and readiness audits refine this by requiring owner, shape, and tests.
- Recurrence surveys can suggest maturity; repetition-proof and ratification-readiness reports refine recurrence into pressure, not proof.
- Access and capability reports can sound operational; capability-use and action/mutation reports refine them into availability without use authority.
- Admission reports can sound universal; artifact and question reports preserve local owner-specific gates.

## Where implementation compresses distinctions

Implementation compresses distinctions in read models, diagnostics, pipeline surfaces, provider contracts, and capability catalogs. The same command or output may simultaneously be a view, artifact, evidence surface, question response, diagnostic record, and operational witness. The repository response is not to collapse the distinctions, but to require shape, inventory, scope, record boundary, and mutation status.

## Missing relationships

- Complete owner map across all constitutional responsibilities.
- Exhaustive act and access-transition inventory.
- Exact Book-readiness threshold for each recurring family.
- Stable taxonomy for Unknown categories.
- Complete provider/source taxonomy and translation ownership.
- Whether governance remains distributed or gains specific implementation surfaces.
- Whether action/mutation vocabulary matures beyond frontier survey.

## Cross-examination result

The wider corpus recovers several independent constitutional pressure families. Their strongest shared rule is conservative: no evidence form, report form, implementation witness, external observation, operator request, or recurring phrase may cross into authority, truth, mutation, ownership, Book law, or universal topology without bounded support and preserved negative authority.
