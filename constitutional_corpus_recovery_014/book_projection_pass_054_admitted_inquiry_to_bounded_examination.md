# Book Projection Pass 054 — Admitted Inquiry to Bounded Examination

## Projection question

Does pass 054 recover an independently missing Book clause for the movement from admitted inquiry to bounded examination eligibility?

## Decision

No Book change is warranted.

## Reason

The current Book already preserves the required consequence. `04.Examination.A` states that bounded examination responsibility may treat recorded material as relevant only when explicit record material supports the local responsibility, subject binding, evidence or provenance requirements, and authority boundary, and that the result is permission to examine further inside that boundary. `04.Examination.B` preserves lawful inactivity when material is irrelevant, unsupported, insufficiently bound, outside authority, or Unknown. `08.Authority.B` preserves admission as bounded participation rather than ratification, and `08.Handoff.A` preserves explicit responsibility transfer only within surviving limits.

Pass 054 contributes topology: the admitted inquiry's identity bundle may be consumed by a local examination boundary to produce examination eligibility, probe-request standing, or lawful inactivity/refusal. It does not require a new Book clause because the Book already forbids treating admission, relevance, handoff, or probe formation as truth, execution, answer standing, mutation, or completion.
