# Constitutional Corpus Recovery 014 — Inventory

## Boundary and method

This inventory records relevant constitutional corpus artifacts outside `book_of_seed/`. It does not use the Book as evidence, does not search for a missing Book clause, and does not modify the Book. Content, not filenames alone, determined grouping: artifacts were admitted when they examined bounded constitutional questions, preserved distinctions, stated negative authority, recorded Unknowns, or tied recurring grammar to implementation witnesses.
