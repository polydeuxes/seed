# Book Projection Pass 055 — Examination Output to Comparison or Finding Standing

## Projection question

Does pass 055 recover an independently missing Book clause for the movement from examination output into comparison or finding standing?

## Decision

No Book change is warranted.

## Reason

The current Book already contains the operative rule in `04.Examination.C`: cross-examination may compare independently preserved testimony or findings only while preserving attribution, provenance, support basis, subject, scope, authority, confidence or uncertainty, Unknowns, standing, and forbidden inferences. It also states that the result is bounded comparison standing and forbids truth establishment, warrant, reliance, source independence, implementation permission, or reconciliation merely because material agrees, contradicts, repeats, or can be compared.

Pass 055 adds pipeline topology rather than a missing Book rule. It identifies the producer as bounded examination output, the constitutional act as bounded evidentiary characterization or cross-examination, the crossing artifact as scoped finding/comparison/conflict/insufficiency/Unknown standing, and the next consumer's eligibility as limited use for sufficiency, warrant, explanation, reliance, refusal, deferment, or further examination.

## Current-state update

The live inquiry pipeline now distinguishes:

```text
bounded examination eligibility
!=
examination output
!=
comparison or finding standing
!=
truth, warrant, reliance, or completion
```

Partial findings, comparison owner maps, runtime realization, and mandatory comparison requirements remain Unknown.
