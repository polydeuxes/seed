# Book Projection Pass 031 — Analytical Competence Is Not Adjudicative Authority

## Book comparison

The Book already distinguishes authority scope, bounded reliance, admission from ratification, refusal, non-performance, stopping, evidence from fact, and comparison pressure from adjudicative authority. It did not directly preserve the wider legal/adjudicative non-promotion boundary for analytical competence.

## Projection decision

A direct Book change was warranted because the distinction is independently supported, present-facing, and constitutional rather than a legal-engine design. The projection preserves a boundary on authority expansion without creating legal ontology or adjudicative machinery.

## Book change

Added `08.Authority.C — Analytical competence is not adjudicative authority` to `book_of_seed/08-authority-communication-and-stopping/authority-scope.md`.

The clause preserves Seed's ability to analyze source packets, support paths, source roles, boundary mismatches, pressure, confidence limits, temporal limits, and Unknowns under bounded repository authority while forbidding promotion into jurisdiction, legal authority, binding legal conclusion, validity determination, invalidity determination, enforceability determination, or institutional adjudication.

## Not projected

Legal research engine, adjudication engine, legal-validity engine, constitutional-validity engine, precedent engine, source-of-law graph, legal advice system, full legal-source taxonomy, and implementation design remain report-only or unsupported.
