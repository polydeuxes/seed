# Fidelity Topology Pass 046 — Applicability Derivation from Survival Demands

Repository authority wins.

## Prior evidence consumed

This pass consumes pass 045 as evidence. Pass 045 recovered that Fidelity identifies its review boundary only by pairing bounded constitutional authority with concrete implementation realization evidence. This pass asks how the applicable authority and invariants are bound to that selected boundary strongly enough to warrant a scoped finding.

## Selected topology pressure

Once a realization boundary is selected, Fidelity still must not list every protected constitutional boundary as applicable. The unresolved topology pressure is how Fidelity determines which authority and invariants must survive at this boundary.

The recovered relation is:

```text
applicable Fidelity authority
is the subset of established constitutional authority
whose warrant, limits, Unknowns, stop conditions, recording boundary, mutation boundary, or consumer limits
are placed at risk or carried by the selected realization seam
```

Applicability is therefore derived from survival demand, not from vocabulary membership.

## Evidence followed

`constitutional_fidelity_characterization.md` states that Fidelity protects constitutional authorities but does not own them. It lists recurring boundaries that must be preserved, but also says implementation freedom remains lawful when it preserves relevant constitutional boundaries. The word `relevant` is decisive: not every protected boundary is active in every Fidelity determination.

`fidelity_projection_reconciliation_044.md` says the determination consumes the constitutional authority to be preserved and asks whether required invariants survived the changed-form mechanism. It does not authorize a universal checklist detached from the boundary.

`07.Realization.B` preserves the same bounded grammar: required invariants are evaluated for the mechanism under review, and later consumption is limited by evidence, boundary, authority scope, consumer limits, and Unknowns.

Pass 041 supplies the first survival-demand example: when the seam is translation, the applicable invariants include warrant, provenance, role, scope, negative authority, confidence, Unknowns, and non-mutation limits. Pass 042 supplies the handoff example: when the seam is boundary-crossing consumer eligibility, applicable invariants include producer boundary, consumer boundary, role, explicit transfer evidence, and non-relocation of authority. Pass 043 supplies the finding example: when the seam is the Fidelity finding itself, applicable invariants include reviewed evidence, boundary, surface, time, Unknowns, and consumer limits.

## Recovered Fidelity relation

Fidelity binds authority to a selected boundary by deriving applicability from what the seam carries or endangers. The applicable authority is neither the whole constitutional corpus nor merely the implementation object's declared purpose. It is the bounded set of established authority conditions that must survive for the particular realization to remain lawful.

This relation has three parts:

1. **Source authority** — completed constitutional evidence establishes the authority and limits that could be applicable.
2. **Seam exposure** — implementation realization evidence shows which parts of that authority are carried, compressed, transformed, hidden, handed off, rendered, recorded, or consumed at this boundary.
3. **Survival demand** — only the authority conditions exposed at the seam become required invariants for the Fidelity finding.

## Cross-examination

### Authority

Applicability remains derivative. Fidelity may not add new invariants because they would make a cleaner checklist, and it may not drop established invariants because implementation lacks a convenient field. Authority comes from constitutional evidence; applicability comes from the authority's exposure at the selected seam.

### Evidence

The evidence must show both that an invariant exists and that the selected seam carries or threatens it. A general statement that Unknowns, mutation boundaries, or handoff limits are always important is not enough to prove their concrete applicability unless the seam's realization evidence places them in play.

### Responsibility

Fidelity does not become the responsibility owner by deriving applicable invariants. It performs a scoped preservation test. The upstream responsibility continues to own its authority; the implementation component continues to own its mechanics where evidence supports that ownership.

### Handoff

When the seam is a handoff, applicability includes only the authority needed for the consumer's lawful role and any explicit responsibility transfer at issue. It does not automatically include every upstream responsibility or every downstream use.

### Reliance

A downstream consumer of the Fidelity finding may rely only on the invariants actually tested. Untested constitutional boundaries remain untested, not silently satisfied.

### Lawful stop

The determination stops when the survival-demand set is supported enough for the scoped finding. If evidence cannot show which invariants the seam carries or threatens, Fidelity must stop with Unknown rather than apply a universal inventory.

## Preservation, crossing, and Unknown

Supported preservation exists when evidence shows that each applicable invariant survived the selected seam within its authority scope. Boundary crossing exists when an applicable invariant is erased, invented, mutated, or relocated by the seam. Unknown is required when invariant existence, seam exposure, or the survival connection is not evidenced.

## Book projection decision

No Book change is warranted in this pass. `07.Realization.B` already refers to required invariants and limits later consumption by authority scope and Unknowns. Pass 046 clarifies how to derive those required invariants for current recovery work, but it does not independently warrant a new Book clause.

## Result

Pass 046 recovers the second topology relation: after a review boundary is selected, applicable constitutional authority is bound to that boundary through survival demands evidenced at the realization seam. Unsupported applicability remains Unknown.
