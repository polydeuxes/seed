# Book Projection Pass 038 — Bounded Competency Declaration Boundary

## Pressure examined

Bounded competency declaration as a possible general Book grammar for local responsibility, subject, authority, evidence/provenance requirements, and stop conditions.

## Projection decision

No Book change.

## Reason

The current Book already preserves the constitutional consequences supported by the evidence: bounded relevance before movement, lawful inactivity, authority non-expansion, governance non-universality, analytical competence without adjudicative authority, and kind labels without ontology closure. The remaining pressure is the name and topology of a general competency declaration. Current witnesses are fixture-local or report-local and repeatedly deny registry, dispatch, actor, Eye, organism, and universal activity readings.

## Frontier update

Future work may revisit bounded competency only if independent evidence shows a present-facing constitutional consequence not already supplied by examination, authority, governance, and kind-label clauses. A table row, enum, label, or local witness shape remains insufficient by itself.
