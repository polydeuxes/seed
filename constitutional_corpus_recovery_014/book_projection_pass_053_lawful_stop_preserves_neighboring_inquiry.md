# Book Projection Pass 053 — Lawful Stop Preserves Neighboring Inquiry

## Projection question

Does lawful stop preserving neighboring inquiry require a new Book clause after passes 051 and 052?

## Decision

No Book change is warranted.

## Warrant

The Book already preserves stopping and completion boundaries, refusal and non-performance, unresolved material, bounded negative findings and Unknowns, question standing, bounded translation for inquiry origination, frontier identity, and admission limits. Together these clauses block a scoped stop, refusal, exhaustion, or answer from erasing neighboring inquiry or becoming global completion.

## Remaining frontier

Exact standing vocabulary for unresolved, deferred, refused, answered, exhausted, and stopped inquiry; retirement/deletion/reopening authority; multi-frontier topology; and implementation realization remain report-only or Unknown. This pass does not declare the inquiry frontier complete.
