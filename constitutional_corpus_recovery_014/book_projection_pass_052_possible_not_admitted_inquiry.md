# Book Projection Pass 052 — Possible Inquiry Is Not Admitted Inquiry

## Projection question

Does the distinction between identity-preserved possible inquiry and admitted inquiry movement require another Book clause?

## Decision

No Book change is warranted.

## Warrant

Current Book coverage already composes the result. `04.Question.A` and `04.Question.B` prevent question posture and translated inquiry from becoming answer, outcome, unbounded investigation, or operator-wording knowledge. `04.Frontier.A` now blocks identity inference and movement from frontier identity. `08.Authority.B` preserves admission as bounded participation rather than ratification, truth, implementation authority, mutation authority, or reusable approval. Advancement and selection clauses preserve need, consideration, selection, and authorization as separate standings.

## Remaining frontier

Exact inquiry-admission artifact forms, owner-local authority changes, retirement/deletion authority, and runtime realization remain report-only or Unknown. The next inquiry frontier pass should examine lawful stop and neighbor preservation rather than repeat the possible/admitted distinction.
