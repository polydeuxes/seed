## Projection question

Does the end-to-end standing topology recovered in pass 063 warrant a new Book clause?

## Decision

No Book change is warranted.

## Reason

Pass 063 recovers a compositional relation graph rather than a missing independent constitutional act. The current Book already preserves the governing boundaries through External Grammar addressability and cross-seam consumption, inquiry origination and frontier identity, admission as bounded participation, bounded examination and comparison, Fidelity determination, egress representation, delivery without uptake, refusal, stopping, and handoff limits.

## Preserved consequence

Future work must compose the existing clauses rather than infer a happy path. A relation may consume a previous relation only within the previous standing and limits, and a missing handoff remains Unknown. Delivery is not uptake, response is not reopening, and representation is not stronger result standing.
