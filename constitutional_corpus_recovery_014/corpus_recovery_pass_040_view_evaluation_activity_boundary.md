# Corpus Recovery Pass 040 — View Evaluation and Activity Boundary

## Selected constitutional pressure

This pass selected the pressure that calculated views, query pairs, diagnostic/audit rows, or representation lenses may be mistaken for observation, notification, awakening, examination occurrence, consumer uptake, broadcast, or universal activity.

## Evidence recovered

`lenses-views-and-roads.md` preserves that a lens exposes, selects, projects, or composes bounded representation without changing the standing of the represented material, and that mechanical compatibility or call adjacency does not establish uptake or a constitutional road. `sqlite_constitutional_witness_slice_001.md` uses `competency_activity_summary` to show recorded changes do not awaken all competencies. `eye_competency_composition_locality_characterization_009.md` states that the `CROSS JOIN` in `competency_change_examination` computes every pair for deterministic analysis but does not mean every competency receives, observes, examines, or is notified of every change. `sqlite_seed_grammar_friction_characterization_010.md` states that view existence and query evaluation are not occurrence or consumer uptake and that a view-calculated competency/change pair is not broadcast, notification, observation, awakening, examination occurrence, or uptake.

## Implementation witnesses

SQLite views and summary queries concretely witness deterministic representation and bounded calculation. Diagnostic and audit surfaces can expose pressure or shape, but they do not by availability alone record occurrence, mutate cluster truth, notify consumers, or create universal activity. The witness is operationally concrete but constitutionally bounded.

## Cross-examination

- Against pass 017 and pass 025: diagnostic visibility and shape-audit mechanics expose surfaces only within declared scope; inventory or audit presence is not authority or complete operational coverage.
- Against pass 019: access or visibility is not capability or use authority.
- Against pass 028: calculated movement or representation is not mutation.
- Against current lenses/views/roads: the Book already blocks treating a lens as a road or uptake merely because it is compatible, adjacent, or visible.

## Constitutional consequence test

The pressure affects what may be relied upon and consumed: a calculated row or view result may be used as bounded representation under its declared method, but not as proof of notification, observation, examination occurrence, consumer uptake, broadcast, or universal activity. Existing Book clauses preserve the core consequence for lenses, uptake, access/use, diagnostic visibility, and movement/mutation. The remaining details are operational mechanics and view topology.

## Determination

No Book change is directly warranted. The frontier is updated to keep view-evaluation/activity topology report-only unless future evidence shows a new present-facing constitutional consequence not composed from existing clauses.
