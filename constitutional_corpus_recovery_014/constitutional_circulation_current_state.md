## Current circulation grammar

Seed currently supports a compositional circulation of standing through bounded relations, not a mandatory linear pipeline.

1. **External or operator expression may become addressable pressure.** The crossing preserves source role or Unknown, provenance or Unknown, scope, uncertainty, confidence and authority limits, negative authority, refusal conditions, and Unknowns. It does not create Constitutional Grammar, repository authority, cluster truth, answer standing, runtime dispatch, or mutation.
2. **Addressable pressure may become translated need or possible inquiry.** Bounded translation preserves need or uncertainty, source, provenance, scope, evidence demand, authority limits, lawful stop, exclusions, negative authority, refusal conditions, and Unknowns. Restatement, clarification, or dispatchability is not admission.
3. **Possible inquiry may become admitted movement.** Local admission authority can add bounded participation or movement standing while preserving the identity bundle. Admission is not answer standing, execution, ownership, mutation, completion, or neighboring-inquiry retirement.
4. **Admitted movement may become examination eligibility.** A local examination responsibility may establish relevance, applicability, or positive lawful inactivity. Eligibility is permission to examine further within the boundary, not selection, execution, truth, or action.
5. **Examination output may become scoped finding standing.** Comparison or finding formation preserves attribution, provenance, support basis, subject, scope, authority, confidence or uncertainty, standing, forbidden inferences, and Unknowns. Agreement, contradiction, repetition, or comparability does not establish truth, warrant, source independence, reliance, reconciliation, implementation permission, or completion.
6. **Scoped findings may become bounded results.** Local sufficiency, refusal, deferment, explanation, handoff, lawful stop, or scoped completion can produce bounded result standing. The result remains limited by source inquiry or subject, evidence territory, provenance, authority scope, confidence or uncertainty, Unknowns, conflicts, negative authority, refusal conditions, neighboring boundaries, and stop/completion limits.
7. **Bounded results may become outward representations.** Representation, communication, rendering, compression, citation, report inclusion, API/CLI emission, or handoff adds presentation or communication standing only while preserving result limits. It does not strengthen truth, authority, reliance, action permission, receipt, understanding, completion, or state mutation.
8. **Outward representation may become delivered or available representation.** Delivery or availability does not prove receipt, understanding, reliance, action authority, responsibility transfer, or completion.
9. **Operator response may re-enter only as new pressure.** A response may become source-attributed pressure, possible inquiry, or admitted movement only through ingress translation and local admission. It does not silently reopen, mutate, correct, supersede, complete, or relocate authority over the prior result.

## Lawful branches and bypasses

The circulation permits clarification, refusal, deferment, partial findings, lawful inactivity, Unknown, local completion, neighboring inquiry, and no further movement. Any relation may stop where authority, source, provenance, scope, evidence demand, preservation, sufficiency, or local responsibility is unsupported. A pressure may never become inquiry; an inquiry may remain possible; admitted movement may select no examination; examination may produce Unknown; a result may be partial or negative; a representation may never be taken up; a response may start a neighboring inquiry rather than reopen the prior result.

## Standing changes

Standing changes are local and act-bound:

- addressability adds source-attributed examination or translation eligibility;
- translation adds limited possible-inquiry or claim standing;
- admission adds bounded participation or movement standing;
- examination eligibility adds permission to examine further;
- finding formation adds scoped comparison or finding standing;
- result formation adds bounded result standing;
- egress adds presentation or communication standing;
- delivery adds delivered/available representation standing;
- re-entry starts new pressure only if ingress boundaries are satisfied.

No standing change silently carries global truth, universal owner authority, execution authority, reliance authority, action authority, correction authority, reopening authority, or completion authority.

## Authority changes

Each seam may add only the authority local to its constitutional act. Later consumers may consume earlier standing only within preserved limits. Responsibility transfer, correction, supersession, reopening, receipt, understanding, reliance, action, mutation, and neighboring-inquiry completion require separate evidence and authority.

## Fidelity obligations

Fidelity obligations are seam-specific:

- preserve source/provenance and source grammar limits at addressability and translation;
- preserve conjunctive inquiry identity, evidence demand, exclusions, negative authority, stop conditions, and Unknowns across possible inquiry and admission;
- preserve admission limits when deriving examination eligibility;
- preserve attribution, support basis, confidence, standing, and forbidden inferences across comparison and finding formation;
- preserve result identity, evidence territory, authority, confidence, Unknowns, refusal conditions, neighboring boundaries, and stop/completion limits across egress;
- preserve delivery as availability only absent separate uptake proof;
- preserve response as new ingress pressure rather than silent reopening.

Erasure occurs when inherited limits disappear. Invention occurs when unsupported owners, taxonomies, reliability, receipt, reliance, action, correction, reopening, supersession, or completion are added. Mutation occurs when a lower or different standing is treated as stronger Seed truth or operational state. Relocation occurs when authority is moved from its bounded constitutional warrant to a source, carrier, interface, formatter, report, delivery surface, or recipient response.

## Refusal, Unknown, and stop boundaries

Refusal, deferment, lawful inactivity, partial result, local completion, and Unknown are first-class circulation outcomes. They preserve bounded standing and do not erase neighboring pressures. Missing preservation evidence, missing local authority, unsupported source/provenance, unsupported translation, conflicts, insufficiency, stale or unavailable material, policy block, non-selected work, and absent uptake proof all support lawful stop rather than silent strengthening.

## Re-entry without silent reopening

An operator response after egress is new source-attributed pressure. It may refer to a prior result, but reference does not reopen, mutate, correct, supersede, complete, or relocate the prior result. Reopening or correction remains unsupported unless a separate authority boundary establishes it.

## Unsupported topology

The following remain unsupported or Unknown: universal circulation owner; canonical circulation envelope; canonical ingress, inquiry, result, egress, or response envelopes; runtime orchestrator; complete owner maps; complete handoff inventories; complete branch topology; complete neighboring-inquiry topology; proof mechanics for receipt, understanding, reliance, action, correction, supersession, reopening, responsibility transfer, and completion; complete source/provider taxonomy; and any claim that the Book, corpus, or circulation is complete.
