# Book Projection Pass 026 — Question Standing Is Not Answer Standing

## Book comparison

The current Book already preserves several parts of this pressure: question-shaped text is not automatically constitutional inquiry; frontier membership is not selection or execution; and need standing alone does not authorize movement. However, the Book did not state the mature recovered distinction as an addressable boundary: a bounded question may establish inquiry posture without establishing an answer or converting operator wording into repository knowledge.

## Projection decision

A direct Book change was warranted because the distinction is independently recurring, cross-family supported, and not merely implementation topology or exhaustive inventory.

## Book change

Added `04.Question.A — Question standing is not answer standing` to `book_of_seed/04-inquiry-and-examination/questions-and-inquiry.md`.

The clause preserves that bounded questions may establish identity, provenance, scope, evidence demand, and lawful stop conditions, while not establishing the answer, selecting the outcome, authorizing unbounded investigation, or promoting operator wording into repository knowledge.

## Not projected

Exact question-form kinds, complete inquiry ownership, frontier implementation mechanics, and question surface inventories remain report-only.
