# Book Projection Pass 030 — Attribution Is Not Responsibility

## Book comparison

The Book already distinguishes provenance representation from verification, evidence from fact, explanation from establishment, explicit responsibility transfer from implicit handoff, and governance coordination from universal adjudication. It did not directly preserve the narrower evidence/provenance boundary that attribution is not responsibility.

## Projection decision

A direct Book change was warranted because the distinction is independently supported across evidence, source, diagnostic, responsibility, and causation witnesses. The projection preserves a constitutional non-promotion boundary without adding a responsibility schema or owner map.

## Book change

Added `05.Evidence.E — Attribution is not responsibility` to `book_of_seed/05-evidence-and-knowledge/evidence-provenance-and-explanation.md`.

The clause preserves attribution, association, attachment, ownership candidates, source-reported blame, account/device/address/role/deployment relations, and event associations as provenance or inquiry pressure only within their claim form. It forbids automatic promotion into responsibility, accountability, fault, blame, intent, negligence, liability, legal responsibility, moral responsibility, implementation ownership, or implementation pressure.

## Not projected

General responsibility schema, liability model, blame engine, security-attribution engine, identity-resolution engine, complete owner map, diagnostic owner-candidate mechanics, source scoring, authority scoring, credibility scoring, root-cause engine, and implementation design remain report-only or unsupported.
