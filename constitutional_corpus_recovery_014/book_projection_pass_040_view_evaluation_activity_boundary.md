# Book Projection Pass 040 — View Evaluation and Activity Boundary

## Pressure examined

Whether calculated views, query pairs, diagnostic/audit rows, or representation lenses require a separate Book clause denying observation, notification, awakening, examination occurrence, consumer uptake, broadcast, or universal activity.

## Projection decision

No Book change.

## Reason

The existing Book already preserves the consequence: lenses and views do not become roads or uptake by compatibility or adjacency; access and visibility do not authorize use; diagnostic visibility remains scoped; movement does not equal mutation; and recording or occurrence requires its own boundary. Current evidence supports bounded representation and deterministic calculation, not a new general activity topology.

## Frontier update

Future passes should not promote view, query, inventory, audit, or diagnostic-row availability into activity, notification, occurrence, or uptake without separate occurrence and consumer-reliance evidence. The unresolved material is topology and implementation mechanics, not a missing Book clause.
