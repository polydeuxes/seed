## Topology reconciliation

Only after the independent surveys above, the recurring topology visible around the apparent Frame neighborhood is this:

```text
External/source-side condition
  -- observation / provider-translation bridge -->
Observation / Evidence
  -- warrant / admissibility / lawful-transition bridge -->
Constitutional representation
  -- event-history / projection bridge -->
Projected or read-model view
  -- presentation / narrative / orientation bridge -->
Operator-facing or investigation-facing framing

Unknown preservation and repository authority border every movement.
```

This is not a Frame type system. It is a recurrent constitutional neighborhood of positions and bridges that makes apparent Frame-like pressure visible without recovering Frames.

### Recovered neighboring positions

The recurring neighboring positions with support are:

1. **External/source-side condition** as source material outside Seed representation.
2. **Observation** as bounded source-attributed report or acquisition.
3. **Evidence** as preserved scoped support material.
4. **Warrant / admissibility / reliance** as lawful support for bounded downstream use.
5. **Constitutional representation / current lawful condition** as evidence-bound represented state and lawful movement status.
6. **Event history / projection / read model** as derived representation and consumer-facing state construction.
7. **Presentation / orientation / narrative methodology** as rendered, investigative, or communicative surfaces that may expose relationships without owning them.
8. **Unknown preservation** as the continuing boundary for unsupported, incomplete, or unresolved claims.
9. **Repository authority** as the governing boundary over all claims.

### Recovered bridges

Recurring bridges are supported only in these bounded forms:

- Source/provider output to observation through observation intake or provider translation.
- Observation to evidence through preservation with source, provenance, and claim limits.
- Evidence to warrant through admissibility, sufficiency, authority boundary, confidence, and negative authority.
- Warrant to lawful transition through bounded local movement or lawful stop.
- Event history to projection through replay and derived-state construction.
- Projection/read model to presentation through report, formatter, explanation, diagnostic, or answer-composition surfaces.
- Narrative/orientation to evidence testing through translation into falsifiable relationships.
- Unsupported candidate to preserved unknown through explicit refusal to promote.

### Recovered streets

The recurring streets are corridors of movement, not ownership recoveries:

1. **Source-to-support street:** external/source-side material can move toward observation and evidence without becoming truth.
2. **Support-to-warrant street:** evidence can support bounded warrant or refusal, depending on authority and sufficiency.
3. **History-to-view street:** recorded events can be projected into current or read-model views without the view owning history.
4. **View-to-presentation street:** projected/read-model material can be rendered or explained without presentation becoming knowledge.
5. **Orientation-to-investigation street:** candidate vocabulary can guide inquiry, be falsified, or become preserved unknown without being promoted.
6. **Translation street:** vocabulary, grammar, story, or provider language can be translated into bounded repository evidence only where warrant survives translation.

### Falsification attempts

- **If Frame were already recovered,** app reachability or repository evidence should show stable knowledge/implementation support. The app check did not recover Frame knowledge candidates, and reviewed documents warn against vocabulary promotion.
- **If Simulation were a supported neighbor,** projection or narrative evidence should authorize a simulation framework. Reviewed evidence instead limits projection to derived state and narrative to methodology/presentation.
- **If Presentation were knowledge,** labels would promote themselves. Repository instructions and reachability audits explicitly reject that.
- **If External Reality collapsed into Constitutional Reality,** observations/provider reports would be facts or truth by existence. Reviewed evidence repeatedly rejects this.
- **If Implementation owned the apparent neighborhood,** implementation evidence would name or require Frame behavior. Reviewed evidence supports implementation-local diagnostics/projection/observation boundaries, not Frames.
- **If Hypothesis were a constitutional participant,** hypothesis posture would carry warrant. Reviewed evidence requires evidence, admissibility, authority, and unknown preservation before stronger claims.

### Confidence

**Medium-high** that a recoverable constitutional neighborhood exists around the apparent Frame pressure, because recurring repository evidence consistently shows adjacent positions and bridges among external/source-side condition, observation, evidence, warrant, constitutional representation, projection/view, presentation/orientation/narrative method, unknown preservation, and repository authority.

**Low** that `Frame` itself is recovered as a constitutional type, family, resolution, owner, implementation surface, or runtime behavior. This survey intentionally does not recover Frames.

**Medium** on the exact topology vocabulary, because several names remain investigative labels and repository authority may later choose different stable names.
