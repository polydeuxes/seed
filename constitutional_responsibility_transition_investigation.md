## Recurring constitutional evidence

Recurring constitutional evidence supports these laws:

1. **Movement is evidence-gated.** A responsibility-relevant movement is lawful
   only where repository evidence, support, provenance, and a competent local
   boundary authorize that specific movement.
2. **Movement is not unbounded conveyance.** Lawful movement is bounded as
   representation, translation, projection, recording, communication,
   evaluation, reliance, or stop-preserving movement; it is not truth conveyance.
3. **Warrant must survive.** Evidence/support, provenance, role, authority
   boundary, negative authority, unknowns, and confidence limits must remain
   available for lawful reliance.
4. **Reliance is downstream and bounded.** A downstream participant may use
   warranted content only for the role, scope, and confidence that evidence
   supports.
5. **Unknowns remain constitutional material.** Evidence gaps and unresolved
   boundaries are preserved rather than silently converted into knowledge,
   ownership, mutation authority, or completion.
6. **Locality remains visible.** Movement stays inside the family, surface,
   artifact, view, diagnostic, report, or responsibility boundary competent for
   the movement.
7. **Lawful stop is positive evidence.** Movement must stop where evidence,
   authority, provenance, unknowns, confidence, mutation boundary, or claim
   strength would be lost or exceeded.
8. **Produced artifact and performed movement remain distinct.** A report,
   explanation, diagnostic row, answer phrase, or projection can preserve or
   expose a movement without itself becoming the moved responsibility.

## Independent survey

The following situations were surveyed independently before comparison. The
phrases are orientation examples only; none is treated as a newly introduced
participant.

### Projection to Observation

**Supporting evidence.** Lawful-transition evidence supports movement from
preserved constitutional material into consumer-specific projection, and from
source-side material into scoped constitutional representation only through a
competent observation or representation boundary. Projection evidence is
read/view bounded and derived from preserved material.

**Contradicting evidence.** Projection is repeatedly not verification, not
source observation, not mutation, not external truth, and not the only preserved
interpretation. A projected forecast or view cannot become observation merely
because time passes or because the view exists.

**Responsibility that appears to move.** Responsibility for present-facing
lawful reliance may move from a projection-shaped concern to an observation-
supported condition only when a later repository-supported observation boundary
actually preserves evidence of the current condition.

**Responsibility that remains.** The projection remains responsible only for
its own derivation, support, confidence, assumptions, and non-verifying boundary.
The observation remains responsible only for the evidence it actually observes
and preserves.

**Visible boundaries.** Derivation, projection version/surface, observation
source, provenance, confidence, current/stale distinction, and non-promotion
remain visible.

**Unknowns.** Whether any particular projection has been superseded by an
observation remains unknown unless repository evidence shows the observation,
its scope, and its authority.

### Derived conclusion to current lawful condition

**Supporting evidence.** Current lawful condition evidence supports local
progressions where evidence assessment can constrain supported selection,
termination, and present condition. Warrant and reliance evidence support use of
content after derivation only when support and authority survive.

**Contradicting evidence.** A derived conclusion is not current lawful condition
by itself. Repository history distinguishes event history, projection, current
belief, context view, decision-facing view, audit output, and external condition.

**Responsibility that appears to move.** Responsibility for what may be relied
on as current may move from derivation to current lawful condition only when
preserved evidence, projection/currentness discipline, confidence, contradiction,
and unknown boundaries support the current condition.

**Responsibility that remains.** The derived conclusion remains responsible for
its reasoning/support chain and claim strength. Current lawful condition remains
responsible for present admissibility, commitment, assessment, selection, and
stop limits where supported.

**Visible boundaries.** Event/support provenance, derivation path, currentness,
contradictions, unknowns, and lawful stop remain visible.

**Unknowns.** The repository does not prove a universal sequence by which every
derived conclusion becomes current condition.

### Operator concern to Repository artifact

**Supporting evidence.** Source-to-constitutional transition evidence permits
operator statements or concerns to become source-attributed material, candidate
claims, inquiry orientation, records, or artifacts when preserved with source,
scope, support, authority, and unknown limits.

**Contradicting evidence.** Operator concern is not repository truth, not
implementation authority, not recovery, not mutation, and not architectural law
by itself.

**Responsibility that appears to move.** Responsibility for preserving the
bounded concern may move from unpreserved operator expression to a repository
artifact when the artifact records the concern with its evidence and limits.

**Responsibility that remains.** The operator concern remains an expression or
source-side input. The artifact remains responsible for preservation and scope,
not for proving the concern true.

**Visible boundaries.** Source identity, artifact path, record scope, claim
strength, negative authority, and unknowns remain visible.

**Unknowns.** Whether the artifact later supports stronger knowledge remains
unknown without separate evidence-gated promotion.

### Reasoning to Reasoning path

**Supporting evidence.** Reasoning-space translation evidence identifies
reasoning path as a diagnostic surface that builds explanation chains from
implemented diagnostic evidence through intermediate and derived conclusions to
consumers and operational-story impact while preserving unknowns and boundaries.

**Contradicting evidence.** Reasoning path is scoped to implemented diagnostic
surfaces. It is not a universal reasoning engine, not new evidence, not mutation,
and not repository truth.

**Responsibility that appears to move.** Responsibility for explaining why a
conclusion exists may move from implicit or distributed reasoning evidence into
a reasoning-path artifact when the path preserves evidence, conclusions,
consumers, impact, unknowns, and boundary.

**Responsibility that remains.** Underlying diagnostics and evidence remain
responsible for their local facts. The reasoning path remains responsible for
explanation shape and preservation, not for creating the facts it explains.

**Visible boundaries.** Diagnostic scope, evidence list, intermediate and
derived conclusions, consumers, story impact, unknowns, and read-only boundary
remain visible.

**Unknowns.** Reasoning outside implemented diagnostic surfaces is not covered
by the reasoning-path responsibility.

### Temporary ownership to Bounded ownership

**Supporting evidence.** Responsibility and local-ownership investigations
recurringly recover bounded authorized movement, local responsibility, explicit
exclusions, compatibility preservation, implementation-backed sufficiency, and
stop conditions.

**Contradicting evidence.** Recurring evidence rejects universal governance,
runtime ownership, support lifetime, and broad ownership claims without
implementation-backed boundaries.

**Responsibility that appears to move.** Responsibility may move from a
provisional or temporary local holder to bounded ownership only when repository
evidence establishes the bounded subject, authority, evidence, local scope,
negative authority, and stop boundary.

**Responsibility that remains.** Neighboring responsibilities remain with their
local surfaces. Temporary or candidate material remains candidate where evidence
does not authorize stronger ownership.

**Visible boundaries.** Owner scope, local authority, exclusions, compatibility
handoff, implementation evidence, and unresolved ownership unknowns remain
visible.

**Unknowns.** A complete ownership topology and universal ownership transition
law remain unsupported.

### Support to Warrant

**Supporting evidence.** Warrant evidence directly supports the movement from
support/provenance into evidence-bound lawful reliance under a preserved
authority boundary. It requires role, authority limit, negative authority,
unknowns, confidence, and stop/non-promotion discipline.

**Contradicting evidence.** Mere support is not warrant if it lacks provenance,
role, authority boundary, negative authority, unknown preservation, confidence
limit, or competent local authorization.

**Responsibility that appears to move.** Responsibility for permitting bounded
lawful downstream use may move from raw support into warrant when the complete
warrant boundary survives.

**Responsibility that remains.** Support remains evidence/support; warrant
remains the entitlement and limit for reliance. Warrant is not reliance itself,
truth, mutation authority, or implementation permission.

**Visible boundaries.** Evidence/support, provenance, role, authority boundary,
negative authority, unknowns, confidence, and stop remain visible.

**Unknowns.** Whether every support form can become warrant remains unknown;
repository evidence supports only bounded cases.

### Current concern to Lawful stop

**Supporting evidence.** Movement-participation and grammar-recovery evidence
support lawful stop when continued movement would exceed evidence, authority,
boundary, unknown, reliance, negative-authority, confidence, mutation, or claim
strength limits.

**Contradicting evidence.** Concern alone is not proof that movement may
continue, and pressure is not recovery. Stop is not proof that future movement is
impossible; it is present lawful termination under current evidence.

**Responsibility that appears to move.** Responsibility for present
constitutional discipline may move from continuing concern to lawful stop when
the repository-supported boundary shows no further lawful move without overreach.

**Responsibility that remains.** The concern remains preserved if supported,
and unknowns remain unknown. Future evidence may reopen movement, but present
work must not infer through the gap.

**Visible boundaries.** Stop reason, unsupported movement, preserved unknowns,
negative authority, confidence, and current evidence limit remain visible.

**Unknowns.** Whether later evidence will support new movement remains unknown.

## Supported movements

After the independent survey, recurring repository evidence supports this bounded
answer:

```text
Constitutional responsibility may lawfully move from one supported
constitutional participant to another only when repository evidence shows that
responsibility-bearing material has crossed a competent local boundary while
preserving warrant: evidence/support, provenance, role, authority boundary,
negative authority, unknowns, confidence limits, locality, and lawful stop.
```

The recurring supported movement is not unbounded conveyance of truth, ownership, runtime
control, or future operational concern. It is **warrant-preserving, boundary-
local movement of the responsibility for lawful reliance, preservation,
rendering, classification, explanation, or stop** from the prior supported
surface to the later supported surface competent for the now-supported condition.

The clearest recurring form is:

```text
source / derivation / projection / concern / support

→ evidence-preserving local boundary

→ warranted current use, artifact, observation, reasoning path,
   bounded ownership, or lawful stop

while the earlier surface keeps its own derivation, provenance,
claim-strength, and non-promotion responsibilities.
```

Therefore, constitutional responsibility **does lawfully move** in repository
evidence, but only as bounded constitutional responsibility changes under
preserved evidence and authority. It does not move by vocabulary, elapsed time,
forecast, concern, presentation, runtime mechanism, or implementation desire.
