## Independently surveyed transitions

### Transition 1: Objective Reality to Constitutional Reality

#### What crosses?

Source-side material may cross only as bounded material available to Seed: observed source material, provider output, operator statement, file/document content, external sample, foreign testimony, imported report, raw input, or candidate claim. What crosses is not the external condition itself, but source-attributed material capable of being preserved, inspected, translated, or represented.

#### What is transformed?

External/source-side material may be transformed into inspected input, provider-language candidate, parsed record, observation, evidence, represented fact, imported testimony, scoped accepted claim, or other bounded constitutional representation already supported by repository evidence. The transformation changes carrier and representation; it does not prove external truth.

#### What must remain preserved?

The transition must preserve source identity where available, source type, vantage, payload or payload reference, sample/observation/preservation timing distinctions, confidence or quality limits where available, evidence identity, import/transfer path where applicable, authority scope, claim strength, negative authority, contradictions, and unresolved unknowns.

#### What explicitly cannot cross?

Objective truth cannot cross as truth merely because material was received. Source existence, provider vocabulary, operator concern, foreign output, presentation label, imported report, repeated phrase, or diagnostic visibility cannot cross as Seed truth, local observation, verified fact, repository knowledge, mutation authority, or universal constitutional law by itself.

#### What authorizes movement?

Movement is authorized by repository-visible evidence satisfying the competent local boundary for the kind of representation being made. Recurring authorization includes valid observation construction, provider-specific validation, source-attributed evidence preservation, fact admission within supported claim strength, scoped import acceptance, warrant-preserving translation, and explicit authority boundaries.

#### What terminates movement?

Movement terminates when evidence is insufficient, the source is malformed or unsupported, authority does not reach the proposed claim, provenance would be lost, claim strength would be exceeded, contradiction cannot lawfully be resolved, the subject remains unknown, or the proposed movement would silently promote observation, candidate, imported testimony, or visibility into truth.

#### What remains local?

The external condition remains outside Seed representation. Source vantage, foreign authority, provider-local syntax, source clocks, and imported testimony remain local to their originating boundary unless explicitly represented with preserved provenance and scope. Seed's constitutional representation remains local to Seed's evidence, authority, warrant, and current lawful condition.

#### Confidence

High that the recurring law is evidence-gated preservation/translation into scoped constitutional representation, not truth transfer. Medium-high that the Objective Reality / Constitutional Reality names are stable enough for this Survey, because repository evidence treats those labels as investigative rather than automatically promoted vocabulary.

### Transition 2: Constitutional Reality to Subjective Reality

#### What crosses?

Preserved constitutional material may cross into consumer-facing view: event-derived state, observations, evidence, facts, relationships, support aggregates, contradictions, confidence limits, unknowns, inquiry conditions, explanation inputs, projection shape, diagnostic findings, reports, and answer material.

#### What is transformed?

Preserved representation is transformed into projection, read model, context view, explanation, diagnostic payload, report, operational story, answer composition, current-facts view, why/impact view, or other bounded consumer-specific surface. The transformation selects and renders already-preserved material for a consumer or question.

#### What must remain preserved?

The movement must preserve derivation from preserved material, evidence/support links, provenance, confidence, contradiction, stale/current distinctions, read-only boundaries where declared, diagnostic record/mutation boundaries, unknowns, unsupported status, authority limits, and the difference between projected current belief and preserved history.

#### What explicitly cannot cross?

Projection cannot cross as truth, verification, mutation, source observation, evidence export, repository knowledge, or the only preserved interpretation. Presentation vocabulary cannot cross as constitutional knowledge without evidence. Diagnostic output cannot cross as cluster truth. Explanation cannot cross as a new reasoning mechanism. Context cannot cross as provider execution or state mutation.

#### What authorizes movement?

Movement is authorized by preserved constitutional material and the bounded surface competent to render it: projection over event history, read-model construction, explanation traversal over projected support, diagnostic inventory/shape visibility, context composition from already projected state and evidence, and warrant-preserving output that stays inside its authority boundary.

#### What terminates movement?

Movement terminates when rendering would require new observation, new fact promotion, mutation, provider execution, authority expansion, unsupported vocabulary promotion, loss of provenance/support, erasure of contradiction or unknowns, or conversion of a consumer-specific selection into canonical truth.

#### What remains local?

Consumer interpretation remains local to the view, diagnostic, report, explanation, context, or answer surface. Presentation labels remain local to presentation unless separately warranted. Projection outputs remain derived and surface-specific. Unknowns may be projected to compatibility shapes while typed or more precise unresolved conditions remain local where repository evidence says they are local.

#### Confidence

High that preserved constitutional material lawfully moves to consumer-specific read/projection/explanation/diagnostic surfaces only under preserved provenance, support, authority, and unknown boundaries. Medium-high that the Subjective Reality label itself is stable enough for this Survey, because the repository supports consumer-specific interpretation while not necessarily stabilizing that label as canonical vocabulary.

### Transition 3: Subjective Reality to Constitutional Reality

#### What crosses?

A consumer-facing view may cross back only as evidence that a surface rendered something, a diagnostic record when recording is explicitly supported, an operator-visible report, a bounded audit finding, or a candidate for inquiry. The view's selected meaning does not cross back as constitutional truth merely by being displayed.

#### What is transformed?

Rendered output may be transformed into a bounded diagnostic record, audit artifact, report evidence, inquiry orientation, or candidate subject for later evidence-gated review. If the repository supports recording, the recorded artifact remains scoped to its record boundary rather than silently becoming cluster truth.

#### What must remain preserved?

The movement must preserve the originating surface, render scope, record scope, event-ledger/mutation distinction, source projected material, unsupported limitations, unknowns, confidence, and the fact that the surface was a consumer-specific selection over already-preserved material.

#### What explicitly cannot cross?

A report, explanation, diagnostic row, read-model row, answer phrase, presentation label, or projected current belief cannot cross back as fact, implementation authority, repository knowledge, ownership, mutation, or universal law merely because it appeared in output. A view cannot retroactively become the source evidence it summarized.

#### What authorizes movement?

Movement is authorized only by explicit repository-supported recording, diagnostic declaration, audit scope, or subsequent evidence-gated promotion through the competent local boundary. Warrant must survive: evidence/support, provenance, role, authority boundary, negative authority, unknowns, and confidence limits must remain available.

#### What terminates movement?

Movement terminates when the output is only presentation, when record scope is absent, when mutation authority is absent, when support/provenance cannot be traced, when the view would be treated as source evidence for itself, when diagnostic findings would become cluster truth, or when a label would be promoted without implementation or repository evidence.

#### What remains local?

The rendering remains local to its surface. Diagnostic records remain local to diagnostic scope unless explicitly operational. Projection/export shape remains local to the consumer boundary. Presentation vocabulary remains local to presentation. Unknowns remain unknown and may remain compressed at public compatibility boundaries.

#### Confidence

Medium-high. The recurring refusal law is strong, but this reverse movement is less central than the source-to-representation and representation-to-view transitions. Repository evidence supports bounded recording and audit artifacts, not automatic return from view to truth.

### Transition 4: Constitutional Reality to Objective Reality

#### What crosses?

Nothing crosses as a power to change Objective Reality merely by representation. At most, Seed may communicate, export, explain, or act through separate operational authority not recovered in this Survey. For this Survey, constitutional representation does not cross into external reality as truth-making force.

#### What is transformed?

Within the bounded evidence reviewed here, constitutional representation may be transformed into exported report, explanation, projection, or communicated claim. That transformation communicates represented material; it does not transform external reality itself.

#### What must remain preserved?

Any outward communication must preserve provenance, authority scope, confidence, support, contradictions, unknowns, and the distinction between represented claim and external condition. Projection export must not erase the difference between evidence export and view export.

#### What explicitly cannot cross?

Seed representation cannot cross as external truth, external event identity, foreign authority, objective occurrence, source sample, or proof that the world is as represented. Projection export cannot cross as evidence export unless support and provenance survive.

#### What authorizes movement?

For the evidence reviewed here, only bounded communication/export/reporting authority is visible. Any stronger operational movement would require separate repository evidence and is outside this Survey.

#### What terminates movement?

Movement terminates when representation would be treated as making reality true, when authority would exceed export/report scope, when support/provenance would be stripped, or when the Survey would need to recover implementation or operational responsibility.

#### What remains local?

Objective Reality remains external. Seed's represented claims, local authority, current lawful condition, and projection choices remain local to Seed. Foreign acceptance remains local to the receiver and cannot be presumed.

#### Confidence

Medium. The refusal is high-confidence: representation is not objective truth. The positive outward movement is intentionally low-detail because recovering operational action or implementation is forbidden by this task.

## Recurring lawful transition behavior

After surveying each transition independently, the recurring lawful movement is:

1. Movement is never naked transfer of truth.
2. Movement is representation-, translation-, projection-, recording-, or communication-bounded.
3. Movement is lawful only when evidence and a competent local boundary authorize the specific transformation.
4. Movement must carry enough provenance, support, authority scope, confidence, contradiction, and unknown state to prevent stronger reliance than the source permits.
5. Movement stops at insufficiency, unsupported promotion, authority loss, provenance loss, unknown erasure, contradiction erasure, mutation overreach, or presentation-vocabulary promotion.
6. Each layer keeps a local remainder that does not cross: external condition, constitutional warrant, consumer surface selection, and presentation vocabulary all remain non-identical.

## Recurring preservation laws

- Preserve source/provenance across movement.
- Preserve authority boundary and negative authority.
- Preserve evidence/support links and claim strength.
- Preserve timing distinctions where relevant: occurrence, sample, observation, preservation, projection, diagnostic, and report time are not automatically the same.
- Preserve contradiction and non-resolution where evidence has not resolved it.
- Preserve unknowns as unknowns, including typed/local unknowns when repository evidence supports local typing and public compatibility projection.
- Preserve the distinction between event history, projection, context/view, diagnostic output, report, and external condition.
- Preserve confidence and evidence-sufficiency limits.

## Recurring refusal laws

- Observation is not automatically fact.
- Fact is not automatically external truth.
- Projection is not verification.
- Explanation is not new evidence.
- Diagnostic visibility is not cluster truth.
- Presentation vocabulary is not repository knowledge without evidence.
- Imported or foreign output is not local truth without scoped acceptance and preserved provenance.
- Agreement is not truth promotion.
- Unknown preservation is not unknown resolution.
- Repeated transition behavior does not authorize a new owner, implementation, architecture, or constitutional family.

## Recurring authorization patterns

- Evidence authorizes movement only through the local boundary competent to interpret that evidence.
- Warrant authorizes bounded reliance only when evidence/support, provenance, role, authority boundary, negative authority, unknowns, and confidence limits survive.
- Translation is lawful when it preserves warranted content while reducing carrier-specific excess and refusing unsupported promotion.
- Projection is lawful when it derives from preserved material and remains read/view bounded.
- Recording is lawful only when the repository-supported record scope and mutation boundary remain explicit.
- Promotion is lawful only when family-local evidence satisfies family-local boundary and stop conditions.

## Recurring stop conditions

Movement stops when:

- evidence is absent, insufficient, stale beyond claimed currentness, malformed, unsupported, or outside the competent boundary;
- provenance, support, authority, confidence, contradiction, or unknown status would be lost;
- claim strength would exceed evidence;
- presentation vocabulary would be promoted without repository evidence;
- a view would become source truth for itself;
- diagnostic output would mutate cluster truth without operational authority;
- comparison would introduce ownership, implementation, runtime architecture, or constitutional families not recovered by repository authority.

## Recurring locality preservation

- Objective Reality remains external and cannot be exhausted by Seed representation.
- Constitutional Reality remains local to Seed's preserved evidence, warrant, authority, current lawful condition, and non-promotion boundaries.
- Subjective Reality remains local to its consumer surface, view, diagnostic, report, explanation, or answer composition.
- Source syntax, provider-local contract, foreign authority, and presentation vocabulary remain local unless translated with preserved warrant and explicit boundaries.
- Unknowns may remain local to implementation or inquiry records even when projected into compressed public compatibility shapes.

- Whether future evidence will recover additional local transition types remains unknown.

Confidence is medium for the layer names because repository evidence supports the layered distinction as an investigative recovery while preserving caution around presentation vocabulary becoming repository knowledge.
