# Contextual Interpretation Warrant Set Slice 001

This slice adds one read-only producer for candidate-scoped contextual interpretation warrant sets.

The producer preserves exact operator material, explicit interpretation candidates, candidate-scoped corrections, bounded retrospective evidence, optional closed-choice selection binding references, clarification evidence, unknowns, conflicts, residual source material, and known loss.

Boundary commitments:

- Conversation history is not treated as the bounded retrospective corpus.
- Examined retrospective material is dispositioned rather than automatically counted as support.
- Source material and corrected material are preserved separately.
- Correction candidates remain separate from interpretation candidates.
- Evidence is candidate-local; warrant material for candidate A is not used for candidate B.
- Warrant standing does not select an interpretation.
- Multiple or unique warranted candidates remain unselected.
- The producer is read-only and does not bind goals, move inquiries, authorize, execute, record, or mutate state.
