## 2. Question-related families and artifacts found

### 2.1 `QuestionFamily` / `QuestionSurfaceInventoryRow`

Current implementation treats `QuestionFamily` as an exact public answerable identity encoded in the static question-surface inventory. The row carries family name, example questions, answering surface, CLI flag, answer responsibility, authority boundary, notes, bounded status, dispatch surface, required args, formatter support, implementation reason, diagnostic relationship, and relationship status.

Constitutional meaning already recovered beyond exact-string mechanics:

- `QuestionFamily` is a public answerability namespace value.
- Identity is exact and inventory-backed; similarity of wording is not admission.
- Inventory admission is not the same thing as bounded-work eligibility.
- Family identity is not equivalent to public diagnostic registration; diagnostic relationship is row metadata, and some inventory families are diagnostic-only.
- Family rows preserve answer responsibility and authority boundary as public explanation material.
- Eligibility derives from bounded ask maps, not from semantic question interpretation.
- Selection of a dispatch surface is separate from eligibility.
- Dispatch delegates to an existing answer surface and does not itself gather evidence or compose all answers.
- Composed question-family explanation is presentation over existing inventory fields; it does not admit, promote, or recover a family.

### 2.2 Family-local answering surfaces

The inventory rows point to existing surfaces such as operational pressure, operational story, knowledge reachability, capability pressure, ownership ambiguity, observation domain coverage, observation permission state, authority-constrained ownership/reachability surfaces, projection shape visibility, reasoning path, selection path, and constitutional pipeline. These are family-local answer surfaces. They own their own evidence, support, rendering, limitation, and stop behavior. `QuestionFamily` dispatch does not make them one universal answer engine.

### 2.3 Diagnostic-only question families

`surface inventory` and `surface shape validation` are visible in the question-family terrain but are not inquiry-answer surfaces for bounded ask. They prove that public inventory visibility and executable eligibility are distinct.

### 2.4 Unknown-family artifacts

Unknown family lookup and definition/explanation behavior preserve an unknown result rather than interpreting free text. Unknown-family refusal is an implemented stop. It is not a typed Unknown record by itself unless a local producer explicitly wraps it as one.

### 2.5 Question Shape / Survey Warrant Family artifacts

Question Shape recurs in investigations as descriptive bounded-inquiry form. Survey Warrant Family recurs as a constitutional/investigation pattern about bounded survey authority. Neither is currently a public bounded ask dispatch identity. The strongest relationship is adjacency: some question shapes may explain why a family is coherent, and survey warrants may discipline investigations, but only explicit registration makes a `QuestionFamily` answerable.

### 2.6 Individual bounded-question artifacts

`BoundedConstitutionalQuestion` is an immutable, deterministic, provenance-preserving artifact built from explicit caller inputs. It carries operator inquiry, inquiry provenance, bounded question text, constitutional intent, scope status, uncertainty, unknowns, caller fields, testimony status, read-only boundaries, and non-mutation flags. Its standing is testimony preservation, not proof that a constitutional question was originated or established by repository evidence.

Downstream individual-question artifacts include:

- constitutional question projection;
- constitutional capability projection;
- selected constitutional views;
- constitutional view composition;
- constitutional pipeline invocation request/result;
- examination frontier;
- examination policy projection;
- examination work selection;
- future probe request handoff.

These belong beside and downstream from QuestionFamily terrain, not inside it. They are roads for an already supplied individual bounded question. They do not redefine `QuestionFamily` as an individual question, and they do not prove every question road begins with an established individual question.

### 2.7 Bounded-goal-relative question reports

Recent goal-to-question and bounded-goal bridge reports preserve testimony about a desired or possible road from bounded goal condition to individual constitutional question. Current implementation does not establish that road as live. The current live goal terrain selects exact bounded goals for inquiry consideration, projects repository/world uncertainty testimony into inquiry need, and can build frontier artifacts. It does not currently produce a constitutionally established `BoundedConstitutionalQuestion` from that condition.

## 5. Recovered disconnected roads

Classification: implementation-backed refusal; semantic road disconnected.

Classification: constitutionally supported but disconnected.

Typed Unknowns preserve unresolved conditions and can describe evidence gaps or implementation unknowns about family relations. No implementation lets an Unknown constrain, select, permit, or refuse a family except through ordinary local refusal strings not modeled as typed Unknown consumers.

Goal focus selection and inquiry-need projection exist. A live producer from bounded goal condition plus current work, pressure, evidence demand, and lawful stop to constitutionally established `BoundedConstitutionalQuestion` was not located.

Classification: represented in recent reports; implementation disconnected.

### D5: bounded goal condition to `QuestionFamily`

### D6: inquiry frontier to individual bounded question

Reports describe a possible frontier-to-question adapter. Current implementation has bounded inquiry frontier artifacts, but no confirmed live adapter establishing a `BoundedConstitutionalQuestion` from one frontier in the current code path surveyed.

### D7: eligibility to selection to dispatch as one universal road

Exact QuestionFamily bounded ask separates eligibility, selected surface value, selected dispatch surface, dispatch request, dispatch result, and surface-local answering. Examination work selection is a different local selection surface. No universal eligibility/selection/dispatch owner was found.

### D8: evidence gathering to answering to completion to retirement

Family-local surfaces gather or consume evidence and render answers. Completion and retirement are separate constitutional stopping terrain. No universal implementation road makes evidence gathering become answering, answering become completion, or completion become retirement.

Classification: constitutionally supported distinctions; disconnected as a single road.

### D9: constitutional acts to question admission

Constitutional acts, admission, authorization, and selection recur in reports and Book terrain, but no implemented act admits a recovered question family or establishes an individual bounded question from operator prose.

Classification: constitutionally supported but disconnected.

### D10: answer responsibility to family recovery

Classification: implementation-local in rows; constitutionally under-characterized as recovery road.

1. Older question-family registration audits emphasize exact public family identity, static inventory registration, bounded eligibility, and dispatch to existing answer surfaces. Recent bounded-question reports sometimes make individual bounded-question topology feel central. Current implementation supports both roads, but they are not one road.

3. Older typed-Unknown characterization distinguishes multiple constitutional Unknown states. Recent bounded-question reports sometimes flatten `unknowns` into fields on a question artifact. Current implementation requires preserving the distinction between typed Unknown records, family-local unknown strings, unclassified reasons, and constitutional Unknown states.
4. The prior “approximately 2 connected / 8 disconnected roads” remains plausible only under a narrow counting frame. With broader implementation terrain, more connected local roads exist, but the big missing roads remain disconnected.

## 11. Smallest truthful description of the current question terrain

The repository contains several question-related territories, not one universal question pipeline.

The strongest current `QuestionFamily` terrain is exact, static, public, inventory-backed answerability: a family is known when a row admits its exact name, eligible only when bounded ask maps permit it, selected only through local dispatch-surface/value helpers, and answered only by an existing family-local surface. This terrain also exposes answer responsibility, authority boundary, diagnostic relationship, and explanation, but it does not recover families from prose, shapes, Unknowns, goals, pressure, or observations.

The strongest individual-question terrain is a read-only testimony/projection terrain: an already supplied `BoundedConstitutionalQuestion` can move through projection, view selection, composition, pipeline invocation, and examination-frontier/work-selection roads. That artifact preserves caller testimony; it does not prove constitutional origination by itself.

The strongest typed-Unknown terrain preserves unresolved state with local type, area, reason, evidence gap, boundary, relationship, implementation, methodological, locality, constitutional, readiness, or future-investigation meaning. Typed Unknowns can discipline terrain descriptions, but they currently do not select or admit QuestionFamilies.

The strongest bounded-goal terrain selects exact bounded goals for inquiry consideration and projects repository/world uncertainty testimony into inquiry need. It does not currently establish either a QuestionFamily or an individual bounded constitutional question.

Therefore the current constitutional question city is a set of adjacent local roads: exact family answerability, individual bounded-question testimony/projection, goal-relative inquiry need, inquiry frontier/examination selection, and Unknown preservation. The major constitutional roads among them are visible, but many remain disconnected or report-only. The truthful map is plural, local, and boundary-preserving.
