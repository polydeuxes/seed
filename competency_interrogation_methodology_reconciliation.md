## Working methodology

These questions remain valuable but have not yet earned full permanence.

### Preconditions

Evidence currently supporting it:

- It repeatedly distinguished what must already exist before a surface can answer: projected state, recorded notes, candidates, inventory facts, selected documents, or diagnostic inputs.
- It prevented self-grounding claims in Capability Verification, Capability Inventory, and Inquiry Orientation.

Evidence still missing:

- More cases where Preconditions changes the answer independently from Evidence and Unknown / Stop.

Why provisional:

- It often moves with Evidence. It should remain because the mistake it prevents is real, but permanence as a separate section is not fully earned.

### Artifact Handoff

Evidence currently supporting it:

- It repeatedly exposed what survives crossing the worker boundary: reports, JSON, notes, verification rows, relation records, provenance, support strings, and excluded meaning.
- It exposed consumer and provenance gaps.

Evidence still missing:

- Stronger recurrence where handoff independently changes constitutional understanding beyond Constitutional reliance.

Why provisional:

- It is essential for handoff-heavy surfaces, but may be excessive for smaller inspection surfaces.

### Locality

Evidence currently supporting it:

- It prevented Documentation Structure from becoming universal structure ownership.
- It prevented Pressure Audit, Capability Inventory, Capability Verification, and Inquiry Orientation from becoming planners, governance owners, or universal intake.

Evidence still missing:

- Proof that Locality remains distinct from Boundaries in all small competencies.

Why provisional:

- It is valuable as an anti-universalization check, but often repeats boundary evidence.

### Continuity

Evidence currently supporting it:

- Public CLI surfaces, JSON output, diagnostic inventory, shape-audit coverage, and tests made continuity concrete in several interrogations.

Evidence still missing:

- Stronger evidence for surfaces whose public contracts are less diagnostic or less versioned.

Why provisional:

- It is useful when compatibility exists, but can become repetitive where implementation evidence already lists the same tests and flags.

### Self-Observation / Drift

Evidence currently supporting it:

- Diagnostic inventory, shape audit, tests, CLI help, and before/after state checks repeatedly acted as drift evidence.

Evidence still missing:

- A consistent rule for when a surface must be diagnostic-inventory visible versus merely test-backed.

Why provisional:

- It is useful but uneven. The methodology can notice visibility gaps without deciding architecture.

### Evolution

Evidence currently supporting it:

- It sometimes distinguishes identity-preserving refactors from identity-changing expansions.

Evidence still missing:

- Independent recurrence where Evolution changes conclusions not already recovered by Identity, Boundaries, or Negative Authority.

Why provisional:

- It remains the weakest recurring major section and is a plausible reconciliation candidate.

### Methodology audit

Evidence currently supporting it:

- It caught weak questions, compressed questions, and elaboration risk in later interrogations.

Evidence still missing:

- Whether every interrogation needs a local methodology audit after this reconciliation exists.

Why provisional:

- It is valuable while the discipline is maturing; it has not earned permanent inclusion in every future report.

### Methodology honesty candidates

Evidence currently supporting it:

- It preserved recurrent insights such as visibility not being authority and verification not being permission without turning them into law.

Evidence still missing:

- A settled preservation home or lifecycle for such candidates.

Why provisional:

- It must remain a notebook mechanism, not a vocabulary stabilizer.

### Remaining questions

Evidence currently supporting it:

- Every interrogation preserved gaps rather than filling them.

Evidence still missing:

- Whether Remaining questions should be merged with Unknown / Stop in smaller reports.

Why provisional:

- It remains useful but may overlap with Unknown / Stop.

## Questions that changed the methodology

These are not merely interesting questions. They materially altered later interrogation discipline through repeated use.

### What authority does this competency appear to possess, but implementation does not actually grant?

Recurrence:

- Pressure Audit appeared to recommend action; interrogation reduced recommendations to inspection references.
- Capability Verification appeared to grant use permission; interrogation reduced verification to confidence/status.
- Capability Inventory appeared to admit capabilities; interrogation reduced inventory to projected status presentation.
- Inquiry Orientation appeared to interpret operator intent; interrogation reduced it to preserved-prose orientation.

Methodological effect:

- Later interrogations began explicitly searching for apparent authority and naming negative authority.

Classification:

- Earned.

### What recurring implementation evidence keeps neighboring boundaries separate?

Recurrence:

- Pressure Audit versus selection/priority/action authority.
- Capability Verification versus Capability Inventory.
- Capability Inventory versus admission/governance.
- Inquiry Orientation versus Operator Intake, source navigation, dispatch, recommendation, and planning.

Methodological effect:

- Neighbor Boundary Analysis became a high-precision section rather than incidental comparison.

Classification:

- Earned.

### To what extent may this artifact be trusted?

Recurrence:

- Documentation Structure could be trusted for structure, not meaning.
- Pressure Audit could be trusted for read-only pressure evidence, not priority or command.
- Capability Verification could be trusted for status inspection, not permission.
- Inquiry Orientation could be trusted for lexical orientation, not intent.

Methodological effect:

- Trust split into trusted fields, trust extent, assumptions, and forbidden downstream use.

Classification:

- Earned, with consumer-governance subquestions still weak.

### What evidence permits movement and what evidence causes lawful stop?

Recurrence:

- Documentation selections, diagnostic inputs, candidate evidence, verification support, inventory state, and note overlap all permit only bounded movement.
- Missing input, missing support, unsupported conclusions, and authority claims cause stop.

Methodological effect:

- Evidence became a movement boundary rather than a generic data list.

Classification:

- Earned.

### What can remain unknown?

Recurrence:

- Meaning, authority, downstream consumers, invocation provenance, semantic intent, lifecycle, permission, and actionability repeatedly remained unknown.

Methodological effect:

- Unknowns became typed constitutional artifacts rather than report defects.

Classification:

- Earned.

### Seed may rely on / Seed must not rely on

Recurrence:

- Repeatedly reduced truthful use after identity characterization.

Methodological effect:

- Interrogations stopped at lawful reliance instead of drifting into recommendation or redesign.

Classification:

- Earned.

## Methodology maturity

Classification: **Stabilizing**.

Why not Exploratory:

- The method has repeated across seven interrogations.
- It has recurring sections, repeated refusal patterns, and repeated evidence-backed reductions.
- It has produced stable recoveries: negative authority, typed unknowns, constitutional reliance, neighbor boundaries, and evidence-class separation.

Why not merely Recovering:

- Later reports interrogate the method itself.
- Questions now have recurrence evidence, survival analysis, reduction challenges, and compression analysis.
- The method has learned to distrust elegance and require recurrence.

Why not Stable:

- Several sections remain overlapping or maturity-dependent.
- Consumer governance remains weak.
- Evolution has not earned independent permanence.
- Question ordering, grouping, phrasing, and completeness remain operator-mediated.
- The methodology exists as architectural notebook practice, not implemented runtime physiology.

Smallest truthful answer:
The discipline is stabilizing as a constitutional boundary-recovery method; it is not stable as a complete, implemented, or self-governing methodology.
