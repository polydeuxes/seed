# Bounded Operator Goal Establishment Slice 001

This slice adds a read-only `BoundedOperatorGoalEstablishment` artifact for the lawful ingress paths currently supported by Seed:

- `ClosedChoiceSelectionBinding`
- `OperatorExpressionInterpretationProjection`

The artifact preserves bounded operator orientation without treating that orientation as constitutional meta-target establishment, operating constraint enforcement, inquiry opening, authorization, execution, recording, or satisfaction judgment.

## Boundary

The implementation accepts lawful operator-origin evidence and produces one of:

- `established` when a bounded outcome and explicit sufficiency conditions are present;
- `provisional` when enough bounded orientation exists for reversible continuation but scope or sufficiency still needs sharpening;
- `refused` when no bounded orientation is supportable from the ingress artifact.

Operator-stated constraints are copied into the goal establishment artifact with provenance. They are not observed, enforced, scheduled, or satisfied here. CPU, memory, power, temperature, and time observations remain outside this slice.

## Preserved material

The artifact preserves:

- intended outcome at the available resolution;
- known and unresolved scope;
- provisional or established sufficiency conditions;
- stop conditions;
- operator acceptance provenance;
- operator-stated constraints;
- unknowns, ambiguities, conflicts, and known loss;
- exact lineage back to the ingress artifact.

Corrections can create a later goal establishment that references the earlier goal without rewriting the original ingress lineage.
