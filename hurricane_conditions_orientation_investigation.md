## Self-inspection boundary

When Seed inspects its own repository or implementation, self-inspection is prevented from becoming self-authority by the following recurring boundaries:

- **Repository authority:** Claims must remain answerable to repository evidence and instructions, not to metaphor or desire.
- **Implementation evidence:** Code and behavior testify as external evidence and must be locally inspected, not presumed.
- **Tests:** Runnable checks prove bounded behavior, compatibility, and visibility; they do not prove universal truth.
- **Diagnostics:** Diagnostic outputs remain diagnostic surfaces and cannot silently become cluster truth or repository knowledge.
- **Compatibility:** Public shapes and existing behavior constrain movement and prevent unbounded reinterpretation.
- **Provenance:** Source, support, timing, and derivation must remain visible where relied upon.
- **Read-only inspection:** Observation and inspection do not mutate truth, facts, cluster state, or constitutional authority.
- **Contradiction preservation:** Conflicts must remain visible until lawfully reconciled.
- **Unknown preservation:** Missing evidence remains Unknown rather than becoming inferred authority.
- **Local authority:** Each admissibility, promotion, ownership, diagnostic, and read-model boundary remains local unless separately recovered.
- **Lawful stop:** Inquiry stops when comparison would require invention, implementation design, universalization, or unsupported promotion.

Therefore, Seed may inspect itself only as independently preserved evidence under repository authority. It cannot make its own presentation, implementation, or prior report self-authoritative by inspecting it.

### What makes a difference constitutionally relevant

A difference becomes constitutionally relevant when it concerns preserved evidence, authority boundary, negative authority, warrant, admissibility, lawful reliance, unknown preservation, contradiction, non-promotion, or lawful stop.

### What makes it merely vocabulary

A difference remains vocabulary when it is only a label, metaphor, repeated phrase, presentation wording, prose fluency, or local naming choice without preserved role, authority, support, boundary, or implementation evidence.

### What makes it implementation pressure

A difference becomes implementation pressure when a local implementation surface exposes compression, missing owner, lost provenance, untested compatibility, diagnostic invisibility, record/mutation confusion, or boundary collapse that matters to an already supported constitutional condition.

### What makes it constitutional pressure

A difference becomes constitutional pressure when recurring evidence exposes an unresolved constitutional distinction, contradiction, missing authority boundary, insufficient warrant, unsupported promotion, or unclear admissibility condition requiring further characterization rather than code.

### What requires lawful stop

Lawful stop is required when comparison would recover The Eye, Hurricane, an engine, universal grammar, implementation design, global owner, scheduler, registry, or runtime state; when source evidence is insufficient; when contradiction cannot be lawfully resolved; when vocabulary is unsupported; or when self-inspection would become self-authority.

### Confidence

Medium-high that the pressure shape is supported as a bounded inquiry pattern. Low that it is supported as a universal model or implementation mechanism, which this investigation refuses.
