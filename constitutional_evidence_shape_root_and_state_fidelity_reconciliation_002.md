## 7. Smallest repository-supported answers

### Does an evidence shape exist before responsibility-local recognition?

Yes in the limited sense of material arrangement or preserved evidence presentation. `Evidence`, `Fact`, external testimony, selected-meaning snapshots, membership-result collections, and boundary clauses can be materially shaped before a local responsibility recognizes relevance or admissibility. Unknown or no if â€śevidence shapeâ€ť means relevance, admissibility, sufficiency, warrant, or authority for a bounded question.

### What establishes that shape?

Plural answer: observation records establish occurrence/material shape; preservation/presentation owners establish attributed testimony or collection shape; projection/finalization establishes reconstructible projected material and support/conflict indexes; consumer-local recognizers establish applicability, admission, frontier askability, or warrant standing.

### Can the same material carry multiple lawful evidence shapes?

Yes. A selected interpretation can have a preserved meaning shape, purpose-local applicability shape, consumer-local admission shape, and later bounded-goal establishment use. A fact can have material subject/predicate/value shape, support/conflict shape, freshness condition, and later consumer-specific relevance.

### What exactly belongs to a root?

Only the local recognition/determination that a bounded question can make over supplied material: selecting coordinates relevant to that question, preserving local Unknown/conflict, and producing a bounded standing or stop. The root does not automatically own observation, presentation, admissibility, sufficiency, warrant, authority, execution, or global truth.

### What belongs instead to observation, admissibility, sufficiency, warrant, or authority?

Observation owns source/occurrence/material recording. Presentation/preservation owns attributed and bounded arrangement. Admissibility owns exact consumer/purpose uptake. Sufficiency owns whether required local support is enough for a next standing. Warrant owns bounded reliance under explicit support/contract/authority/dependency constraints. Authority owns permission/scope only where independently evidenced; external testimony and provider language do not supply it by themselves.

### How does a recognized shape make a local question applicable?

It supplies the minimum identity, provenance, coordinate, requirement, clause-family, or support standing that the local question requires. Applicability does not mean admission; frontier establishment does not mean inquiry executed; support standing does not mean authority; warrant does not mean execution.

### What constitutional subject, if any, does the implementation aggregate named `State` faithfully realize?

Smallest faithful subject: a reconstructible projection aggregate over append-only event history, finalized with local derived indexes and compatibility collections, subordinate to ledger authority. It does not faithfully realize a single constitutional subject named â€śprojected knowledgeâ€ť without narrowing. Its contents include projected material, recognized support/conflict standings, lifecycle posture, conditions, outcomes, and external/legacy compatibility grammar.

### Which uses of state can now be removed, and which still require further recovery?

Removable in later rename work: local uses where evidence now supports a narrower subject, such as cache freshness, projection snapshot, support standing, conflict posture, membership classification, applicability standing, admission standing, frontier condition, or warrant standing. Still require further recovery: aggregate `State`, `StateProjector`, lifecycle/status families, compatibility identifiers, serialized fields, and contaminated tool/capability/registry roads.
