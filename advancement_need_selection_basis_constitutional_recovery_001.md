## Selection-basis grammar recovered

### Book grammar

The strongest realization-invariant Book grammar is plural and local:

```text
bounded candidate set
+ locally lawful selection basis
+ owner-required identity / applicability validation
→ selection act result or lawful non-selection
```

The Book does not require one universal selection-basis type. It says a selection act may consume a bounded candidate set plus **selection evidence, exact comparison, or policy**, validate the identity and applicability required by that owner, and choose zero or more candidates or preserve lawful non-selection. It also warns that consideration selection, representation selection, meaning selection, closed-choice binding, policy/work selection, and operational-realization selection remain distinct local families.

Therefore, lawful basis for advancement-need consideration selection may be any local standing that the responsible consideration-selection road recognizes as sufficient for selecting an already-established advancement need. Current Book grammar permits several shapes in principle:

1. **Exact externally supplied selection evidence**: a bounded candidate set plus exact reference testimony that identifies one candidate.
2. **Applicable policy standing**: a bounded candidate set plus a policy that is applicable to that candidate set and has enough local rules to choose or preserve non-selection.
3. **Exact comparison standing**: a bounded candidate set plus comparison against a bounded purpose, where the comparison itself is a responsible constitutional act rather than name similarity or presentation order.
4. **Dependency testimony plus downstream warrant**: dependency standing may contribute, but only when a local owner establishes applicability, comparison, sufficiency, or warrant for selection.

No Book evidence supports automatic promotion from visibility, dependency pressure, candidate eligibility, uniqueness, route usefulness, or criticality vocabulary into selection basis.

| Distinction | Status | Recovery |

| testimony != applicable selection evidence | explicit Book grammar / locally supported | Testimony may be available, visible, or addressable without being applicable to the selector. |
| applicable selection evidence != sufficient selection basis | explicit Book grammar | Applicability, admission, consumption, and sufficiency are separate consumer-local boundaries. |
| sufficient selection basis != selection warrant | locally supported / partly Unknown | Book grammar names warrant and sufficiency but does not define one universal selection warrant; local owners must say whether sufficiency is the warrant or an additional warrant exists. |
| identity match != applicability | explicit Book grammar | Identity checks are owner-required validation, but exact identity alone does not necessarily establish applicability for every road. |
| applicability != admission | explicit Book grammar | Applicable material may remain unadmitted. |
| admission != sufficiency | explicit Book grammar | Admitted material may still be insufficient for the target consumer. |
| sufficiency != warrant | locally supported / Unknown globally | Some roads collapse them locally; the Book does not universalize that collapse. |
| dependency pressure != selection basis automatically | explicit Book grammar / locally supported | Visibility or pressure can create a lawful possibility for later acts but does not authorize uptake or selection without a local basis. |

## Realization-invariant constitutional topology

```text
bounded purpose / goal / horizon
→ candidate standing production
→ candidate set preservation
→ optional reference projection / addressability
→ selection-basis production or preservation
→ selector-local identity and applicability validation
→ selector-local admission if required by that road
→ selector-local sufficiency or warrant judgment if required by that road
→ consideration-selection act
→ selected-for-consideration standing OR lawful non-selection
→ downstream consumer-specific uptake / reprojection
```

Responsibilities are not collapsed:

- Candidate producers establish native advancement-need standing.
- Candidate-set assemblers preserve the bounded candidate universe and alternatives.
- Reference projectors make native items addressable without changing standing.
- Selection-basis producers may produce exact evidence, policy, comparison, or warranted dependency testimony.
- Selectors consume only the standing their local road permits.
- Downstream consumers revalidate the selected standing for their own purpose.

## Critical-path testimony relation

The local relation supported by the Book and implementation is:

```text
downstream constitutional act required
+ upstream standing required by that act
+ standing currently unresolved
+ owner refuses movement without it
+ no represented lawful bypass
→ locally dominating dependency testimony
```

This is dependency testimony or pressure, not automatic selection basis. It can contribute to selection only if a local selection-basis responsibility establishes that the testimony is applicable to the bounded goal and horizon, compares it against the candidate set or applies a policy, and reaches sufficiency or warrant for selecting one candidate.

Answers to the critical-path questions:

- **Does locally dominating dependency testimony constitute selection evidence?** Unknown globally; not automatically. It may become selection evidence only through a local producer/selector that says so.
- **Does it require applicability judgment?** Yes under Book grammar if it is to be consumed by a selector.
- **Does it require comparison against the bounded goal and horizon?** Locally yes when the purpose is one bounded goal and horizon; otherwise the pressure is not bounded to the selection act.
- **Does it require sufficiency or warrant before one need may be selected?** Yes, unless the local road defines exact admitted evidence itself as sufficient. Current advancement-need road does that only for exact reference focus, not for dependency pressure.
- **Can several locally dominating dependencies coexist?** Yes. Current advancement need grammar preserves coexisting needs as unordered and does not prove singular dominance.
- **What preserves lawful non-selection when no basis distinguishes one candidate?** The selector must preserve no-selection, missing identity, ambiguity, conflict, mismatch, absent, or non-selectable states plus non-selected alternatives rather than select by order, uniqueness, or pressure vocabulary.

## Seed self-orientation topology

For Seed as the intended consumer, the constitutional expectation is:

```text
current bounded goal and horizon
→ current advancement-need candidate set
→ current dependency standing
→ candidate lawful selection bases
→ basis applicability / admission / sufficiency / warrant
→ consideration-selection act or lawful non-selection
→ selected-for-consideration standing
→ downstream frontier/work road
→ reprojection after new testimony or standing
```

Seed should not assume self-orientation requires autonomous execution. Depending on current standing, it may lawfully:

- preserve non-selection when no applicable basis distinguishes one need;
- request or await missing testimony;
- apply an existing policy if one is present and applicable;
- perform exact comparison if a responsible comparison act exists;
- produce selection evidence if a responsible evidence-formation act exists;
- select one need for consideration only after the local basis is applicable and sufficient or warranted.

Current evidence supports exact-reference external direction as one road. Current evidence does not show Seed producing an internal policy/comparison/warranted dependency selection basis from its own dependency standing for the initial advancement-need consideration-selection act.

## Independent producer recovery

### Candidate and dependency producers

Native family projection producers establish family-local advancement need standing for clarification, inquiry, authority, and operational realization. Their purpose is bounded by the selected goal and horizon. They preserve established, unsupported, unknown, conflicting, excluded, outside-current-scope, or unclassified alternatives. They do not select one need for consideration, rank needs, select a route, select the next action, authorize work, execute, record, write the event ledger, or mutate cluster state.

`GoalAdvancementNeedSet` preserves these native projections as an unordered bounded set. `AdvancementNeedReferenceSet` exposes exact native references and marks established native records selectable, but neither produces the constitutional reason to choose one selectable record now.

### Selection-basis producer

Current evidence shows an external exact-reference road: `NeedFocusEvidence` can carry exact reference testimony. The selector validates that testimony against the reference set and can select exactly one selectable reference.

Current evidence does **not** show an internal responsibility producing:

- exact selection evidence from Seed's own dependency standing;
- applicable advancement-need selection policy standing;
- exact comparison standing against bounded purpose and horizon;
- locally dominating dependency standing declared suitable for selection;
- sufficiency for dependency-based consideration selection;
- a distinct selection warrant for choosing one advancement need from dependency pressure.

Absence of a `NeedFocusEvidence` producer alone is not the conclusion. The broader absence is that no repository-evidenced responsibility converts Seed-owned dependency standing into any local lawful selection basis of the permitted plural forms.

## Independent selector recovery

The current consideration selector consumes `AdvancementNeedReferenceSet` plus iterable `NeedFocusEvidence`. It validates:

- presence of evidence;
- evidence state (`exact_reference`, `missing_identity`, `ambiguous`, `conflict`, `Unknown`);
- a single named reference id;
- need set, selected goal, goal establishment, horizon, family, native projection, and native lineage bindings;
- existence of the reference in the visible set;
- duplicate-lineage conflicts;
- selectability of the reference.

It does not validate or own:

- formation of focus evidence;
- whether the external subject was right to focus the candidate;
- comparison against the bounded goal and horizon beyond identity binding;
- policy applicability;
- dependency dominance;
- sufficiency or warrant for a dependency-based selection;
- downstream inquiry frontier admissibility;
- route, next action, or authorization.

Its standing output is `AdvancementNeedConsiderationSelection`: selected reference when the exact-reference road succeeds, otherwise lawful non-selection preserving no-focus, missing, ambiguous, conflict, mismatch, absent, duplicate, non-selectable, unknown, conflict, visible, and non-selected references.

## Producer-to-selector cross-examination

The road is faithful for external direction because exact incoming reference testimony is the selection evidence consumed by the selector, and the selector revalidates identity and selectability against the bounded candidate set. It is incomplete for Seed self-orientation because dependency producers and reference projectors produce candidates and dependency visibility, not a basis for choosing one candidate.

Incoming exact-reference testimony is sufficient only within this selector because an external or caller-side subject has already supplied the focus. The selector verifies exact identity and selectability; it does not establish that the external subject's reason is applicable or wise beyond the selector-local exact-reference road. Admission is present only as selector-local acceptance of exact focus evidence into the selection act; a separate named admission artifact is absent and appears unnecessary for the current external exact-reference road. For an internal dependency-pressure road, admission/applicability/sufficiency/warrant remain missing or Unknown.

No current surface was found that silently converts dependency visibility into selection basis without warrant. The stronger issue is absence: critical-path or dependency projections remain testimony/visibility unless a local selection-basis owner exists.

## Fidelity findings

Direct answers:

- **What lawful basis does the current selector actually consume?** Exact reference focus testimony naming one visible selectable advancement-need reference in the current reference set.
- **Is incoming exact-reference testimony sufficient only because an external subject already chose the focus?** Yes for this road. The selector consumes the already-nominated exact reference and validates it; it does not originate the focus.
- **Does the selector merely verify identity, or does it also establish applicability?** It verifies identity, binding, evidence-state shape, presence, duplicate conflict, and selectability. It establishes selector-local applicability only for exact-reference testimony as consumable by this selector; it does not establish broader policy/comparison/dependency applicability.
- **Is an admission act present, absent, or unnecessary for this bounded selection road?** A separate admission artifact is absent. For the current external exact-reference road it appears unnecessary because selector-local validation and consumption are the local admission equivalent. For Seed self-orientation via dependency pressure, whether admission is required remains Unknown, and the upstream applicability/sufficiency/warrant acts are absent.
- **Does any current surface convert dependency visibility into selection basis without warrant?** No improper conversion was found. Current surfaces refuse that crossing.
- **Can Seed form a lawful basis from its own dependency standing?** Not currently shown.
- **If not, what exact constitutional act is missing?** A local selection-basis formation act that consumes current bounded candidate/dependency standing and produces an applicable and sufficient/warranted basis for selecting one established advancement need, or preserving non-selection when no basis distinguishes one.
- **Is the missing act evidence formation, applicability, admission, sufficiency, warrant, comparison, policy, or another repository-evidenced responsibility?** Current evidence most strongly supports: missing **selection-basis formation** with at least applicability plus sufficiency/warrant; if the chosen route is comparison, then an exact comparison act is missing; if the chosen route is policy, an applicable policy projection is missing. The Book does not force one route.

## Conclusion

The current implementation is faithful for external direction but incomplete for self-orientation.

More exactly: exact-reference testimony is a lawful basis for externally directed advancement-need consideration selection in the current road. Seed currently produces candidate and dependency standing, and it transports that standing through a need set and reference set, but it does not currently produce an applicable internal selection basis from its own dependency standing. The missing repository-evidenced act is a local selection-basis formation act with applicability and sufficiency/warrant discipline, optionally realized as policy or exact comparison if Book and implementation evidence later support that route.
