## Recurring constitutional evidence

### 1. Frontier pressure is not recovery

Recent repository work repeatedly reduces large attractive concepts into smaller invariants. The recent sequence named in the task is consistent with the repository's older discipline:

- goal was reduced to pressure;
- acceptance was reduced to family-local admission;
- work over time was reduced to durable boundary preservation;
- artifact availability was reduced to bounded reachability-without-promotion;
- constitutional proprioception was reduced to grammar-bounded orientation;
- bounded constitutional investigation was reduced to bounded inquiry admission discipline;
- implementation projection was reduced to evidence-bound implementation comparison;
- implementation pressure was reduced to boundary-insufficiency discrimination under preserved constitutional limits.

This pattern matters because a visible phrase at the frontier does not become a recoverable constitutional participant merely because it is attractive, repeated, or adjacent to prior work.

### 2. Grammar-bounded orientation is already recovered as the smaller invariant

`constitutional_proprioception_characterization.md` explicitly refused to recover `constitutional proprioception` as a standalone participant and recovered `grammar-bounded orientation` instead. Its app visibility likewise treated `constitutional proprioception` as not preserved in the knowledge-reachability audit. That recovery provides a strong precedent: self-description-like frontier phrases should be reduced to the boundary work they actually perform, if any.

For the current pressures, this means `realization`, `equilibrium`, `learning`, and `forgetting` should not be promoted unless repository evidence shows a smaller recurring boundary work that is not already covered.

### 3. Bounded inquiry admission supplies the route from pressure to future work

`bounded_inquiry_recovery_characterization.md` treats broad architectural possibility as recoverable only after reduction to a bounded repository investigation. It refuses promotion from recurrence, pressure, operator excitement, vocabulary, or accumulated reports into implementation, authority, or runtime capability.

Therefore this survey may recommend at most one future bounded investigation only if a pressure is specific enough to ask a repository-answerable question and recurring enough to avoid novelty-driven speculation.

### 4. Implementation projection and implementation pressure are already narrowed

`constitutional_implementation_projection_frontier_survey.md` refused to recover implementation projection, self-modification, code generation, architecture synthesis, autonomous planning, and an Eye. It recovered the smaller work of projecting recovered grammar onto implementation artifacts as evidence-bound comparison without promoting implementation artifacts into constitutional authority.

`constitutional_implementation_pressure_characterization.md` then narrowed implementation pressure further: comparison becomes pressure only when it exposes evidence-bound boundary insufficiency after compatibility, locality, family-local acceptance, preserved Unknown, and lawful stop are checked. That is already a mature recovery and should not be reopened here under the larger label `constitutional realization pressure`.

### 5. Pressure is visibility before command

`constitutional_pressure_goal_boundary_frontier_survey.md` separates diagnostic pressure from priority, course selection, and goal authority. Pressure can expose attention-worthy evidence and recommend inspection, but it does not authorize mutation, planning, scheduling, fact creation, or constitutional movement by itself.

This is the governing boundary for the present survey. The surveyed frontier pressures may orient attention. They do not by themselves justify implementation, architecture, a planner, an ontology, or another constitutional family.

### 6. Cross-family pressure recurrence is stronger than another layer

`architectural_coordinate_system_investigation.md` found stronger evidence for recurring pressure-like questions than for a single new hierarchy or layer. It also warned that specific compressed names such as continuity, evidence, locality, and self-observation are only candidate compressions; repository evidence more directly supports precise pressures such as evidence before movement, visibility not authority, diagnostic output not truth, pressure not recovery, answer not mutation, attachment not participation, and frontier not implementation readiness.

This evidence suggests that a newly visible frontier pressure is more likely to be a local pressure pattern than a new constitutional participant.
