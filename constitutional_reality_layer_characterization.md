# Constitutional Reality Layer Characterization

## Scope and discipline

This characterization investigates whether recurring repository evidence supports a constitutional distinction among:

```text
Objective Reality
↓
Constitutional Reality
↓
Subjective Reality
```

The distinction is treated as investigative orientation only. This document does not propose implementation, runtime layers, processing pipelines, federation architecture, trust systems, ontology layers, or new constitutional families. Repository authority wins.

## Objective Reality characterization

### Recurring responsibilities

Repository evidence supports a bounded responsibility for what this investigation calls Objective Reality: it is the external source-side condition, report, artifact, communication, sample, document, operator statement, provider output, or foreign Seed output that can become available to Seed.

Recurring responsibilities visible in the repository evidence:

1. **Provide source material or source assertions.** Operational sources, structured sources, document sources, operators, providers, local platform APIs, files, Prometheus, Ansible inventory content, and foreign Seeds can supply observations, payloads, records, reports, samples, claims, or testimony.
2. **Remain external to Seed's constitutional representation.** External material may be reported, sampled, imported, inspected, transferred, or observed, but repository evidence repeatedly refuses to treat the external condition itself as already Seed state.
3. **Carry vantage, source, clock, and boundary limits.** Provider observations, source sample times, operator statements, foreign claims, and external occurrence times recur as scoped by source path, vantage point, collection path, authority, and clock reliability.
4. **Generate uncertainty and failures.** Unknown file bytes, malformed provider responses, unsupported formats, unavailable observations, foreign assertions, stale samples, and contradictory reports can all appear before Seed has enough support for stronger representation.

### Recurring boundaries

- External occurrence is not observation time, preservation time, knowledge time, diagnostic elapsed time, or projected current state.
- Provider output is not automatically fact, truth, or current belief.
- Operator communication is evidence-bearing input, not automatically repository truth.
- A foreign Seed's output is not local observation or local truth by crossing the boundary.
- Similar external content is not automatically shared event identity.

### Recurring non-responsibilities

Objective Reality does not, by itself:

- preserve Seed provenance;
- decide Seed authority;
- normalize claims into Seed facts;
- produce current lawful condition;
- resolve contradictions;
- prove truth;
- select read-model output;
- create diagnostic or report interpretation;
- erase unknowns by being observed.

### Confidence

**Medium-high.** The responsibility recurs across source intake, time characterization, provider translation, knowledge/evidence documentation, and federation reconciliation. Confidence is not high because the phrase "Objective Reality" is not itself a stabilized repository term and must remain an investigative label.

## Constitutional Reality characterization

### Recurring responsibilities

Repository evidence supports a middle responsibility: Seed's constitutional representation preserves, normalizes, warrants, scopes, and explains claims without equating representation with truth.

Recurring responsibilities visible in repository evidence:

1. **Preserve observations and evidence with provenance.** Evidence preserves source, payload or normalized payload reference, observed time, source type, confidence or source quality where available, and links to observations, facts, or source facts.
2. **Normalize claims into represented facts.** Facts are normalized claims backed by evidence. Promotion into a fact means Seed has represented a claim in its vocabulary, not that Seed verified external truth.
3. **Aggregate support and contradiction without erasure.** Fact support aggregation groups compatible support, preserves conflicting facts, and exposes current belief through support, confidence, source type, freshness, and recency rather than silent averaging.
4. **Maintain authority, warrant, confidence, unknowns, and negative authority.** Constitutional work preserves evidence sufficiency, non-promotion, unsupported conclusions, bounded unknowns, confidence limits, and authority boundaries.
5. **Preserve current lawful condition without making every current status a fact.** Current lawful condition can include Null, unknown, evidence sufficiency, unsupported candidates, bounded unknowns, lawful stop, frontier status, selected concern, and completion status.
6. **Keep provenance and inference separate.** Provenance records where knowledge came from; inference records deterministic derivation; explanation renders a traversal of already-projected support.
7. **Prevent diagnostics and visibility from mutating cluster truth.** Diagnostic findings are bounded by record scope, event-ledger/mutation flags, and inventory/shape-audit visibility rather than becoming unscoped cluster truth.

### Recurring boundaries

- Observation preservation is not truth proof.
- Evidence support is not fact normalization by itself.
- Fact promotion is not current-state selection, verification, or external truth.
- Projection/current belief is not the only preserved interpretation of evidence.
- Warrant, confidence, support, and authority are not interchangeable.
- Unknown preservation is not resolution.
- Diagnostic recording is not cluster mutation unless explicitly operational.

### Recurring non-responsibilities

Constitutional Reality does not, by itself:

- create external reality;
- make a provider report true;
- make imported foreign output local observation;
- collapse trust into authority;
- collapse provenance into trust;
- turn a projection export into evidence export;
- decide consumer presentation vocabulary merely because a label is rendered;
- require every fact to be independently corroborated where the claim strength is directly supported by one scoped observation.

### Confidence

**High** for the recurring responsibility of preserving and normalizing evidence-backed representation without conflating it with truth. **Medium** for naming that responsibility "Constitutional Reality," because that name is investigative and not recovered as a repository-stabilized term.

## Subjective Reality characterization

### Recurring responsibilities

Repository evidence supports a consumer-side responsibility: reports, diagnostics, read models, projections, explanations, answer composition, operator views, and planning/decision context interpret already-preserved constitutional material for a specific consumer or surface.

Recurring responsibilities visible in repository evidence:

1. **Project and select for a view.** Projections deterministically read over preserved observations, evidence, facts, relationships, support records, conflicts, and projected state to answer view-specific questions.
2. **Compose context for consumers.** Context views assemble projected State, Evidence Graph, contradictions, confidence, projected issues, requirements, capabilities, and summary counts for decision-provider consumption without executing providers or mutating State.
3. **Render explanations and reports.** Explanation traverses already-projected support, conflicts, alias resolution, provenance, and inference; diagnostics and operational reports render visibility into local app behavior and boundaries.
4. **Expose ambiguity, gaps, and unknowns.** Public surfaces may render unknown dictionaries, unsupported targets, blocked observations, contradictions, stale facts, missing evidence, and limitations without resolving them.
5. **Remain surface-specific.** Diagnostic payloads, read-model rows, answer navigation, projection-shape views, current-facts output, and operational-story outputs are consumer-specific compositions rather than new truth systems.

### Recurring boundaries

- Projection is a read view, not the only preserved interpretation of evidence.
- Context views are read-only and do not execute runtime behavior, invoke providers, mutate State, append events, replay the ledger, or create persistence.
- Explanation renders why projected belief is current; it does not add a reasoning mechanism or external command execution.
- Diagnostic visibility and presentation labels do not automatically become repository knowledge.
- Projection export is not evidence export unless support and provenance survive.

### Recurring non-responsibilities

Subjective Reality does not, by itself:

- observe the external world;
- create constitutional warrant;
- promote claims into facts;
- establish authority;
- make selected output true;
- mutate cluster state when declared read-only;
- erase provenance, contradiction, or unknowns;
- make presentation vocabulary constitutional law.

### Confidence

**Medium-high.** Read models, projections, diagnostics, explanation, and context views repeatedly show consumer-specific interpretation. Confidence is not high because "Subjective Reality" is not repository vocabulary and because not every consumer surface may belong to one family.

## Supported transitions

## Objective Reality to Constitutional Reality

Recurring repository evidence supports a transition from external/source-side material into Seed's constitutional representation, but only as bounded preservation, inspection, translation, observation, evidence, fact representation, or imported testimony.

### Preserved transformations

- Raw file or provider input can become an inspected input artifact, provider-language candidate, provider-specific parsed record, observation, evidence, and fact.
- Provider output can become source-attributed observation and then evidence-backed fact when shape and claim strength allow.
- Operator statements can become evidence-bearing observed facts or desired-configuration declarations rather than direct truth.
- Documents and structured sources can produce evidence; extracted claims may become facts only with validation and provenance.
- Foreign Seed output can become imported report, foreign testimony evidence, imported claim, mirrored state, or scoped accepted claim, but not automatically local truth.
- External occurrence or source sample timing can be preserved as provenance while remaining distinct from observation, preservation, knowledge, diagnostic, and projection timing.

### Preserved authority

- Authority is scoped and not created by source existence, trust, import, or projection export.
- Provider/source authority can support claims only within the scope that the repository's evidence and local governance allow.
- Foreign authority must be explicitly accepted within scope and still preserve foreign provenance.
- Fact promotion cannot exceed the claim strength directly supported by evidence.

### Preserved provenance

- Source, source type, payload, observed time, confidence, evidence IDs, supporting facts, inference rules, source fact IDs, and import/transfer paths recur as preserved provenance.
- Cross-Seed provenance includes originating Seed identity, source observation/evidence/event/claim identity, evidence chain, transfer path, export/import time, transformation history, trust context, authority context, and integrity context when available.
- Provenance remains separate from trust and authority.

### Preserved unknowns

- Unknown format, binary/empty source, unsupported provider contract, missing evidence, unsupported candidate, insufficient evidence, blocked observation, unavailable projected detail, unknown family, and unknown current condition remain visible rather than converted into facts.
- Unknowns may be internally typed and later projected to public compatibility shapes without resolving them.

### Confidence

**High** that external/source-side material transitions into Seed representation only through scoped observation/evidence/fact/import paths with provenance. **Medium** that this exactly maps to the names Objective Reality and Constitutional Reality.

## Constitutional Reality to Subjective Reality

Recurring repository evidence supports a transition from preserved constitutional representation to consumer-specific interpretations, reports, diagnostics, projections, read models, explanations, and context views.

### Recurring projections

- State projection derives current facts, support aggregates, recent measurements, alias/identity indexes, relationship edges, entity types, graph validation findings, and explanation inputs from the event ledger.
- Projection and read-model surfaces answer current-facts, why, impact, unhealthy/down, relationships, entity types, decision-context, projection-shape, and diagnostic visibility questions.
- Fact support aggregation and current belief select representative support without deleting conflicting evidence.
- Diagnostic projections expose operational shape, timing, cache, visibility, and inventory boundaries.

### Recurring consumer-specific interpretation

- Operators receive summaries, reports, explanations, current views, and diagnostics.
- Decision providers receive Context Views rather than direct State traversal.
- Answer composition and navigation surfaces consume preserved support and render bounded answer material.
- Projection exports, mirrors, and reports can communicate selected current views while remaining distinct from evidence export or authority.

### Preserved constitutional invariants

- Read-only projections do not append events or mutate State.
- Unsupported facts can be excluded by default while contradictions remain visible when retained.
- Explanation remains traversal over existing projected support and inference.
- Diagnostics preserve record/mutation boundaries.
- Projection output must not hide that evidence, provenance, contradiction, authority, and unknowns are preserved elsewhere.

### Confidence

**Medium-high.** The transition recurs strongly, but the repository treats many surfaces as local owners rather than one unified subjective family.

## Unsupported transitions

Repository evidence does not support these transitions:

- Objective Reality directly becomes constitutional truth.
- Provider output directly becomes verified fact without evidence, scope, provenance, and claim-strength discipline.
- Operator communication directly becomes repository truth.
- Foreign Seed output arrives as local constitutional truth.
- Projection output becomes evidence export merely by being exported.
- Mirrored state becomes shared truth.
- Shared content becomes shared event identity without stronger evidence.
- Diagnostic output becomes cluster truth or host/service/filesystem truth by being recorded.
- Presentation vocabulary becomes repository knowledge merely by recurring in rendered surfaces.
- Subjective/report/read-model output creates constitutional warrant by being visible to a consumer.

## Preserved invariants

1. Observation is source-attributed and does not prove external truth.
2. Evidence preserves why a claim is available for consideration.
3. Facts normalize evidence-backed claims without implying verification.
4. Projection selects, summarizes, ranks, suppresses, or explains views without becoming the only preserved interpretation.
5. Provenance, trust, authority, confidence, support, and truth remain distinct.
6. Unknowns remain visible when evidence is insufficient or a boundary is unsupported.
7. Diagnostic visibility remains bounded and non-mutating unless explicitly operational.
8. Cross-Seed import preserves foreign provenance and does not erase local/foreign support paths.
9. Federation is a communication/governance boundary, not automatic authority or shared truth.
10. Constitutional language can be authoritative repository representation without being equivalent to constitutional truth.

## Federated Seed pressure test

The proposition:

```text
Federated Seed output
arrives as
constitutional truth.
```

is not supported by recurring repository evidence.

The stronger recurring boundary is:

```text
Federated Seed output arrives as foreign testimony, imported report, imported claim, projection export, mirrored state, foreign evidence, or scoped authority claim depending on what crossed the boundary and what provenance survived.
```

Recurring evidence supports:

- **External testimony:** A foreign Seed's claim is testimony unless locally promoted or accepted under scoped authority.
- **External evidence:** Seed-to-Seed sharing is evidence transfer, not truth transfer.
- **Constitutional observation/import:** The receiving Seed may preserve a local import record that Seed A reported, claimed, projected, exported, or preserved something.
- **Constitutional warrant:** Local warrant may arise only through local authority, support, provenance, verification, or scoped acceptance; it does not arrive automatically with the foreign output.
- **Another recurring boundary:** Projection export, mirrored state, shared event, imported event report, imported claim, shared state, federation, trust, authority, identity, and verification are explicitly non-collapsible.

Confidence is **high** that federation does not transfer truth by default and **medium-high** that the recurring positive boundary is foreign testimony/evidence/import with preserved provenance and optional local promotion.

## Preserved unknowns

- Whether the repository should ever stabilize the names Objective Reality, Constitutional Reality, and Subjective Reality remains unknown.
- Whether every consumer-facing projection, diagnostic, report, answer, recommendation, and planning surface belongs to one recurring Subjective Reality family remains unknown.
- Whether every constitutional responsibility listed here has one implementation owner remains unknown; current evidence often supports local owners and local surfaces.
- Whether future federation implementation will introduce additional import statuses remains unknown.
- Whether constitutional warrant, current lawful condition, fact support, and promotion should be characterized under one broader repository family remains unknown.
- Whether all unknown-producing surfaces should share typed unknown preservation remains unknown.
- Whether all source-side external inputs can be uniformly classified before provider/source selection remains unknown.

## Confidence

Overall confidence is **medium-high**.

The repository strongly supports non-collapse among external source material, Seed's evidence-backed constitutional representation, and consumer-specific projections or reports. It also strongly supports that constitutional representation is authoritative repository language without being equivalent to truth.

Confidence is not high because the three proposed layer names are not recovered as established repository vocabulary, the repository often favors local implementation-backed owners over broad families, and the investigation remains observational.

Subjective Reality uniquely carries consumer-specific interpretation: projections, read models, diagnostics, reports, explanations, context views, answers, recommendations, and operator-facing surfaces. Its constitutional responsibility is to render or select from preserved constitutional representation for a consumer while preserving invariants, boundaries, provenance, contradictions, and unknowns, and without creating truth or authority by presentation.
