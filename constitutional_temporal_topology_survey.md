## Topology reconciliation

### Recurring neighboring positions

Recurring evidence supports the following neighboring positions around temporal constitutional relationships:

1. **Temporal source/provenance positions:** external occurrence, provider/source sample assertion, Seed observation/collection, Seed preservation/event-ledger time, knowledge/justification time, temporal claim/revision time, projection/report/diagnostic visibility time.
2. **Lawful-use positions:** evidence binding, warrant, current lawful condition, continued lawful reliance, confidence, contradiction, negative authority, stop condition, unknown preservation.
3. **Derived-output positions:** event history, projection, read model, reasoning path, derivation, answer composition, report/diagnostic/view.

These are neighboring positions, not one collapsed Time object.

### Recurring bridges

The recurring bridges are evidence-preserving movements:

- **Provider/source material to observation/evidence:** provider-language translation carries source-local assertions, including timestamps where present, without converting them into occurrence truth.
- **Observation/evidence to warrant:** evidence binding, provenance, support, authority boundary, negative authority, unknown preservation, and confidence make bounded reliance lawful.
- **Preserved history to projection/read model:** projection derives current views from preserved material while remaining read/view-bounded.
- **Support path to derivation/reasoning path:** derivations and reasoning paths expose how later outputs depend on prior preserved material.
- **Warrant/current condition to continued reliance:** continued reliance remains lawful only while the present bounded condition and its warrant survive; otherwise movement stops or remains unknown.
- **Answer composition from bounded inquiry:** answers compose selected evidence, derivation, warrant, negative authority, unknowns, and confidence without becoming new evidence by presentation alone.

### Recurring streets

The recurring streets are not roads owned by a temporal district. They are lawful movement paths:

1. **Source/provider temporal assertion -> translated observation/evidence -> temporal claim.**
2. **Observation/evidence -> warrant -> current lawful condition -> bounded reliance.**
3. **Preserved event history -> projection/read model -> reasoning path -> answer composition.**
4. **New evidence or contradiction -> lawful transition -> revised current lawful condition or stop.**
5. **Diagnostic/report/view output -> bounded record or inquiry candidate only when recording authority exists; otherwise presentation remains local.**

### Recurring temporal boundaries

- Provider/source time is not observation time.
- Observation time is not occurrence time.
- Preservation time is not observation time or occurrence time.
- Knowledge/justification time is not ledger preservation time.
- Projection/current-view time is not preserved truth or external current reality.
- Diagnostic elapsed, progress cadence, and cache visibility are operational visibility, not constitutional readiness.
- Temporal claim/revision time is a supported claim boundary, not raw timestamp authority.
- Continued reliance is not timeless; it is bounded by warrant, current lawful condition, evidence sufficiency, authority, confidence, contradictions, and preserved unknowns.

## Answer to the core question

According to recurring repository evidence, the recurring constitutional topology surrounding temporal constitutional relationships is a non-collapsing neighborhood of source/provider temporal assertions, Seed observation, Seed preservation, temporal claim support, projection/read-model derivation, current lawful condition, warrant, continued lawful reliance, stop conditions, and preserved unknowns.

Its bridges are not timers or schedulers. They are evidence-preserving lawful movements: provider/source material to observation/evidence, observation/evidence to warrant, preserved history to projection/read model, support path to derivation/reasoning path, warrant/current lawful condition to bounded continued reliance, and bounded inquiry to answer composition.

Its streets are lawful transition paths in which temporal material may move only with preserved provenance, support, authority boundary, negative authority, confidence, contradiction state, and unknowns. The topology repeatedly refuses collapse among provider time, observation time, occurrence time, preservation time, knowledge time, projection time, diagnostic time, and temporal claim time. It also refuses to promote unsupported candidate handles into constitutional participants.
