## Topology reconciliation

### Recurring neighboring positions

Recurring neighboring positions between evidence and lawful downstream action are:

1. **Observation / recovered evidence** — bounded evidence-bearing material or preserved signal.
2. **Evidence binding / provenance** — attachment to support, source, role, authority, and confidence.
3. **Warrant** — evidence-bound lawful reliance under preserved authority boundary.
4. **Admissibility / promotion boundary** — local competent boundary where evidence may or may not enter a downstream constitutional role.
5. **Current lawful condition** — present bounded law-constrained status such as sufficient, insufficient, Null, selected, unresolved, complete, stopped, or frontier-only where supported.
6. **Reliance** — disciplined downstream use of warranted content inside role, scope, authority, unknown, and confidence limits.
7. **Lawful movement / transition / answer composition / stop** — downstream constitutional action or non-action, only where locally authorized.
8. **Unknown preservation and negative authority** — recurring boundary companion across the street, preventing unsupported promotion.

### Recurring bridges

- Observation-to-evidence bridge: preservation of bounded material with provenance.
- Evidence-to-warrant bridge: evidence binding, authority assessment, role preservation, confidence calibration, and negative authority.
- Warrant-to-admissibility bridge: competent local boundary decides whether evidence may be admitted, promoted, selected, or must remain candidate/unknown.
- Warrant-to-current-condition bridge: present lawful status may be stated only within evidence and authority boundaries.
- Current-condition-to-reliance bridge: downstream surfaces may consume a status only in its supported role.
- Reliance-to-action bridge: lawful movement, transition, answer composition, or stop occurs only within preserved warrant and local competence.
- Unknown/negative-authority bridge: unresolved, unsupported, insufficient, or forbidden promotions remain visible and can lawfully stop movement.

- Warrant is not syntax, provider representation, record class, translation mechanism, or implementation object.
