## Required-question findings
1. **Recurring kinds emerge from fields before names.** The recurring field combinations are: bounded ask status plus Question Family rows; registry-only diagnostic declarations; shape-audited implementation validation rows; repository-file structural readers; projected-state operational readers; and the two recordable diagnostic fact emitters. These are recurring because multiple surfaces preserve the same combinations of dispatch, inventory, state/repo-file use, JSON/record support, ledger behavior, and validation.
2. **Distinguishing characteristics.** Bounded ask/question rows preserve `question_family`, examples, `answer_responsibility`, `authority_boundary`, `bounded_status`, required args, dispatch surface, formatter, and JSON support. Diagnostic rows preserve operational shape fields: projected-state use, repo-file use, record support/scope, fact emission, ledger writes, mutation, diagnostic-fact reads, flags, and descriptions. Shape audit specs preserve module path, build/format/json/record functions, CLI flags, markers, and mutation markers.
3. **Question Families span multiple recurring kinds.** Most current Question Families are bounded ask surfaces, but two are diagnostic-only (`surface inventory`, `surface shape validation`) and two are not dispatchable (`source definition/import lookup`, `inquiry orientation`). Therefore the current Question Surface Inventory does not represent only one executable kind.
4. **Surfaces outside the Question Surface Inventory are not merely miscellaneous.** Outside-QSI diagnostics recur as projected-state readers, repo-file structural readers, registry/classification audits, graph/taxonomy/confidence views, history/reference/pressure/correlation views, and recordable diagnostic observations. Their shared diagnostic inventory fields and shape audit validation make them recurring implementation-backed kinds rather than arbitrary exclusions.
5. **Bounded `ask` participates only in one cluster plus parameterized variants.** Bounded ask is represented by `eligible_now` and `eligible_with_parameters` statuses in the Question Surface Inventory. It does not cover diagnostic-only, not-dispatchable, or most diagnostic-inventory-only surfaces.
6. **Answer responsibility differs by kind.** Question Family rows preserve answer responsibility as operator/repository questions. Diagnostic rows preserve description and operational shape declarations, not `answer_responsibility`. Shape audit rows answer whether declarations match observed implementation shape.
7. **Authority behavior differs by kind.** Question rows preserve prose authority boundaries. Diagnostic rows preserve booleans for ledger writes, mutation, diagnostic fact emission, record scope, and diagnostic-fact reads.
