## Supported answer

According to recurring repository evidence, **Reliance** is the constitutional use-status that follows when a bounded downstream consumer may treat preserved content as lawfully usable for a specific role without promoting that content beyond its evidence, authority boundary, negative authority, unknowns, and confidence limit.

In bounded form:

```text
Constitutional Reliance is the lawful downstream use of warranted content
within the preserved authority boundary.
```

Reliance is therefore not the same thing as expression and not the same thing as warrant.

```text
Expression carries candidate content.
Constitutional Warrant authorizes bounded lawful reliance.
Constitutional Reliance is the bounded lawful use that warrant permits.
```

Reliance occupies the receiving side of warrant. Warrant is the preserved entitlement and limit; reliance is the constitutionally disciplined act or condition of consuming the warranted content only in the role, scope, and confidence that repository evidence permits.

## Identity

```text
What constitutional thing

is Reliance?
```

Reliance is a **constitutional consumption discipline**.

It is the recurring role by which a downstream surface may use evidence-bound content after the repository has preserved enough warrant to make that use lawful. Reliance is visible wherever repository evidence distinguishes what a consumer may lawfully do with content from what it must refuse to do with the same content.

Recurring evidence supports this identity because:

- warrant is repeatedly characterized as evidence-bound lawful reliance under preserved authority boundary;
- lawful translation preserves warranted content that remains lawful for its downstream consumer;
- Observation Agreement may carry agreement evidence forward while refusing truth promotion, grammar ownership, mutation authority, semantic authority, and responsibility assignment;
- Responsibility Evaluation may rely on implementation evidence, counterexamples, authority boundaries, missing visibility, reasons, unknowns, and confidence only to classify responsibility-like claims, not to assign responsibility beyond evidence;
- Typed Unknown Preservation makes unresolved conditions part of the reliance boundary rather than defects to infer through;
- Constitutional Inquiry State and Self-Awareness show that inquiry artifacts may know what they may say, what they may not say, where they must stop, and what remains unknown;
- Lawful Bounded-Continuation Judgment depends on reliance limits, stop conditions, unknown preservation, negative authority, and confidence calibration to decide whether another lawful move remains;
- Family Completion Audits rely on scoped implementation-backed completion evidence while refusing whole-domain completion.

Reliance is therefore not a new content item, representation, source, or mechanism. It is the lawful status of downstream use when preserved warrant permits use and preserved negative authority forbids overuse.

## Inputs

```text

```

Recurring repository evidence supports these conditions as permitting lawful reliance:

1. **Repository evidence binding.** Reliance is lawful only when the content remains attached to repository evidence, implementation evidence, tests, diagnostics, generated architecture evidence, app behavior, prior bounded investigations, audits, or completion evidence where those are the relevant authorities.

2. **Preserved constitutional warrant.** The content must carry the evidence-bound entitlement and lawful limit that warrant supplies.

3. **Bounded subject or content identity.** The selected subject, relation, value, agreement, unknown, inquiry condition, responsibility-like claim, continuation condition, or completion conclusion must remain within the identity and resolution supported by evidence.

4. **Provenance or support preservation.** The consumer must retain enough support to inspect why the reliance is lawful.

5. **Role and consumer boundary.** The use must remain inside the role that the receiving surface is authorized to consume: observation, candidate agreement, evidence, inquiry condition, continuation judgment, responsibility classification, scoped completion, diagnostic visibility, or another repository-supported role.

6. **Preserved authority boundary.** Reliance must not expand content into truth, mutation, responsibility assignment, ownership, implementation permission, planning authority, execution authority, semantic authority, or whole-domain completion unless separately supported.

7. **Negative authority.** The content must preserve what it explicitly refuses to become, because reliance is lawful only when forbidden promotions remain forbidden.

8. **Unknown preservation.** Evidence gaps, unsupported conclusions, incomplete authority, boundary uncertainty, ambiguity, and unresolved states must remain visible rather than being silently resolved.

9. **Confidence or evidence-strength calibration where supported.** Reliance must remain limited to the strength of evidence carried by the source surface.

10. **Stop and non-promotion discipline.** When evidence ends, lawful reliance stops instead of inferring through the gap.

These inputs recur across translation, warrant, expression, evidence binding, observation agreement, responsibility evaluation, typed unknown preservation, inquiry state, inquiry self-awareness, bounded continuation, and family completion evidence.

## Relationship to Warrant


Constitutional Warrant and Constitutional Reliance form an authorization/use relationship.

```text
Warrant authorizes.
Reliance consumes within what warrant authorizes.
```

Warrant is the cross-district constitutional reliance invariant: the preserved evidence-bound entitlement and limit that allows content to remain lawfully relied upon across forms, neighborhoods, districts, and inquiry artifacts.

Reliance is what becomes lawful because warrant survives. It is the downstream use-status of content whose evidence, provenance, role, authority boundary, negative authority, unknowns, and confidence limits remain preserved.

The relationship is not mechanical, runtime, dependency-like, or trust-like. Warrant does not create a runtime reliance object. Reliance does not create warrant. Warrant supplies the constitutional permission and boundary; reliance is lawful only while it stays inside that permission and boundary.

## Relationship to Expression

```text
What relationship

exists between

Expression

and

Reliance?
```

Expression has a carrier relationship to reliance, but expression is not sufficient for reliance.

Expression can carry or expose candidate content to a bounded repository surface. Reliance becomes lawful only if that content has constitutional warrant: repository evidence, provenance/support, role, authority boundary, negative authority, unknown preservation, and confidence or evidence-strength limits must survive.

Therefore:

```text
Expression can make content available.
Warrant determines whether the content may be relied upon.
Reliance is the bounded lawful use of that content.
```

Expression may vary without changing reliance when the same lawful reliance profile survives. Expression changes or destroys lawful reliance when it changes subject identity, relation, value, provenance, authority, negative authority, unknowns, confidence, consumer role, or non-promotion boundary.

Mere expression does not permit reliance. Provider syntax, operator prose, repeated vocabulary, diagnostic visibility, presentation labels, report sections, courtroom-like language, physiology labels, or city-lens terms do not become lawful reliance by being visible or fluent.

## Synthesis

```text

```

Constitutional Warrant is insufficient without Constitutional Reliance because warrant names the preserved entitlement and limit, but the recovered organism also requires a disciplined downstream use-status that keeps consumers inside that entitlement and limit.

Recurring repository evidence does not merely ask whether content is preserved. It asks whether a bounded consumer may lawfully use that content without changing its role, expanding its authority, erasing negative authority, resolving unknowns by implication, overclaiming confidence, promoting agreement into truth, assigning responsibility without support, turning scoped completion into whole-domain completion, or continuing after evidence ends.

Warrant supplies the constitutional authorization:

```text

```

Reliance is necessary because the authorization must be consumed lawfully. Without Reliance, warrant would remain a preserved property with no characterized constitutional account of the downstream use it permits and limits. The organism would know what authorizes bounded lawful reliance, but not what constitutional role the relying itself occupies when observation, agreement, responsibility evaluation, inquiry state, continuation judgment, typed unknown preservation, and family completion actually consume warranted content.

Therefore, Constitutional Reliance is the constitutional consumption discipline that makes warrant operative without allowing warrant to become truth promotion, mutation authority, planning authority, implementation permission, trust framework, dependency system, or universal mechanism.
