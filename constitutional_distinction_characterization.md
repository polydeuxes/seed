## Recovered constitutional role

Across recurring constitutional recoveries, recovered distinctions consistently perform the role of **evidence-bound constitutional boundary preservation for lawful movement and reliance**.

In bounded form:

```text
Recovered distinctions are the recurring constitutional work by which repository evidence
separates adjacent participants, authorities, responsibilities, artifacts, capabilities,
movements, or boundaries so that each keeps only the authority and role it has earned,
unsupported promotion is refused, unknowns remain visible, lawful reliance stays bounded,
and later movement, handoff, characterization, or stop can occur without collapse.
```

The smallest supported answer is:

```text
Recovered distinctions preserve constitutional boundaries.
```

The fuller recurring answer is:

```text
Recovered distinctions preserve evidence-bound constitutional boundaries so that
movement and reliance remain lawful rather than collapsing observation into evidence,
evidence into fact, translation into promotion, producer into consumer, unknown into
failure, recurring product into canonical vocabulary, or frontier pressure into
implementation readiness.
```

This role includes several recurring subfunctions, but none should be promoted into a universal system:

1. **Prevent collapse.** Distinctions prevent one constitutional participant or condition from taking the role of another.
2. **Maintain authority boundaries.** Distinctions preserve what a surface, artifact, claim, competency, or movement may and may not do.
3. **Preserve locality.** Distinctions keep support family-local, provider-local, inquiry-local, diagnostic-local, or artifact-local where evidence requires locality.
4. **Support bounded lawful reliance.** Distinctions allow downstream use only within warrant, role, evidence, provenance, confidence, and negative authority limits.
5. **Enable lawful movement.** Distinctions make it possible to move from observation to inquiry, evidence to support, translation to bounded output, or pressure to frontier without skipping authority gates.
6. **Enable lawful stop.** Distinctions make unsupported, insufficient, unknown, frontier-only, or outside-authority outcomes successful stopping conditions rather than failures.
7. **Increase constitutional resolution.** Distinctions make constitutional recovery more precise without inventing ontology, implementation ownership, universal grammar, or canonical vocabulary.
