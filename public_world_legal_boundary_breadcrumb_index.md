## Breadcrumb chain overview

The recovered chain is:

```text
packet inclusion != evidence
source role != truth
source count != independence
legal authority != constitutional warrant
precedent chain != source-of-law lineage
boundary mismatch != invalidity
analytical competence != adjudicative authority
legal analysis artifact != legal advice
```

The chain composes by preserving increasingly strong stop points. Each artifact allows a narrower repository-visible statement while refusing a stronger promotion. The useful future role of the chain is review discipline: it helps Seed keep material inspectable, bounded, attributed, source-limited, confidence-limited, Unknown-preserving, and stopped before unsupported movement.

The chain does not compose into one broad slogan. It also does not create a public-world packet schema, source-role schema, source-of-law graph, scoring system, legal research system, validity engine, adjudication engine, advice engine, or implementation task.

## How the artifacts compose into a public-world packet review discipline

The public-world side of the chain composes as a pre-ingestion discipline:

1. Treat packet inclusion as selection/review context only.
2. Identify source roles only as warrant-bounding provenance roles.
3. Preserve claim form before using any material as support.
4. Preserve provenance, source identity, artifact identity, temporal provenance, support path, and confidence limits.
5. Separate direct evidence, background, claims about claims, vendor claims, advocacy reports, journalism, legal sources, and social-media repetition.
6. Treat source count as count until source independence is specifically supported.
7. Treat repetition, volume, popularity, consensus, and visibility as weak visibility facts unless competent local support makes them admissible for the exact claim.
8. Preserve contradiction, conflict, insufficiency, and Unknowns without choosing a preferred narrative.
9. Stop before ingestion unless a competent repository boundary admits the material.
10. Stop before truth adjudication unless a competent truth claim and support path exist.

This discipline allows Seed to say bounded things like `this packet contains a source-attributed claim` or `these materials repeat a claim`. It does not allow Seed to say `the claim is true`, `the source is authoritative for the target fact`, `the repeated claim is proof`, or `implementation should begin`.

## How the artifacts compose into a legal-source review discipline

The legal-source side of the chain composes as a source-limited discipline:

1. Treat legal sources as bounded artifacts with source identity, version, issuer/forum, jurisdiction, date, role, and authority limits.
2. Separate constitutional text, amendment text, ratification/adoption provenance, statutes, regulations, court opinions, holdings, dicta, reasoning, facts, procedural posture, commentary, doctrine history, and narrative.
3. Treat a legal authority as authority only within its preserved role, jurisdiction, time, forum, posture, and claim form.
4. Treat precedent chains and citation chains as dependency/provenance evidence, not independent source-of-law lineage.
5. Treat a court-described boundary as a source-attributed description, not a source-derived constitutional boundary.
6. Treat source-of-law gaps, mismatches, conflicts, and contradictions as analysis outputs, not legal-effect determinations.
7. Preserve negative authority, supersession, repeal, amendment, overruling, distinguishing, stays, injunctions, and other limiting authority when known; preserve Unknown when not reviewed.
8. Stop before legal validity, constitutional validity, enforceability, adjudicative authority, or advice unless separate competent authority and support are preserved.

This discipline allows Seed to preserve legal-source analysis as useful review material. It does not allow Seed to create a legal research engine, source-of-law graph, legal validity engine, constitutional validity engine, adjudication engine, or legal advice engine.

## Lawful stop points

### Stop before ingestion

Stop before ingestion when material is merely in a packet, visible in an artifact, selected by an operator, repeated across sources, source-attributed, or useful background. Ingestion would require a repository-supported boundary that admits the material into observations, facts, events, projected state, or ontology with the correct record scope, authority, and support.

### Stop before truth adjudication

Stop before truth adjudication when the support shows only source role, claim visibility, repetition, agreement, background, narrative coherence, count, volume, confidence, or contradiction. None of those is truth without competent claim-form support, provenance, source authority, independence/dependency handling, confidence calibration, contradiction review, and Unknown preservation.

### Stop before legal validity or constitutional validity

Stop before legal or constitutional validity when the evidence shows only legal authority, precedent dependency, citation recurrence, court description, source-of-law gap, mismatch, insufficiency, conflict, or analysis. Validity claims require separate source-of-law support, jurisdiction, temporal authority, procedural posture, competent authority, negative authority, confidence limits, and lawful stop.

### Stop before adjudicative authority

Stop before adjudicative authority when Seed has only analytical competence. Seed may identify and preserve support gaps, mismatch, boundary pressure, and Unknowns, but may not decide valid/invalid, constitutional/unconstitutional, legal/illegal, enforceable/unenforceable, binding, or decided without separately preserved adjudicative authority and output-surface authority.

### Stop before legal advice

Stop before legal advice when the artifact is general source analysis, packet review, or boundary synthesis. Advice-like movement would require audience, jurisdiction, governing law, relevant facts, professional role or advice authority, attorney-client/non-attorney boundary where relevant, current legal-source support, procedure, negative authority review, confidence limits, Unknown preservation, and output-surface authority.

### Stop before implementation pressure

Stop before implementation pressure when the evidence supports only constitutional projection-behavior constraints and manual-review discipline. Implementation pressure requires a concrete repository-visible failure or owner need; absent that, the lawful outcome is no implementation recommendation.

## Implementation-pressure activation conditions

Future implementation pressure would become lawful only if repository-visible evidence showed at least one concrete current condition, such as:

- a produced artifact family repeatedly fails to preserve source role, provenance, support path, claim form, authority boundary, confidence/evidence-strength limit, negative authority, Unknowns, or lawful stop;
- manual review becomes impossible for an existing supported consumer and the specific implementation owner is visible;
- an existing diagnostic, audit, probe, view, CLI flag, or recordable output misstates or hides one of these boundaries;
- a diagnostic inventory or diagnostic shape audit entry is missing or inaccurate for a changed diagnostic surface;
- event-ledger behavior records diagnostic findings as cluster truth or mutates cluster state contrary to boundary discipline;
- an existing ingestion/projection path actually promotes packets, source roles, counts, repetition, authority volume, legal authority, mismatch, analysis, or advice-like content into stronger repository truth;
- tests expose a concrete failure in a current source-review, world-ingestion, projection, or output path;
- a current consumer requires bounded fields and repository evidence identifies the narrow field, owner, behavior, and failure.

If such evidence appears, implementation must still be the smallest implementation-backed change that fixes the concrete failure and preserves diagnostic inventory / shape-audit obligations for any changed diagnostic surface.
