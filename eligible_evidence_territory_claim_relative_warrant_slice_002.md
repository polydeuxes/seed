## Why tuple presence was insufficient

A non-empty `eligible_evidence_territory_refs` tuple proves only that testimony supplied a string reference. It does not prove territory existence, territory eligibility, selected-need applicability, frontier-boundary applicability, reliance-purpose sufficiency, source/provenance support, currentness, availability, producer occurrence, or lack of material conflict. Family label, established standing, inquiry disposition, producer lineage, source lineage, evidence classes, provenance roles, and absence of explicit conflict are likewise not independently sufficient.
