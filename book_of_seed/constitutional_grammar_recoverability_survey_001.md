# Constitutional Grammar Recoverability Survey 001

## Method and scope

This survey uses the Book of Seed as constitutional testimony and does not inspect implementation acts except where the Book itself lists representative anchors. Those anchors are treated as illustrative witnesses only, not as sources of constitutional grammar. The provisional words supplied for the investigation are treated as search coordinates, not as constitutional kinds.

Maximum global conclusion: the Book preserves a strong negative and boundary grammar for many nearby phenomena, and directly preserves several local acts and standings. It does not preserve a single universal sequence named relevance -> applicability -> admissibility -> selection -> comparison -> sufficiency -> warrant -> authority -> movement, nor a universal evidence-uptake act.

## 2. Recovered distinctions and recoverability classifications

### 2.1 Constitutional grammar and external/source grammar

- Book-native phrase: external grammar; constitutional grammar; attributed source grammar; bounded translation; addressability without assimilation.
- Supporting clauses: external grammar may be identified, compared, and attributed without becoming Seed constitutional grammar; sufficient promotion warrant is unresolved. Translation must state source, scope, external context, translated term or claim, uncertainty, and authority limit. Addressable material must preserve source role or Unknown, provenance or Unknown, source grammar boundary, scope, uncertainty, confidence limits, and authority limits.
- Constitutional subject: boundary between grammar encountered in external material and grammar warranted for Seed reasoning.
- Act or relation: address, translate, compare, attribute, consume across a seam.
- Material consumed: provider vocabulary, external representations, source confidence labels, source-attributed material.
- Standing produced: addressable or translated material; possible applicable evidence or examination standing for bounded use.
- Scope: addressed or translated scope only.
- Authority consequences: no native constitutional grammar, cluster truth, repository authority, adopted law, or source-owner authority transfer without separate warrant.
- Forbidden inferences: lexical recurrence, usefulness, provider confidence, attachment, and convenience do not create constitutional authority.
- Unknown/stop conditions: sufficient promotion warrant remains unresolved; Unknown or unavailable provenance limits reliance.
- Neighboring distinctions: external source grammar differs from Book-native law; translation differs from adoption; addressability differs from standing.
- Clause type: direct plus negative and Unknown.
- Classification: **Directly recoverable** for source/native boundary and **Unknown** for promotion warrant.
- Alternative account: repeated external vocabulary could become repository grammar by recurrence. The Book rules this out for authority because lexical recurrence is expressly non-authoritative, but it does not resolve the positive promotion path.

## 3. Separate finding: constitutional `state`

The Book does establish a recoverable constitutional phenomenon behind some state-like language: bounded repository-governed current constitutional standing produced by replay/reconciliation/projection from records and facts under projection rules, scope, authority, confidence limits, and Unknown boundaries.

The warranted name is not an unrestricted constitutional State. The Book-native name is lower-case **state** in the chapter title and bounded resolution, more precisely **projected state** or **current constitutional standing**. The Book also uses “current lawful condition,” “context views,” and “candidate convergence” as neighboring sources of bounded standing. Therefore:

- one common phenomenon is recoverable for the state/projection chapter: replayed and reconciled projection under bounded authority;
- several neighboring phenomena remain distinct: events, facts, current lawful condition, context views, diagnostic visibility;
- ordinary implementation terms such as `state build` or `projection cache` are not independently constitutional; the concordance lists aliases without granting equivalence;
- no objective, universal, complete, or oracle-like constitutional State is recoverable.

Classification: **Directly recoverable with scope limitation**. Stronger accounts are forbidden.

## 4. Separate finding: common evidence-uptake or relevance act

The Book does not establish a single common act called relevance recognition, evidence uptake, or evidence presentation uptake. It establishes a broader **constitutional uptake** grammar as consumer-side dimensional grammar: a warranted boundary that gives upstream material bounded constitutional use or standing, or makes an assertion whose warrant materially depends on that material. It also establishes local acts and relations: bounded relevance in examination, method applicability, evidence support binding, provenance applicability, testimony consumption, comparison, admission as bounded participation, reliance, projection, recording, and establishment.

Thus the recoverable topology is plural and responsibility-local:

- material becoming visible: reachability/local visibility/frontier membership;
- material becoming relevant to a bounded question: bounded examination relevance only where explicit record material supports local responsibility, subject binding, evidence/provenance requirements, and authority;
- material being applicable: examination methods become candidates; evidence/provenance can be applicable represented support/lineage;
- material being admitted: bounded participation or admitted meaning in local posture;
- material being compared: bounded comparison standing;
- support becoming sufficient: only contrastively and locally recoverable, with ordering unresolved;
- reliance becoming warranted: bounded reliance within preserved warrant limits;
- authority becoming established: through interpreted/scope-bound operator expression or explicit approval and other competent authority boundaries;
- movement becoming lawful: separately warranted transition.

Classification: **Structurally recoverable** for dimensional constitutional uptake; **Not presently recoverable** for a universal evidence-uptake/relevance act.

## 5. Direct answers to required questions

### What constitutional grammar is directly recoverable from the Book?

Directly recoverable grammar includes: external/native grammar boundary; artifact versus standing; reachability without promotion; production authority versus constructability; lens versus road; constitutional uptake as dimensional consumer-side grammar; act versus act artifact; constraint/condition results; selection act and selection standing; selection versus authorization; orientation versus movement; bounded question standing; frontier membership/visibility; bounded examination relevance; cross-examination comparison standing; evidence support binding; testimony versus established fact; recorded assertion standing; projected state/current constitutional standing; projection visibility boundary; lawful realization and fidelity determination; operational realization warrant versus execution; execution versus recording; bounded reliance; admission as bounded participation; authority scope; handoff/communication limits; refusal/non-performance.

### What grammar is recoverable only through distinctions or prohibitions?

Recoverable chiefly through distinctions/prohibitions: general admissibility beyond local participation; general applicability outside examination/evidence/provenance local uses; sufficiency versus completion; stopping/completion ordering; comparison outside bounded cross-examination; warrant as a general constitutional family beyond operational realization/reliance; road sufficiency; external grammar promotion warrant; ownership comparison as support/discrepancy/residue rather than ownership establishment.

### What grammar remains merely possible?

Merely possible but not forced: a universal evidence-uptake sequence; a single relevance-recognition act; admissibility as a synonym for relevance plus sufficient support; sufficiency as a general precondition to every lawful movement; state as a generalized repository memory beyond projected-state clauses; road sufficiency as a complete producer-to-consumer calculus; a unified ordering of question -> frontier -> selection -> dispatch -> examination -> answer -> sufficiency -> completion.
