# External Grammar Promotion and Relation Compression Repair 001

## Status

This is a bounded Book of Seed recurrence-and-repair pass using three Fidelity distinctions as a quiet reading lens. It does not create a new constitutional framework, ontology, taxonomy of external grammars, universal promotion process, universal relation family, implementation seam, architecture, or realization selection.

The governing distinctions used only as reading discipline were:

```text
realization pressure != inherent language incapacity
consumer-side relation family != one universal act or stage
a constitutional distinction expressed through external grammar != promotion of that external grammar
```

The stronger translation rule is preserved:

```text
Python grammar remains external grammar.
SQL grammar remains external grammar.
A distinction expressed through either may be constitutionally recovered and warranted.
Only the bounded constitutional distinction receives standing.
The realization grammar remains attributed external grammar.
```

## Method actually used

The pass searched Book clauses for recurrence of three compression patterns:

1. witness grammar promoted with a recovered distinction;
2. plural local relations collapsed into one named act, stage, engine, process, or universal verb;
3. current realization pressure stated as language inevitability.

Implementation was not inspected to invent missing constitutional grammar. Existing Book reports naming implementation or SQLite witnesses were treated only as clues to the external witness grammar that shaped a clause.

## Exact Book edits made

### Edit 1 — Realization pressure is not inherent incapacity

File: `book_of_seed/realization_language_compression_orientation_001.md`

Before:

> The compensations required by a realization are evidence of the constitutional dimensions that realization cannot naturally preserve.

After:

> The compensations required in the examined realization are evidence of constitutional distinctions that current realization practice did not preserve without added boundaries; they are not, by themselves, proof of inherent language incapacity.

### Edit 2 — Fidelity question rebounded to current realization practice

File: `book_of_seed/realization_language_compression_orientation_001.md`

> Which compensations reveal constitutional dimensions that the language cannot represent faithfully by default?

### Edit 3 — Preserved contrastive lesson without language-incapacity claim

File: `book_of_seed/realization_language_compression_orientation_001.md`

### Edit 4 — SQLite witness pressure bounded to this witness

## Clauses intentionally left unchanged

- The external grammar clause in `01-grammar-and-standing/external-and-constitutional-grammar.md` already states that realization languages remain external grammar until bounded constitutional warrant states what survived and what remains limited or Unknown.
- The uptake clause in `01-grammar-and-standing/lenses-views-and-roads.md` already distinguishes consumer-side relation family, assertion-preserving road subfamily, consumer constraints, consumer acts, and handoff structures. A further edit would risk creating the universal relation-family definition this pass is forbidden to establish.
- The bounded reliance clause in `08-authority-communication-and-stopping/authority-scope.md` already treats reliance as bounded consumption under warrant and denies standing expansion.
- The handoff clause in `08-authority-communication-and-stopping/communication-and-handoff.md` already attributes typed value transport and adapter handoff as handoff structure rather than automatic uptake or road standing.

## Alternative readings and remaining Unknowns

- The repeated “cannot naturally preserve” wording may have meant “does not preserve under current idiom.” Because inherent incapacity lacked direct warrant, the safer Book wording now preserves only current realization pressure.
- The phrase “Consumer uptake is a later bounded act of receipt, reliance, refusal, or use” remains potentially compressed. It could be harmless shorthand, but receipt, reliance, refusal, and use may be several consumer-local relations or acts rather than one act. This pass leaves it unchanged because a direct repair would require choosing a positive relation-family structure beyond the evidence inspected here.
- The SQLite grammar limit may be a faithful report of one SQL witness, but the safer correction prevents the witness from becoming a global claim about SQL as such.
- No evidence in this pass warrants a taxonomy of provider vocabulary, repository structure, implementation classes, artifact schemas, database relations, diagnostic terminology, historical papers, operator metaphors, serialization, or persistence grammar.

## Questions exposed for later bounded passes

1. Where the Book says “uptake” in orientation prose, does it mean receipt, reliance, refusal, use, assertion adoption, constraint checking, handoff, or an unresolved compression?
2. When diagnostic or audit terminology names a visibility surface, does the Book preserve only diagnostic-scope testimony, or does any clause accidentally promote diagnostic vocabulary into constitutional grammar?
3. When artifact schemas or database relations expose a distinction, does the Book preserve the bounded distinction without importing schema-field or relation names as constitutional nouns?
4. When provider vocabulary or operator metaphors help find a distinction, does the resulting clause attribute the source grammar and stop at the bounded constitutional distinction?
5. When serialization or persistence grammar appears, does the Book distinguish preservation, durability, retrieval, rehydration, occurrence, renewed warrant, and consumer reliance without making any one of them a universal stage?

## Closing finding

The recurrence pass found two clear warranted repairs: overstrong realization-language incapacity wording in the realization orientation, and overbroad SQL view-capacity wording in the SQLite grammar-friction characterization. It also found one likely future pressure point around “consumer uptake” as “a later bounded act of receipt, reliance, refusal, or use,” but left it unchanged because the current Book already contains a stronger adjacent correction and because further repair would risk inventing a universal relation-family grammar.
