# Operational Realization Compression Constitutional Correction 001

## Scope and negative authority
This correction is limited to the active Book of Seed clauses directly needed to decompress the former operational-realization chapter after PR 1926 removed `ExecutionProposal` as a constitutional primitive. It amends active Book navigation and active chapter text only. It does not modify implementation, tests, CLI behavior, events, projections, archived documents, historical reports, root reports, recoveries, investigations, audits, surveys, excision records, or topology reports. It does not reopen the completed `ExecutionProposal` correction.

## Compressed operational-realization grammar
The active chapter formerly risked presenting one apparent road from purpose, need, gap, capability demand, candidate capability, reachability, selection, warrant, authority, representation emission or transport, and possible external realization. That grammar compressed independently varying constitutional coordinates into the phrase "operational realization." "Operational realization" was not retained as a universal Seed-native road from need to external effect.

## Coordinates recovered
The compressed phrase was decompressed into declared purpose or required outcome, need or gap standing, capability demand, candidate capability standing, mechanism identity, mechanism availability, mechanism-fitness warrant, representation compatibility, method compatibility, dependency standing, resource feasibility, reachability, selection standing, authority standing, request representation formation, emission occurrence, delivery or receipt, invocation occurrence, performance occurrence, refusal or non-occurrence, result testimony, recording, external-effect evidence, outcome evaluation, and causal standing. Capability standing, mechanism fitness, selection, authority, representation, emission, invocation, performance, result testimony, outcome evaluation, and external-effect evidence remain independently warranted coordinates.

## Capability standing
Capability standing remains only bounded support that a transformation may be possible under declared conditions. It may expose required input and output, mechanism evidence, representation or invocation grammar, conditions, scope, dependencies, resource requirements, authority requirements, known failures, temporal applicability, Unknowns, and conflicts. It is not a mechanism, selected mechanism, invocation permission, performance occurrence, recording, external effect, or causal standing.

## Mechanism-fitness standing
Mechanism fitness remains only a bounded conclusion that a particular mechanism may fit a declared purpose under stated evidence, conditions, scope, and Unknowns. The correction did not retain an "operational realization warrant" as a special constitutional kind. Current warrant wording is bounded as general warrant grammar applied to mechanism fitness.

## Selection and authority boundaries
Selection remains a bounded choice among candidates and its basis. It does not prove mechanism fitness, satisfy constraints, authorize action, form a request representation, emit a request, invoke a mechanism, perform an act, or record a result. Authority standing remains separately established by a responsible authority boundary and is not created by selection, capability, mechanism fitness, feasibility, readiness, representation, emission, invocation, performance report, or result record.

## Representation and emission boundaries
Representation formation and emission remain separate. Representation compatibility is not successful translation. Request representation formation is not emission. Emission is not delivery or receipt. Receipt is not interpretation, uptake, invocation, performance, or external effect.

## Invocation and performance occurrence
Invocation occurrence and performance occurrence remain independently evidenced coordinates. Invocation is not performance. Performance is not successful outcome. Refusal, non-occurrence, result testimony, and recording each preserve their own bounded standing and Unknowns.

## External-effect evidence
A provider response, performance report, reported occurrence, or recorded result is not verified external effect. External-effect evidence requires separate competent evidence preserving source, scope, temporal standing, authority, method, conflicts, and Unknowns.

## Interaction and outcome standing
Movement-linked interaction was preserved only as possible evidentiary relation among separately preserved standings, not a monolithic episode or universal interaction object. Reported occurrence is not verified effect. After-observation is not attributed result. Outcome evaluation is not causal standing, and an observed outcome does not prove the selected movement caused it. Capability revision is not mechanism selection, and learning is not automatic later action.

The former `07.Realization.A` and `07.Realization.B` clauses were cross-examined. Their faithful content is a general comparison of implementation witness against constitutional grammar: whether authority, evidence boundary, warrant, scope, Unknowns, representation or emission boundary, recording boundary, and mutation boundary survived translation, emission, transport, distribution, narrowing, naming, rendering, caching, or changed form without erasure, invention, mutation, or authority relocation. The clauses remain in the renamed chapter as `07.CapabilityMechanism.C` because the chapter still owns mechanism-fitness and implementation-witness comparison adjacent to capability standing. Fidelity was not converted into a mechanism-specific fitness engine, score, registry, diagnostic, or global certification.
