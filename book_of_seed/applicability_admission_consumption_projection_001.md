## After

## Constitutional distinction projected

The projected relation is:

```text
available upstream material
↓
consumer-local applicability evaluation
↓
applicable / inapplicable / Unknown / conflict
↓
consumer-local admission
↓
admitted / unadmitted / Unknown / conflict
↓
responsible consumer use
↓
new consumer-local assertion or standing
```

This is not declared as a mandatory sequence for every consumer. It is a constitutional distinction needed wherever this relationship is claimed.

## Negative authority

The projection does not authorize any of the following claims:

- applicability implies admission;
- admission implies consumption by the final target consumer;
- consumption re-establishes upstream standing;
- consumer-local use proves upstream producer occurrence;
- consumer-local use expands authority beyond the declared purpose;
- applicable material must be admitted;
- admitted material must be consumed;
- a consumer must resolve Unknown, conflict, or refusal at any crossing.
