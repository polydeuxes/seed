# Sensing, Gap, Capability, and Learning Amendment 001

## Status

Canonical amendment from merged `main` after PR 1899. This operation amended Book text and focused Book invariant tests only. It does not modify production code, CLI behavior, persistence, projections, selectors, observation sources, capability implementations, handoff artifacts, ExecutionStatus, or runtime behavior.

## Central Finding

Seed can lawfully revise its understanding of a bounded gap, candidate capabilities, and later-consumable standing only when prior testimony or standing survives with enough provenance for a bounded examination to explain the revision. The recovered constitutional topology is not one object and not one linear implementation pipeline: sensing can produce bounded testimony; remembering can preserve testimony or standing; comparison can establish insufficiency; capability demand can name a required transformation; capability standing can support candidate transformations under conditions; selection, warrant, authority, and handoff remain separate; resulting testimony can support outcome evaluation; outcome evaluation may revise gap or capability standing; learning establishment explains an evidence-supported revision; adaptive reliance is a later consumer act.

The amendment does not canonize Experience as a universal learning container, a universal sensory framework, a universal provider abstraction, a universal testimony superclass, or a complete learning loop. PR 1899's provisional “Fact” wording is corrected by preserving the established grammar that ObservationIngestor produces evidence-linked Fact-shaped artifacts, not independent Fact standing.

## Sensing and remembering

Sensing is production of bounded testimony about an environment, operation, or condition through a declared acquisition and interpretation boundary. External material is not Observation; Observation is not environment truth; Observation production is not recording; Observation is not current standing; sensing is not learning; and activity visibility is not a sensing result. Remembering is preservation of sufficient testimony or standing for later lawful recovery, including raw testimony, bounded history, rebuildable standing, or compressed established understanding. Sensing is not remembering, remembering is not current projection, projection pruning is not ledger erasure, retained history is not learning, and non-rebuildable is not preservation-required.

## Requirement, need, and gap topology

A required outcome or bounded purpose can be compared with current condition standing to establish need, insufficiency, or gap. Need is not gap by identity. A gap requires a declared reference condition and scope: a required condition, declared purpose, constitutional question, capability demand, budget, authority requirement, representation requirement, or other bounded criterion. Need established does not open movement, insufficiency is not permission, gap is not authorization, gap is not capability demand automatically, current difference is not material gap automatically, and unresolved question is not failed capability.

Direct answer: Does every established need constitute a gap? No; a need may expose bounded advancement requirements without establishing a scoped insufficiency against a declared reference condition. Direct answer: Does every gap open movement? No. Direct answer: Does a gap automatically establish a capability demand? No.

## Gap-family distinctions

The Book accepts gap-family distinctions as navigational, not as a closed schema. Outcome, evidence, knowledge, capability, reachability, dependency, authority, representation, and resource or budget gaps may be distinct when the evidence and reference condition distinguish them. The Book rejects collapses where capability Unknown equals capability absent, unreachable capability equals nonexistent capability, authority unavailable equals technical incapability, insufficient evidence equals failed outcome, or one unsupported candidate equals capability gap. Learning may refine which family is actually present.

## Capability demand

A capability demand is a bounded requirement for some transformation or outcome whose lawful realization would address a declared need or gap. Gap may expose transformation need, but not the correct transformation, required mechanism, capability existence, reachability, or permission. Capability demand is not capability, selected mechanism, authorization, or movement opened.

## Capability standing

Capability standing is bounded support that a transformation can be realized under declared conditions. Dimensions may include inputs, outputs, mechanism identity, version, grammar, behavioral evidence, scope, dependencies, authority reachability, resource requirements, failures, unknowns, conflicts, and temporal standing. No universal capability schema is prescribed. Declared capability is not verified capability; capability is not mechanism, selected mechanism, reachability, authority, reliance warrant, or movement selection.

Direct answer: Is a capability identical to a mechanism? No. Direct answer: Does one successful interaction verify a general capability? No. Direct answer: Does one failed interaction establish that a capability is absent? No.

## Passive capability learning

Capability learning is not movement-linked learning only. Provider rejection, operational measurement, behavioral probes, repeated dependency-exposing failures, or contradictory testimony may support revised capability standing after bounded examination and establishment. Behavior observation is not capability standing automatically, repeated behavior is not verified universal capability, and changed capability record is not learning automatically.

Direct answer: Can passive observation revise capability or gap standing? Yes, after bounded examination and establishment.

## Observation history and trajectory standing

Observation history is not trajectory or transition standing. Repeated observations do not establish outage, recovery, flapping, trajectory, trend, transition, consequence, or cause without separate examination preserving temporal standing, comparison authority, conflicts, and uncertainty.

## Movement-linked interaction

A lawful movement-linked evidentiary subject may be composed from declared purpose or gap, prior condition standing, selected movement, selection basis and authority, handoff identity, reported realization/refusal/non-occurrence, resulting testimony, bounded outcome evaluation, and uncertainty. It need not be monolithic. Selection is not realization; handoff is not realization; realization report is not verified effect; after-observation is not attributed result; interaction episode is not causal proof; interaction episode is not learning automatically.

## Outcome evaluation

Outcome evaluation is relative to declared purpose, need, gap, or capability demand. It may determine gap reduced, unchanged, enlarged, transformed, resolved within scope, or Unknown; candidate capability supported, contradicted, unchanged, or unverified; dependency or authority gap exposed; or outcome unattributable. It is not reward/success/failure/completed by identity. Movement occurred is not gap reduced; desired after-state observed is not movement caused it; gap reduction is not complete need satisfaction or causal proof; local outcome is not universal capability; failed outcome is not capability absence.

Direct answer: Does gap reduction prove that the selected movement caused the result? No.

## Gap revision

Gap revision is evidence-supported revision of insufficiency standing. It may show the gap remains, narrowed, widened, was mischaracterized, cannot be established, or that the requirement changed. It may convert an apparent capability gap into dependency, authority, representation, resource, budget, or evidence insufficiency. New observation is not gap revision automatically, changed current State is not changed gap standing automatically, gap revision is not movement authorization, and gap resolved within scope is not all advancement complete.

## Capability revision

Capability revision is evidence-supported change in bounded capability standing. It may revise support, lack of support, Unknown, conflict, applicability, representation compatibility, dependency, authority, resource feasibility, temporal applicability, and known failure boundaries. Outcome followed movement is not capability established; one success is not general verified capability; one failure is not capability absent; capability revised is not mechanism selected, movement opened, or causal standing.

## Learning establishment

Learning establishment is an evidence-supported revision of retained understanding because prior observations, comparisons, probes, measurements, or interactions warranted a new standing. It is best treated as a provenance-bearing establishment relation or explanation of revised standing, not necessarily a new universal artifact. It distinguishes “current knowledge changed because new testimony replaced current standing” from “understanding was revised because prior evidence, comparison, or interaction warranted new standing.”

Direct answer: Is learning constitutionally identical to model training? No.

## Adaptive reliance

Adaptive reliance is later consumption of revised gap, capability, or learned standing. It is independent from learning establishment. Changed selection is not learning automatically and not lawful adaptation automatically. A later consumer must expose the revised standing consumed, prior evidence support, applicable need/gap/capability demand, constrained decision or movement, reliance warrant, and remaining uncertainty. Raw experience history is not required selector input; established capability or gap standing may mediate lawful later reliance.

Direct answer: Must a later selector consume raw historical episodes directly? No. It may lawfully consume sufficiently established gap, capability, or learned standing with preserved provenance. Direct answer: Does a selector changing its answer prove learning? No. Direct answer: Does changed capability or gap standing automatically open movement? No.

## Causal standing

Movement M followed by outcome O is not movement M caused outcome O. Gap reduction and capability revision do not establish causation automatically. Causal standing remains distinct and stronger, requiring its own intervention, before/after, alternative-cause, temporal, criteria, counterevidence, repeatability, scope, and uncertainty examination.

## Operational self-sensing

External observation produces testimony about environment; operational measurement produces testimony about Seed's bounded operation. Operational measurements may support learning about resource feasibility, route cost, cache effectiveness, provider acquisition cost, projection behavior, budget reachability, dependency degradation, and ordinary operational behavior. They may revise self-capability standing, resource or budget gap standing, route reachability, and later stopping or selection only through bounded examination. Operational measurement is not capability standing automatically, baseline is not capability automatically, duration exceeded is not route impossible, route expensive is not unauthorized, and self-capability learning is not activity status.

## ExecutionStatus exclusion

Activity visibility is not sensing result, operational measurement, capability testimony, gap standing, interaction outcome, or learning. This amendment performs no ExecutionStatus cleanup and does not use status emission as proof that an operation, capability, or realization occurred.

## Fact-standing correction

ObservationIngestor to evidence-linked Fact-shaped artifact does not independently establish Fact standing, current standing, verified standing, trajectory standing, gap standing, capability standing, or learning standing. Later standing requires separately warranted boundaries.
