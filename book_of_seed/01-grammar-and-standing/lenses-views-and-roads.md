# Lenses, Views, and Constitutional Roads

## Constitutional subject
The difference between observing constitutional material through a view and possessing a warranted transition between constitutional subjects.

## Core question
What makes a relationship a constitutional road rather than an informative lens or repository adjacency?

## Initial resolution
Views and lenses may select, compose, or present state without authorizing movement. Repository adjacency and call order are evidence for investigation, not constitutional sequence.

## Important distinctions
- lens != constitutional road
- repository adjacency != constitutional sequence
- visibility != transition warrant

## Representative repository anchors
- `seed_runtime/constitutional_view_selection.py`
- `seed_runtime/constitutional_view_composition.py`
- `seed_runtime/constitutional_pipeline.py::invoke_constitutional_pipeline`

## Counterexamples or failure modes
- Reading a diagnostic display order as a lawful process order.
- Inferring a road because one module imports another.
