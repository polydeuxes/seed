# Book Fidelity Repair: State and Restrained Movement 001

## 1. Corrected direction of constitutional authority

This repair reverses the direction used by PR 1805. The Book is examined as a preservation artifact, not as a self-warranting constitutional source. Already-recovered constitutional grammar governs the examination: standing, responsibility, evidence, warrant, constraint, bounded acts, lawful transitions, preserved limits, refusal, stopping, and Unknown are primary. Objects, records, views, projections, state-like labels, and artifacts are carriers or witnesses only.

The repair question is therefore not whether the Book says ``state`` or ``constitutional uptake``. The question is whether each clause preserves a restrained movement relation: prior standing plus evidence and warrant, consumed by a constrained responsible act, producing bounded resulting standing while preserving prohibited movement and Unknowns.

## 4. Restrained movements compressed beneath object-oriented language

The affected district compresses these local movements:

- Recorded occurrence assertion becomes recorded history: an event records that an assertion was made, not that the asserted occurrence is true.
- Supported claim becomes fact standing: a fact carries support within its provenance and authority limits.
- Recorded material becomes projected material: a responsible projection replays bounded inputs under declared rules.
- Projected material may support current lawful condition: only within scope, freshness, projection rule, confidence, authority, and Unknown limits.
- Projected material may support current constitutional standing: only through a bounded consumer act with evidence, warrant, constraints, and preserved limits.
- Material becomes visible: a surface can expose what it reports without creating source truth or authority.
- Material becomes relevant: examination may proceed further inside a local responsibility boundary.
- Material becomes admissible or admitted: participation in a next bounded posture may be allowed without truth or reusable approval.
- Support becomes sufficient: only for a local next posture or stopping condition where the boundary warrants it.
- Reliance becomes warranted: only within the preserved role, purpose, source/provenance, evidence, authority, scope, confidence, and Unknown limits.
- Authority becomes established: only by a competent bounded source or approval after interpretation and scope binding.
- Movement becomes lawful: only through a warranted transition in lawful position, standing, focus, or advancement posture.

These movements are not universal and need not occur in this order.

## 6. Projection, current standing, lawful condition, visibility, and recorded history

- Recorded history: preserved event or record that an assertion was made; it does not prove the asserted occurrence.
- Visibility: a surface exposes bounded output or material within recorded scope, inputs, freshness, shape, and mutation boundary; visibility is not uptake.
- Projection: a replay- and rule-shaped view of bounded inputs; projected material may support later determinations but is not standing by identity.
- Current lawful condition: a bounded condition supported by preserved material and projection rules for a question or responsibility; it is not complete memory or objective reality.
- Current constitutional standing: repository-governed standing established or supported through a responsible bounded act with evidence, warrant, constraints, authority, confidence, and Unknown limits.

## 7. Fidelity characterization of constitutional uptake

Constitutional uptake is faithful only as a dimensional consumer-side relation family. It can describe local transitions where a responsible consumer gives upstream material bounded constitutional use, bounded consumer-side standing, or warranting role in a consumer assertion. It includes a constitutional road subfamily when assertion preservation is warranted. It is not faithful as a universal act, universal stage, root, replacement for relevance recognition, or object taxonomy. The Book still leaves Unknown the complete positive criteria for every possible uptake variant; the repair preserves only the consumer-side family and the local road distinction.
