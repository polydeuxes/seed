## Independent surviving Book grammar

The following grammar survives without relying on the abandoned Seed-owned tool road:

- External grammar remains external until a bounded translation preserves source, scope, uncertainty, and authority limits.
- Artifact shape and public reachability do not establish constitutional standing.
- Producer occurrence, constructor availability, consumer admissibility, and current standing are separate.
- A lens/view/projection can expose or compose material without creating a constitutional road.
- A road requires warranted assertion-preserving uptake by a responsible consumer.
- Act, act artifact, constraint, policy, precondition, selection, authorization, approval, occurrence, refusal, and non-performance remain distinct.
- Need opens possible movement but does not grant permission.
- Request, proposal, occurrence, result, record, testimony, evidence, fact, projection, and current standing remain separate.
- Recording preserves that a claim/assertion was made; it does not automatically establish truth.
- Testimony/Observation can become evidence; fact standing requires support/admission/promotion boundaries.
- Projection/View can support current standing only through a responsible consumer boundary preserving evidence, warrant, freshness, confidence, authority, and Unknowns.
- Egress representation can communicate bounded standing without strengthening it.
- External delivery/realization/receipt/understanding/reliance are not proved merely by Seed rendering or handoff.

## Observation/View branching assessment

The operator hypothesis was:

```text
Observation → View
```

nested within:

```text
ingress → egress
```

### What the Book and repository support

- Observation/testimony is ingress-side or source-attributed standing when produced from external/operator/returned material through an ingress or observation boundary.
- Current implementation projects Observations directly into `ObservationView` as a read-only Observation-facing View. That branch does not require prior Fact standing.
- Evidence and fact establishment are possible intermediate responsibilities for fact- or standing-facing Views, not mandatory stages for every View.
- Observation ingestion preserves an Observation event, an Evidence event, and only an optional Fact event. Fact promotion may be suppressed while Observation and Evidence remain available.
- View/projection is an internal recoverable representation over bounded material, not standing by identity.
