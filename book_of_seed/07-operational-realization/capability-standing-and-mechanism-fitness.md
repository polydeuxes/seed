# Capability Standing and Mechanism Fitness

## Constitutional subject
The bounded standing by which Seed may say that a transformation may be possible under declared conditions, and the separate bounded conclusion that a particular mechanism may fit a declared purpose under stated evidence and limits.

## Core question
What support may establish capability standing or mechanism fitness without turning either coordinate into selection, authority, representation emission, invocation, performance, result testimony, or external effect?

## Bounded resolution
"Operational realization" is not retained here as a universal Seed-native road from need to external effect. Where implementation or ordinary language still uses that phrase, the phrase has only bounded shorthand standing and must be decomposed into independently warranted coordinates: declared purpose, need or gap standing, capability demand, candidate capability standing, mechanism identity, mechanism availability, mechanism-fitness warrant, representation and method compatibility, dependencies, resource feasibility, reachability, selection, authority, request representation formation, emission, delivery or receipt, invocation, performance, refusal or non-occurrence, result testimony, recording, outcome evaluation, external-effect evidence, and causal standing. Unevidenced coordinates remain Unknown.

Capability standing is bounded support that a transformation may be possible under declared conditions. It may expose required input and output, mechanism evidence, representation or invocation grammar, conditions, scope, dependencies, resource requirements, authority requirements, known failures, temporal applicability, Unknowns, and conflicts. It is not a selected mechanism, invocation permission, performance occurrence, recording, or external effect, and the Book does not prescribe a universal capability artifact or schema.

Mechanism fitness is a bounded conclusion that a particular mechanism may fit a declared purpose under stated evidence, conditions, scope, and Unknowns. It remains distinct from capability standing, mechanism availability, reachability, selection, authorization, representation formation, emission, invocation, and performance. A warrant supporting mechanism fitness is ordinary warrant grammar applied to that local conclusion, not a special constitutional primitive that creates a road to external effect.

## Addressable boundaries for capability standing and mechanism fitness

### 07.CapabilityMechanism.A — Capability standing remains bounded transformation support
Evidence may support that a transformation is possible under declared input, output, condition, scope, temporal, dependency, resource, representation, invocation, authority, and failure limits. Mechanism evidence may contribute to that standing, but capability standing is not the mechanism itself, not a selected mechanism, not authority to invoke, not proof of performance, not result testimony, not verified external effect, and not causal standing.

### 07.CapabilityMechanism.B — Mechanism fitness is local warrant, not realization authority
A mechanism-fitness finding examines a particular mechanism against a declared purpose and preserved capability, representation, method, dependency, resource, temporal, provenance, conflict, and Unknown limits. Mechanism exists does not establish mechanism availability. Mechanism available does not establish reachability. Reachable does not establish fit for purpose. Mechanism fitness does not establish selection. Selection does not establish authority. Authority standing does not establish invocation. Invocation does not establish performance, successful outcome, or verified external effect.

### 07.CapabilityMechanism.C — Fidelity determination is general implementation-witness comparison
A fidelity determination is a bounded constitutional comparison of implementation witness against the constitutional grammar it claims to preserve. It consumes the constitutional authority, evidence boundary, scope, representation or emission boundary, recording boundary, mutation boundary, and implementation witness for the translated, emitted, transported, distributed, narrowed, renamed, cached, rendered, or changed-form mechanism under review. It asks whether required invariants survived without erasure, invention, mutation, or relocation of authority while preserving lawful implementation freedom and structural asymmetry. The resulting artifact is a scope-limited qualitative finding: faithful within scope, unfaithful boundary crossing, or Unknown. This is not a mechanism-specific fitness engine, score, public diagnostic, global certification, runtime engine, or proof of external effect.

## Important distinctions
- capability standing != mechanism
- mechanism exists != mechanism available
- mechanism available != reachable
- reachable != fit for purpose
- mechanism fitness != selection
- selection != authority
- authority standing != invocation
- representation compatibility != successful translation
- representation formation != emission
- emission != receipt
- receipt != invocation
- invocation != performance
- performance != successful outcome
- performance report != verified external effect
- outcome observed != selected movement caused outcome
- recording != realization
- possible realization != occurrence
- external realization != one constitutional stage
- fidelity determination != runtime engine, score, public diagnostic, or global certification

## Representative repository anchors
- `seed_runtime/candidate_operational_realization.py` is current implementation vocabulary for a read-only candidate projection. Its artifact has bounded candidate standing over mechanism identity, availability, grammar, behavior, representation compatibility, method compatibility, dependencies, authority reachability, Unknowns, and conflicts; it does not rank, select, authorize, emit, invoke, perform, record, mutate the cluster, or prove external effect.
- `seed_runtime/capability_verification.py` is current implementation vocabulary for read-only capability verification inspection. It joins capability candidates to projected verification facts for inspection; it does not select capabilities, evaluate policy, invoke tools, plan, or execute.
- `seed_runtime/operational_realization_need_projection.py` is current implementation vocabulary for read-only need projection around bounded realization components. Its standing is limited to requirement or need classification and explicitly does not select, warrant, translate, prepare invocation, request authority, authorize movement, start execution, start recording, write the event ledger, or mutate the cluster.
- `seed_runtime/operational_realization_warrant.py` is current implementation vocabulary for a read-only warrant over one selected candidate. After decompression, it witnesses only candidate-specific mechanism-fitness support with preserved reachability, selection, grammar, behavior, representation, method, mechanism, dependency, authority-reachability, provenance, Unknown, and conflict limits. Its own notes say it does not construct an external representation, authorize, schedule, emit, or execute.

## Counterexamples or failure modes
- Equating a catalog entry, declared capability, or mechanism name with an available working mechanism.
- Treating mechanism availability or reachability as proof of mechanism fitness.
- Treating a mechanism-fitness warrant as selection, authorization, request formation, emission, invocation, performance, recording, or verified external effect.
- Treating representation compatibility as successful translation, emission as receipt, receipt as invocation, invocation as performance, or a performance report as verified external effect.
- Treating an observed outcome as proof that selected movement caused the outcome.
- Generalizing one bounded fidelity finding into a complete owner map, completion declaration, implementation topology, or external-effect finding.

## Interaction, outcome, capability revision, and learning

A movement-linked interaction may be a lawful evidentiary subject composed from separately preserved artifacts and relations: declared purpose or gap, prior condition standing, selected movement, selection basis and authority, emission or transport identity, reported occurrence, refusal, or non-occurrence, resulting testimony, bounded outcome evaluation, and uncertainty. It need not be one monolithic durable object and must not become a universal Experience container or a universal interaction episode. Observation-derived learning is not movement-linked interaction; selection is not occurrence; emission or transport is not occurrence; occurrence report is not verified effect; after-observation is not attributed result; and interaction testimony is not causal proof or learning automatically.

Outcome evaluation is relative to a declared purpose, need, gap, or capability demand. It may determine that a gap was reduced, unchanged, enlarged, transformed, resolved within scope, or remains Unknown; that a candidate capability was supported or contradicted within scope; that capability standing is unchanged; that performance or external effect remained unverified; that dependency or authority gap was exposed; or that outcome cannot be attributed. It is not reducible to reward, success, failure, completion, external-effect evidence, or causal standing. Movement occurred is not gap reduced, desired after-state observed is not movement caused it, gap reduction is not complete need satisfaction or causal proof, local outcome is not universal capability, failed outcome is not capability absence automatically, and outcome evaluation is not causal standing.

Capability revision is evidence-supported change in bounded capability standing. It may revise support, lack of support, Unknown, conflict, applicability conditions, representation compatibility, dependency requirements, authority requirements, resource feasibility, temporal applicability, and known failure boundaries. New behavior observed is not capability movement warranted automatically, one success is not universal capability standing, one failure is not capability absence, capability revision is not mechanism selection, and capability revision is not authority to act.

Learning establishment may revise retained understanding when prior standing, admitted prior evidence, bounded examination or comparison, sufficiency, and warrant establish revised bounded standing. Learning may concern condition, trajectory, insufficiency, gap, capability, reachability, dependency, authority, representation compatibility, resource feasibility, expectation, or strategy standing only within preserved evidence, authority, scope, and limits. Learning is not model training by identity, changed storage by identity, automatic later selection, automatic later action, adaptive reliance, or proof of causation.
