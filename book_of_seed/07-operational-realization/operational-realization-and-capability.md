# Operational Realization and Capability

## Constitutional subject
The relationship between a required outcome, an observed or candidate capability, and a concrete mechanism that could realize it.

## Core question
What establishes that a mechanism can lawfully and sufficiently realize a bounded operational need?

## Bounded resolution
Catalog declarations and behavioral observations can support capability candidates. Operational realization requires attributed mechanism evidence, invocation grammar, and selection appropriate to the bounded need.


## Addressable boundaries for lawful realization

### 07.Realization.A — Lawful realization preserves authority without requiring structural symmetry
An implementation mechanism may lawfully realize a constitutional responsibility when it preserves the authority, evidence boundary, warrant, scope, Unknowns, handoff, recording boundary, and mutation boundary that govern the responsibility. One-to-one structural symmetry between constitutional vocabulary and implementation mechanics is neither required nor sufficient. Compatibility, orchestration, distribution, narrowing, naming, rendering, caching, or dispatch mechanics remain constitutionally neutral while they do not claim, erase, relocate, mutate, or decide constitutional authority.

### 07.Realization.B — Fidelity determination is a bounded constitutional act
A fidelity determination examines a specific realization boundary by consuming the constitutional authority to be preserved and the implementation realization evidence for the translated, handed off, distributed, narrowed, or changed-form mechanism under review. It asks whether required invariants survived without erasure, invention, mutation, or relocation of authority, while preserving lawful implementation freedom and structural asymmetry. The resulting artifact is a scope-limited qualitative finding: faithful within scope, unfaithful boundary crossing, or Unknown. Later reliance, responsibility transfer, lawful stop, implementation ownership recovery, diagnostic recording, or further projection may consume that finding only within its stated evidence, boundary, authority scope, consumer limits, and Unknowns.

## Important distinctions
- declared capability != verified capability
- capability != selected mechanism
- possible realization != warranted execution
- lawful realization != structural symmetry
- fidelity determination != runtime engine, score, public diagnostic, or global certification

## Counterexamples or failure modes
- Equating a catalog entry with an available working mechanism.
- Selecting a mechanism based only on name similarity.
- Treating compatibility, successful rendering, or consumer acceptance as proof that constitutional authority survived.
- Generalizing one bounded fidelity finding into a complete owner map, completion declaration, or implementation topology.

## Capability standing, interaction, and outcome amendment 001

Capability standing is bounded support that a transformation can be realized under declared conditions. It may preserve required input and output, mechanism identity, version, invocation or representation grammar, behavioral evidence, conditions and scope, dependencies, authority reachability, resource requirements, known failures, unknowns, conflicts, and temporal standing, but the Book does not prescribe a universal capability schema. Mechanism exists does not establish capability; one successful observation does not establish general capability; capability under condition C is not capability under all conditions; capability is not mechanism, reachability, authority, reliance warrant, movement selection, or selected mechanism.

Capability revision is evidence-supported change in bounded capability standing. It may revise support, lack of support, Unknown, conflict, applicability conditions, representation compatibility, dependency requirements, authority requirements, resource feasibility, temporal applicability, and known failure boundaries. An outcome following movement does not establish capability, one successful episode does not verify a general capability, one failed episode does not establish capability absence, capability revised does not select a mechanism, capability revised does not open movement, and changed capability standing does not establish causal standing.

Capability learning may be passive as well as movement-linked. Observed provider rejection of a representation, operational measurement showing route cost beyond budget, behavioral probes supporting a mechanism under condition C, repeated failures exposing a dependency, or testimony contradicting a declared capability may support revised capability standing after bounded examination and establishment. Behavior observation is not capability standing automatically, repeated behavior is not verified universal capability, and a changed capability record is not learning automatically.

A movement-linked interaction may be a lawful evidentiary subject composed from separately preserved artifacts and relations: declared purpose or gap, prior condition standing, selected movement, selection basis and authority, handoff identity, reported realization, refusal, or non-occurrence, resulting testimony, bounded outcome evaluation, and uncertainty. It need not be one monolithic durable object and must not become a universal Experience container. Observation-derived learning is not movement-linked interaction; selection is not realization; handoff is not realization; realization report is not verified effect; after-observation is not attributed result; and interaction episode is not causal proof or learning automatically.

Outcome evaluation is relative to a declared purpose, need, gap, or capability demand. It may determine that a gap was reduced, unchanged, enlarged, transformed, resolved within scope, or remains Unknown; that a candidate capability was supported or contradicted within scope; that capability standing is unchanged; that realization remained unverified; that dependency or authority gap was exposed; or that outcome cannot be attributed. It is not reducible to reward, success, failure, or completed. Movement occurred is not gap reduced, desired after-state observed is not movement caused it, gap reduction is not complete need satisfaction or causal proof, local outcome is not universal capability, failed outcome is not capability absence automatically, and outcome evaluation is not causal standing.

The movement road remains bounded purpose, established need or gap, capability demand, candidate capability standing, reachability, consideration and selection, warrant, authority, handoff, and possible external realization. Learning does not bypass this road: learned capability is not selected movement, learned gap is not permission to act, best historical outcome is not authorized choice, and adaptive reliance is not automatic execution.

Direct answers preserved by this clause: Is a capability identical to a mechanism? No. Does one successful interaction verify a general capability? No. Does one failed interaction establish that a capability is absent? No. Does gap reduction prove that the selected movement caused the result? No. Must a later selector consume raw historical episodes directly? No. It may lawfully consume sufficiently established gap, capability, or learned standing with preserved provenance. Does a selector changing its answer prove learning? No. Is learning constitutionally identical to model training? No. Are trajectory, interaction-outcome, gap-revision, capability-revision, learning, adaptive-reliance, or causal-standing implementations complete today? No. Current Seed contains partial separable ingredients only.

## Constrained movement capability and interaction correction 001

Capability standing constrains possible realization movement by answering which transformations remain supportably possible under which conditions and scope. It may admit a candidate, exclude a candidate, narrow applicability, preserve Unknown, expose conflict, redirect inquiry, or require further evidence. Capability standing is not selected movement, reachable movement, authorized movement, warranted reliance, handoff, realization, or mechanism ownership.

Capability revision may be understood as constrained movement in possible-realization standing. It consumes prior capability standing, new behavioral, observational, measurement, or interaction evidence, bounded examination, scope, conflict, uncertainty, and warrant to establish revised support, lack of support, Unknown, conflict, applicability, representation compatibility, dependency, authority, resource feasibility, temporal applicability, or known failure boundaries. New behavior observed is not capability movement warranted automatically, one success is not universal capability standing, one failure is not capability absence, and capability revision is not mechanism selection.

Movement-linked interaction is best understood as an evidentiary road among separately preserved standings rather than an Episode or Experience object by identity. To examine the consequence of selected movement, Seed must keep connected the prior lawful position, selected movement, selection warrant, authority, handoff, reported realization/refusal/non-occurrence, resulting testimony, and outcome comparison. Selection is not realization, handoff is not realization, realization report is not verified effect, and after-observation is not attributed result.

Outcome evaluation is a constraint on later standing movement. Resulting testimony plus declared purpose, gap, or capability demand plus a comparison method may establish a bounded outcome finding. That finding may constrain gap standing, capability standing, stopping, further inquiry, or later candidate consideration, but outcome evaluation is not automatic gap revision, automatic capability revision, causal standing, or proof that completed means successful movement.

Direct answers preserved by this clause: Does capability standing itself select movement? No. Can capability revision be understood as movement in realization standing? Yes, when bounded by prior capability standing, new evidence, examination method, warrant, conditions, scope, conflict, and Unknowns.
