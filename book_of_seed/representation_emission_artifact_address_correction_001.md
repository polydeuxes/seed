## Representation-formation boundary

Representation formation does not make material operator-facing or external.

A representation may exist without being emitted. Representation formation does not establish emission occurrence, operator-facing occurrence, external presentation, delivery, receipt, interpretation, uptake, reliance, responsibility transition, authority transition, or external realization.

## Emission-occurrence boundary

A responsible emission occurrence is required to establish bounded testimony that the representation was presented toward a candidate consumer boundary.

Emission occurrence remains distinct from every later consumer or external coordinate. Emission occurred does not establish delivery. Delivery does not establish receipt. Receipt does not establish interpretation. Interpretation does not establish uptake. Uptake does not establish reliance. Reliance does not establish responsibility transition. Responsibility transition does not establish authority transition. Authority transition does not establish external realization.

## Representation assertion standing

The representation itself may carry only the bounded assertion standing warranted by its source material, formation method, declared purpose, scope, provenance, authority limits, and preserved Unknowns. Formation, rendering, convenience, or later emission does not strengthen that assertion standing.

## Emission testimony standing

An emission occurrence may establish bounded testimony that a responsible producer presented the bounded representation toward a candidate consumer boundary. That testimony is testimony that presentation occurred; it is not delivery, receipt, interpretation, uptake, reliance, responsibility transition, authority transition, or external realization.
