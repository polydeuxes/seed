## Representation structure vs formation act

Representation form or structure describes preserved material shape. Representation formation describes the responsible construction act by which a bounded representation is made. A preserved representation shape may carry fields, identity, provenance, limits, and assertion form without proving that every possible producer occurrence, consumer occurrence, or downstream crossing happened.

## Formation vs emission occurrence

Representation formation does not establish emission. Emission requires separately evidenced presentation of the bounded representation toward a candidate consumer boundary. Emission may establish bounded testimony that presentation occurred; it does not establish delivery, receipt, interpretation, uptake, reliance, responsibility transition, authority transition, or external realization.

## Emission vs transport structure

Transport structure describes possible carriage, routing, addressing, or compatibility structure. It is not emission occurrence and does not prove transport completion. Transport structure does not establish delivery. Delivery does not establish receipt.
