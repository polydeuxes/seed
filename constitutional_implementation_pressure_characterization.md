## Short answer

Recurring repository evidence does **not** support `Implementation Pressure` as a standalone constitutional participant.

The distinction from grammar-bounded implementation comparison is smaller:

```text
Implementation comparison becomes implementation pressure only when the
comparison exposes a repository-evidenced boundary insufficiency: an observed
artifact, owner, handoff, behavior, diagnostic shape, or compatibility condition
no longer fits comfortably inside the currently preserved local boundary, while
remaining bounded enough to demand attention, preserve Unknowns, or stop.
```

In shorter form:

```text
grammar-bounded comparison
→ evidence-bound boundary insufficiency, if any
→ investigation pressure or lawful stop
```

The shared constitutional work is therefore **boundary-insufficiency discrimination under preserved limits**.

Comparison can lawfully end with compatibility, expected locality, acceptable divergence, family-local distinction, preserved Unknown, or lawful stop. Pressure appears only when the comparison reveals unresolved boundary strain that is evidence-bound and local enough to merit investigation attention. Even then, pressure remains attention or inquiry pressure; it is not implementation authority.

## Recurring implementation evidence

### 1. Recovery slices distinguish pressure-bearing compression from compatibility-preserving change

Implementation recovery and architectural recoverability evidence repeatedly require more than comparison. A candidate distinction must have implementation owner or call-path evidence, current compressed ownership, an observable compatibility-preserving separation path, and tests or generated architecture evidence capable of preserving behavior.

This means implementation pressure is not any divergence. It appears when comparison reveals compressed responsibility, invisible boundary, or insufficiently separated owner/handoff that is grounded in implementation evidence.

### 2. Compatibility preservation often absorbs divergence without pressure

Operational responsibility slices and architectural recoverability audits repeatedly preserve public CLI behavior, JSON shape, schemas, event payloads, manifests, recommendation ranking, policy behavior, and execution dispatch. Divergence or separation can be acceptable when compatibility boundaries hold.

Therefore divergence alone does not distinguish pressure. The pressure-bearing condition is not difference; it is unresolved boundary insufficiency after compatibility, locality, acceptance, and Unknown preservation have been checked.

### 3. Producer -> artifact -> consumer recoveries are local handoff comparisons

Producer/artifact/consumer recoveries compare emitted artifacts with downstream consumer roles. When the artifact carries enough support and the consumer uses it only for the supported role, the result is family-local handoff or lawful acceptance.

Pressure appears only when the comparison exposes missing handoff visibility, compressed ownership, orphaned output, unclear consumer authority, or unsupported promotion pressure. It does not appear from handoff existence alone.

### 4. Diagnostic surfaces prove pressure is bounded visibility

The app's `--diagnostic-inventory --json` output exposes many read-only diagnostic surfaces with shape fields such as record scope, event-ledger behavior, and mutation boundaries. The app's `--pressure-audit` output identified orphaned predicates and fragile predicates as pressure items with evidence and recommended inspection, not mutation or planning.

This reinforces that pressure is a diagnostic/investigation visibility relation: it can classify evidence-bearing strain and point to an inspection surface, while remaining non-mutating and non-authoritative.

### 5. Knowledge reachability shows comparison can preserve Unknown or bounded reachability

The app's knowledge reachability audit evaluates candidates across preserved, projected, read-model, inquiry, and rendered surfaces. That kind of comparison can produce staged reachability, truncation, absence, or Unknown without creating pressure by itself.

The constitutional distinction is therefore not `compared` versus `not compared`; it is whether comparison reveals unresolved boundary insufficiency that cannot be lawfully handled as compatibility, locality, acceptance, preserved Unknown, or stop.
