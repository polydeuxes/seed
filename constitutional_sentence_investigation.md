## Independently inspected candidate sentences

### 1. Observation supports Evidence

#### What bounded participants are present?

- A bounded source-attributed report or acquisition payload.
- A source, source boundary, source-local vocabulary, vantage point, subject-as-reported, time or preservation timing where available, scope, confidence or quality limits where supported, and provenance.
- A receiving evidence or support surface that may later consume the observation.
- A claim, fact, projection, audit, explanation, ambiguity, or stop surface only as later possible consumers, not as Observation's identity.

#### What lawful relationship is asserted?

A source-attributed Observation may support Evidence when repository authority admits the observation as safe, attributable, context-bearing support. Observation can become evidence-bearing through lawful preservation or ingestion, but the support relation is not automatic truth transfer.

#### What constitutional conditions govern it?

- The observation must remain source-attributed.
- Provenance, scope, vantage point, subject-as-reported, confidence, freshness or collection constraints where present, and promotion decisions must remain attached.
- The receiving boundary must be competent to preserve or consider the report.
- Evidence formation must preserve support and provenance rather than consuming Observation as truth.
- Missing observation is not negative evidence.
- Fact promotion, verification, projection, history, and event preservation remain separate and optional.

#### What makes this sentence lawful?

It is lawful when a competent source reports bounded material and an authorized repository boundary preserves that report as possible support without overclaiming truth. The lawful statement is not merely that Observation exists; it is that an attributed observation can support evidence under preserved provenance and admissibility limits.

#### What makes this sentence unlawful?

It is unlawful when the report is treated as automatic truth, automatic evidence, automatic fact, verification, promotion authority, projection authority, host truth, identity, runtime state, or universal first event. It is also unlawful when source vocabulary is collapsed into repository knowledge or when provenance and subject-as-reported are stripped.

#### Sentence != ?

Observation supports Evidence != Observation is truth.

Observation supports Evidence != Observation is Evidence in all cases.

Observation supports Evidence != Observation creates Fact.

Observation supports Evidence != source vocabulary becomes repository knowledge.

### 2. Evidence warrants Reliance

#### What bounded participants are present?

- Bounded content: claim, relation, value, observation, agreement, report, unknown, inquiry condition, classification, diagnostic result, or translated representation.
- Repository evidence, support, provenance, role, subject identity, authority boundary, negative authority, confidence limit, and preserved unknowns.
- A potential consumer or downstream surface with a specific proposed use.

#### What lawful relationship is asserted?

Evidence may warrant bounded Reliance when it entitles a specific consumer to use bounded content for a specific role inside the authority boundary that the evidence actually supports.

#### What constitutional conditions govern it?

- The evidence must bind the bounded subject to support and provenance.
- The content's role must remain stable through translation or presentation.
- Authority must be competent and no broader than the evidence permits.
- Negative authority and non-promotion conditions must remain visible.
- Unknowns must be preserved rather than resolved by assertion.
- Confidence must remain calibrated.
- Downstream use must stay inside the warranted role.

#### What makes this sentence lawful?

It is lawful when repository evidence and provenance survive into the consumer's use, and the consumer relies only within the role, subject, confidence, and authority boundary actually supported.

#### What makes this sentence unlawful?

It is unlawful when visibility, repeated vocabulary, fluent expression, provider syntax, diagnostic output, presentation labels, operator prose, useful report shape, or candidate support is treated as enough for reliance. It is also unlawful when reliance broadens authority, strips provenance, changes role, inflates confidence, mutates cluster state without authority, or converts unknowns into facts.

#### Sentence != ?

Evidence warrants Reliance != evidence alone is sufficient for every use.

Evidence warrants Reliance != authority alone is sufficient.

Evidence warrants Reliance != syntax preservation.

Evidence warrants Reliance != proof, truth, universal trust, implementation instruction, or mutation authority.
