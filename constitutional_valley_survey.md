# Constitutional Valley Survey

## Boundary

This is exactly one bounded survey. It uses the completed Scout audit as the search warrant and treats characterization as a surveying technique rather than as an independent constitutional competency.

The survey increases local observational resolution for every visible Scout valley while refusing competency recovery, district recovery, topology recovery, bridge recovery, ownership recovery, runtime recovery, implementation recommendation, and slice recommendation.

Each valley is treated independently. Recurrence is evidence of visible terrain only, not evidence of relationship, architecture, or promoted repository knowledge.

Repository authority wins.
