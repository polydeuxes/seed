## Constitutional identity

The recovered constitutional movement is:

```text
Constitutional Responsibility Movement.
```

Its bounded identity is:

```text
The evidence-gated, warrant-preserving movement by which
responsibility-bearing material may cross from one competent constitutional
participant to another through a competent local boundary, while preserving
provenance, authority, unknowns, confidence, locality, and lawful stop.
```

It is a constitutional movement, not a mechanism, not an owner, not a runtime
handoff, not a scheduler, not a planner, not a notification system, and not a
universal framework. It names the recurring lawful crossing of
responsibility-bearing material under preserved constitutional invariants.

## Recurring constitutional purpose

The recurring purpose is to permit responsibility to move only when movement is
lawful and inspectable.

It prevents two opposite failures:

1. **Unlawful freezing:** responsibility-bearing material remains trapped in a
   prior participant even after repository evidence supports a competent local
   boundary crossing.
2. **Unlawful drift:** responsibility silently becomes truth, ownership,
   mutation authority, implementation authority, runtime behavior, presentation
   vocabulary, or universal law without preserved warrant and boundary.

The movement exists so that constitutional material can be translated,
projected, recorded, composed, relied upon, or stopped without losing the
conditions that made it lawful.

## Positively owned constitutional responsibility

Constitutional Responsibility Movement owns only this constitutional
responsibility:

```text
Determine whether responsibility-bearing material may lawfully cross a
competent local boundary between already supported constitutional participants,
and preserve the warrant, provenance, authority boundary, negative authority,
unknowns, confidence limits, locality, and stop condition that govern that
crossing.
```

It positively owns the movement discipline, not the neighboring participants. It
can say that a responsibility-bearing crossing is lawful, unsupported,
authority-blocked, confidence-limited, locality-limited, unknown-preserving, or
stopped. It does not own the source participant's full responsibility, the
receiving participant's full responsibility, or any implementation behavior.

## Constitutional comparisons

### Ownership

**What is preserved?**

Local positive responsibility, source/receiver distinction, authority boundary,
negative authority, and provenance are preserved.

**What changes?**

Responsibility-bearing material may become lawfully usable by another competent
participant after crossing a competent local boundary.

**What remains distinct?**

Ownership is the participant's local responsibility. Constitutional
Responsibility Movement is the lawful crossing discipline for
responsibility-bearing material. Movement does not assign universal ownership or
make the receiving participant own more than the evidence supports.

**What repository evidence supports the distinction?**

`constitutional_responsibility_transition_investigation.md` distinguishes the
movement of responsibility-bearing material from runtime ownership and from
collapsing neighboring responsibilities. Implementation-local ownership slices
recover one local boundary at a time and stop instead of creating universal
ownership.

### Lawful Transition

**What is preserved?**

Evidence-gating, competent boundary, source/target distinction, provenance,
authority, unknowns, confidence, locality, and stop conditions are preserved.

**What changes?**

The characterized movement narrows lawful transition to the specific case where
what crosses is responsibility-bearing material.

**What remains distinct?**

Lawful Transition is broader: it includes movement among reality layers,
representations, projections, recordings, communications, and refusal
conditions. Constitutional Responsibility Movement is the responsibility-bearing
subset of that broader lawful transition discipline.

**What repository evidence supports the distinction?**

`constitutional_lawful_transition_survey.md` characterizes broad lawful movement
as bounded transformation rather than truth transfer. The completed
responsibility-transition investigation narrows that evidence to lawful movement
of constitutional responsibility between supported participants.

### Movement

**What is preserved?**

Bounded subject, evidence/support/provenance, authority, locality, unknowns,
confidence, and lawful stop are preserved.

**What changes?**

The movement is characterized specifically by responsibility-bearing material,
not by every constitutional change, representation, artifact emission, or
surface output.

**What remains distinct?**

Movement remains the broader category. Constitutional Responsibility Movement is
not a canonical movement framework, event model, lifecycle, or taxonomy. It is
one bounded characterization of recurring responsibility-bearing crossing.

**What repository evidence supports the distinction?**

`constitutional_movement_participation_survey.md` explicitly refuses a canonical
movement framework while identifying recurring participants required before
lawful movement may occur.

### Warrant

**What is preserved?**

Evidence-bound lawful reliance under preserved authority boundary is preserved,
including support, provenance, role, authority limit, negative authority,
unknowns, and confidence limit.

**What changes?**

Warrant travels with responsibility-bearing material as the condition that makes
post-crossing reliance lawful.

**What remains distinct?**

Warrant is the entitlement and limit. Constitutional Responsibility Movement is
the crossing that must preserve that entitlement and limit. Movement cannot
substitute for warrant.

**What repository evidence supports the distinction?**

`constitutional_warrant_characterization.md` identifies warrant as the property
that survives lawful translation when syntax, provider vocabulary, presentation
label, and record class do not.

### Reliance

**What is preserved?**

The downstream use boundary is preserved: use remains within role, scope,
authority, confidence, unknowns, and non-promotion limits.

**What changes?**

A receiving participant may lawfully rely on the moved material only to the
extent its warrant survives the crossing.

**What remains distinct?**

Reliance is downstream consumption. Constitutional Responsibility Movement is
the boundary crossing that may permit such consumption. The crossing is not the
same as consuming the content.

**What repository evidence supports the distinction?**

`constitutional_reliance_characterization.md` states that reliance is the lawful
downstream use of warranted content within the preserved authority boundary and
is distinct from warrant and expression.

### Current Lawful Condition

**What is preserved?**

Present bounded lawful status, evidence sufficiency or insufficiency,
unsupportedness, lawful stop, unknowns, and non-command character are preserved.

**What changes?**

After a lawful responsibility movement, the current lawful condition may be that
responsibility-bearing material has lawfully crossed, remains blocked, remains
unknown, or must stop.

**What remains distinct?**

Current Lawful Condition is the present status. Constitutional Responsibility
Movement is the lawful crossing discipline that may produce or be constrained by
that status. It is not a runtime state variable, behavior, command, or projected
view.

**What repository evidence supports the distinction?**

`current_lawful_condition_characterization.md` distinguishes current lawful
condition from timeless law, raw event, projection/read model, behavior,
implementation runtime detail, and presentation label alone.

### Projection

**What is preserved?**

Derivation from preserved material, support links, provenance, confidence,
unknowns, non-verifying boundary, and consumer-specific locality are preserved.

**What changes?**

Responsibility-bearing material may be rendered into a projection or consumed
from a projection only when the projection remains derived, bounded, and
non-promoting.

**What remains distinct?**

Projection is a derived consumer-specific rendering. Constitutional
Responsibility Movement is not projection and does not make projection true,
observational, mutating, or uniquely authoritative.

**What repository evidence supports the distinction?**

`constitutional_lawful_transition_survey.md` treats projection as a bounded
view that cannot cross as truth, verification, mutation, source observation, or
repository knowledge. `docs/reasoning_space_translation_investigation.md`
identifies projection-shape surfaces as translation into projection
interpretation while preserving authority boundaries.

### Observation

**What is preserved?**

Source identity where available, vantage, payload or payload reference,
observation/preservation timing distinctions, provenance, confidence, authority
scope, claim strength, contradictions, and unresolved unknowns are preserved.

**What changes?**

Responsibility-bearing material may move from or toward observation-supported
conditions only when a competent observation or representation boundary actually
preserves the evidence needed for that role.

**What remains distinct?**

Observation is source/evidence-side preservation under its own boundary.
Constitutional Responsibility Movement cannot construct observation by naming
movement, cannot convert projection into observation, and cannot make source
material true merely because it crossed a boundary.

**What repository evidence supports the distinction?**

`constitutional_lawful_transition_survey.md` distinguishes source-side material
from truth transfer and requires valid observation construction, source
attribution, support, authority, and unknown preservation before movement into
constitutional representation.

## Attempted falsification

The characterization would be false if repository evidence showed any of the
following:

1. Responsibility moves merely because a phrase recurs.
2. Responsibility movement assigns runtime ownership or execution authority.
3. Projection verifies itself or becomes observation by display.
4. A diagnostic, answer, report, or presentation label becomes repository truth
   by appearing in output.
5. Warrant can be dropped while reliance remains lawful.
6. Unknowns can be silently resolved during movement.
7. One universal movement framework or participant taxonomy is recovered.
8. Current lawful condition is equivalent to implementation state or behavior.

Reviewed evidence supports the opposite. The knowledge-reachability app check
reported the candidate phrase as not preserved. Lawful-transition evidence
refuses truth transfer and unsupported promotion. Movement-participation evidence
refuses a canonical movement framework. Warrant and reliance evidence require
preserved support, authority, unknowns, and confidence. Current-lawful-condition
evidence refuses runtime state and presentation labels. The characterization
therefore survives falsification within the reviewed boundary.

## Lawful stop conditions

The movement must stop when:

- evidence is insufficient;
- provenance would be lost;
- warrant would not survive;
- authority does not reach the proposed crossing;
- negative authority forbids the proposed use;
- the receiving participant is not competent for the material;
- the local boundary is unsupported;
- unknowns would be erased or silently resolved;
- confidence would be overstated;
- locality would be exceeded;
- projection would be treated as verification;
- observation would be constructed without observation evidence;
- diagnostic or answer output would become cluster truth;
- implementation, runtime ownership, scheduling, planning, alarms, polling,
  timers, notifications, forecasting, or execution ownership would have to be
  recovered;
- a new constitutional participant would have to be introduced.
